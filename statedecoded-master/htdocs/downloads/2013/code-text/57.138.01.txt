                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.138.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division along with L.A.F.D. Standards No. 53
and No. 59 shall regulate the maintenance of all automatic sprinkler systems and
standpipe systems.  When the provisions of this division are in conflict with
L.A.F.D. Standards No. 53 or No. 59, the requirements of this division shall
apply.
