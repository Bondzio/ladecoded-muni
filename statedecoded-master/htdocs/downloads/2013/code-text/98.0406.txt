INSPECTION FEES FOR OFF HOURS INSPECTION. (§ 98.0406)

  INSPECTION FEES FOR OFF HOURS INSPECTION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 171,185, Eff. 7/22/96.)	The Department may, at its
discretion, make inspections at other than normal working hours upon application
therefor by a permittee.  A fee in addition to fees charged elsewhere in this
Code, at a rate of $100.00 per hour shall be charged for such inspection, time
to include travel to and from lace of inspection, with a minimum of $300.00.
