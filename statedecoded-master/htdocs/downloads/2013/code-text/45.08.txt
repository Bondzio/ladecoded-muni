                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DANCING ACADEMY – WHEN INFANT MAY ATTEND. (§ 45.08)

  DANCING ACADEMY – WHEN INFANT MAY ATTEND.	No person conducting, maintaining
or carrying on a dancing academy shall permit any person under the age of
sixteen (16) years to enter, dance or be instructed in dancing between the hours
of six o’clock P.M. and twelve o’clock midnight of any day, unless such
infant be accompanied by his parent, guardian or person exercising parental
control over such infant, or by any other adult person authorized in writing by
the parent, guardian, or any other adult person exercising parental control of
such infant, to accompany the infant to any dancing academy.
