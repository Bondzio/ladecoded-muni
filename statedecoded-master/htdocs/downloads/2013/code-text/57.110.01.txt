                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.110.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division and applicable provisions of Titles 19
and 24, C.A.C. shall regulate fire and life safety requirements for assembly
occupancies.  Such provisions apply to all assembly occupancies requiring a
Fire Permit and define who shall be responsible for providing the fire and life
safety within.  The provisions recognize that life safety consists of more than
exit requirements alone and address the proper use of decorative materials, open
flame, flammable liquids and gases, and the operation of motion picture theaters
and projection rooms.  The provisions do not address building construction
features that are regulated by Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Building Code).
