                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WELDING AND CUTTING. (ยง 57.101.16)

ย WELDING AND CUTTING.	A.	Whenever welding and cutting operations are conducted
on an airport or in an aircraft factory or hangar, a Special Permit shall be
obtained from the Chief.	B.	All welding and cutting operations shall comply with
the requirements of L.A.F.D. Standards No. 50 and 51.	C.	Every welding cart or
stand used for welding apparatus shall be equipped with at least one portable
fire extinguisher having a 10B:C classification or greater.
