                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FLOOR COVERING. (ยง 57.80.07)

ย FLOOR COVERING.	All combustible flooring under and adjacent to spray booths
or spray tunnels shall be protected with a covering of noncombustible material
so installed as to prevent the combustible flooring from coming into contact
with the flammable liquids used in the spraying process, and of such a nature as
to facilitate the safe cleaning and removal of residues.
