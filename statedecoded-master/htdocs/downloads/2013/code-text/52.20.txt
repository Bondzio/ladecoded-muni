                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NEWS MEDIA IDENTIFICATION CARDS – RETURN OF. (§ 52.20)

  NEWS MEDIA IDENTIFICATION CARDS – RETURN OF.	The holder of a card who no
longer covers, on a regular or on-call basis. news events at which police and/or
fire lines are established, or whose card has been revoked, shall return such
card to the Board within ten (10) days of such occurrence. (Amended by Ord. No.
150,115, Eff. 10/20/77. )
