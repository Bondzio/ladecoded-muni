                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.74.01)

  SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	The provisions of this division
and LAFD Standard No. 68 shall regulate the storage, processing, and use of
toxic materials.  The design and construction of industrial equipment,
containers, and special vessels for the use of toxic materials shall be
regulated in accordance with Divisions 41, 42, and 43 of this article.	If a
conflict arises between LAFD Standard No. 68 and the provisions of this
division, the more restrictive shall apply.
