                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENFORCEMENT. (§ 45.24)

  ENFORCEMENT.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,212, Eff. 10/23/98, Oper. 10/23/99.)	Violations.  It
shall be unlawful for any person to violate any provision of this article.  Any
person or who violates any of the provisions of this article or causes or
permits another person to violate any of the provisions of this article is
guilty of a misdemeanor.  Each violation of the provisions of this article
shall constitute a separate offense.
