                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TAXICABS – CARS FOR HIRE – DEFRAUDING. (§ 43.17)

  TAXICABS – CARS FOR HIRE – DEFRAUDING.	No person shall hire any
automobile for hire or taxicab with intent to defraud the person from whom it is
hired or refuse to pay the legal fare of any automobile for hire or taxicab
after having hired the same.
