                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

QUALIFIED INSTALLER. (§ 97.0302)

  QUALIFIED INSTALLER.	No person shall install any boiler regulated by this
Code, including any electrical and plumbing work that is incidental to
installation, unless that person is a qualified installer or a person working
under the direct supervision of a qualified installer.	For the purpose of this
section, a qualified installer is a person who holds a valid contractor’s
license in the proper classification issued by the State of California.
