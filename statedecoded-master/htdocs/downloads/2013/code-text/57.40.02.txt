                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPROVAL OF DEVICES. (§ 57.40.02)

  APPROVAL OF DEVICES.	All devices which are used in connection with gasholder
systems shall be of a type and construction suitable for their proposed use. The
Chief shall approve devices upon satisfactory evidence that they are designed
and constructed for safe operation. The Chief shall classify, as approved,
devices that have been approved, listed, or labeled by any approved testing
agency.
