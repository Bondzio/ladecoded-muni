                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME. (§ 57.115.11)

  SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME.	A.	Smoking shall not be permitted inside any tent or
membrane structure or in any adjacent areas where hay, straw, sawdust, or other
highly combustible materials are used or stored.  Approved “NO SMOKING”
signs shall be conspicuously posted in accordance with Division 23 of this
article.  It shall be the responsibility of the permittee to enforce this
prohibition.	B.	No fireworks, open flame, or other device emitting flame or fire
shall be used inside or immediately adjacent to any tent or membrane structure
while open to the public.
