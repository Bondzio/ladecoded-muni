                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEPOSITING SOLID WASTE. (ยง 190.02)

ย DEPOSITING SOLID WASTE.	It shall be unlawful for any person to deposit or
cause to be deposited any putrescible and non-putrescible solid wastes including
combustible or noncombustible rubbish, refuse, trash, dewatered, treated or
chemically fixed sewage sludge, or discarded solid or semisolid wastes or
regulated recyclable materials of any kind whatsoever upon or in any street,
public area, any private premises in this City, or in the Los Angeles River, or
in the bed thereof without obtaining all required approvals and/or permits to do
so.
