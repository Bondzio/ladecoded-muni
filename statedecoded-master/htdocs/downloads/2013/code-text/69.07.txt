                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REVERSE GEARS. (§ 69.07)

  REVERSE GEARS.	It shall be unlawful for any person to operate on the waters
of the entrance channel any power or motor-driven vessel that does not have a
reverse gear or the means to reverse and stop the vessel, except when
participating as a contestant in a race, regatta, demonstration, exhibition, or
other special event which has been authorized by the Director.
