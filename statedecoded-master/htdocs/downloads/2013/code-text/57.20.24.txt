                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EXERCISE OF CAUTION WITH FIRE. (ยง 57.20.24)

ย EXERCISE OF CAUTION WITH FIRE.	No person shall construct, erect, install,
locate, equip, maintain, or use any incinerator, exterior fireplace or barbeque
device, or burn any combustible material so as to constitute or occasion a fire
hazard by the use, or burning thereof, or as to endanger the life or property of
any person thereby.
