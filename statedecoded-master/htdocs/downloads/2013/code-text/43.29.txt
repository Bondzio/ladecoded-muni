                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EMPLOYERS – REQUIRING EMPLOYEES TO WEAR SERVICE UNIFORMS. (§ 43.29)

  EMPLOYERS – REQUIRING EMPLOYEES TO WEAR SERVICE UNIFORMS.	No person engaged
in any private business, occupation or employment shall directly or indirectly
require or make it a condition of the employment of any person that such person
so employed shall wear the uniform of the military, naval or marine service of
the United States while performing the duties of such private business,
occupation or employment.
