                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITIONS. (§ 154.01)

  DEFINITIONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173,810, Eff. 4/16/01.)	The following words and phrases,
whenever used in this article, shall be construed as defined in this section. 
Words and phrases not defined here shall be construed as defined in Sections
12.03, 151.02, 153.02 and 162.02, if defined there.	Order to Repair.  Any order
or notice to comply, correct or abate or any substandard order issued by the
Department of Building and Safety, the Los Angeles Housing Department, or the
Los Angeles County Department of Health Services or any notice of fire and life
safety violation issued by the Fire Department.
