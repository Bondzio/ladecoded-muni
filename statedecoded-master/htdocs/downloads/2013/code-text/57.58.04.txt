                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STORAGE HEIGHT. (ยง 57.58.04)

ย STORAGE HEIGHT.	The height of a Storage Pile shall not exceed 15
feet.	EXCEPTIONS:	1.	When a Storage Pile is bordered on two long sides by Access
Driveways or Main Aisles, its height may be increased to 20 feet.	2.	Single
crated items, scenery backdrops, screens, and flats having height dimensions
exceeding 20 feet when in a normal storage position are exempt from the 20 feet
maximum height providing all other requirements are in compliance.
