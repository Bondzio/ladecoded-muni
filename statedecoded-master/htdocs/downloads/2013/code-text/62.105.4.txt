                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SLOPE OF DRIVEWAY APPROACHES. (§ 62.105.4)

  SLOPE OF DRIVEWAY APPROACHES.	Driveway approaches shall be constructed so as
to accommodate a 2 1/2 percent upward slope from the top of the existing or
proposed curb, or if none then from a point determined by the City Engineer. 
(Added by Ord. No. 142,306, Operative 2/9/72.)
