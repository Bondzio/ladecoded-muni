                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PEDDLERS – VENDERS – HOURS. (§ 42.13)

  PEDDLERS – VENDERS – HOURS.	Notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 8
of this Code, no person shall peddle fruits or vegetables in or upon any street
or sidewalk other than between the hours of 8 o’clock A.M. and 8 o’clock
P.M.
