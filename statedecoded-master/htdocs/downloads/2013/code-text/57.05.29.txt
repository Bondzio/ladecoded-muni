                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS – DUPLICATION. (§ 57.05.29)

  PERMITS – DUPLICATION.	Duplicate Permits may be issued by the Department to
replace any previously issued Permit which has been lost or destroyed upon the
filing of a statement by the permittee or an authorized representative attesting
to such action and upon payment to the Department of a fee of $1.00 therefor.
