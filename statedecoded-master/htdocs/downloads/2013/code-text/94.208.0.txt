                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

F. (§ 94.208.0)

  	Section 208 of the C.P.C. is hereby adopted by reference with the following
additions and amendments.	Fire Separation Area.  A fire separation area is an
area enclosed by construction having a fire-resistive rating of at least one
hour with all openings protected by minimum three-fourths-hour self-closing fire
assemblies or a water curtain.
