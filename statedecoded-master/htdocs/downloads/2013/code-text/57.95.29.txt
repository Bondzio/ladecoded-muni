                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTRICAL BONDING. (§ 57.95.29)

  ELECTRICAL BONDING.	A tank vessel may be electrically connected to the
terminal piping, through which the cargo is to be transferred, prior to
connecting of the cargo hose.  This electrical connection, if made, shall be
maintained until after the cargo hose has been disconnected and any spillage has
been removed.
