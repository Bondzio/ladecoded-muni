                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ADMINISTRATION. (§ 103.27)

  ADMINISTRATION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 137,438, Eff. 11/7/68.)	The Board may adopt and enforce
reasonable rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of this article. 
The Board may amend such rules and regulations from time to time. The Board
shall cause such rules and regulations to be printed and distributed to
permittees affected thereby.
