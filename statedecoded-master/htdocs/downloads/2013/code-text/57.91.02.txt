                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RESTRICTIONS ON LOCATIONS. (§ 57.91.02)

  RESTRICTIONS ON LOCATIONS.	No Refinery or Natural Gasoline Plant shall be
located within:	A.	100 feet of any building not a part of such Refinery or
Natural Gasoline plant unless otherwise approved by the Chief.	B.	300 feet of
any institutional, residential, educational or assembly occupancy.
