                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE. (ยง 57.61.14)

ย MAINTENANCE.	A.	Regularly scheduled inspection and maintenance of all safety
devices shall be performed weekly to insure that such devices are maintained in
good repair at all times.	B.	Safety devices shall be guarded against physical
damage and inadvertent tampering.	C.	Safety devices shall not be shorted-out or
bypassed.	D.	All ovens or furnaces, ducts, pipes, fans, motors, and other
equipment shall be kept free from deposits of residue.
