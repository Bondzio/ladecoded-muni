                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITIONS. (§ 57.121.02)

  DEFINITIONS.	The following words and phrases whenever used in this division
shall be defined as follows:	Alarm Signal – A signal indicating an emergency
requiring immediate action, such as an alarm for fire from a manual pull
station, a waterflow alarm, or an alarm from a fire protective signaling
system.	Central Station – An office to which remote alarm and supervisory
signaling devices are connected, and where personnel are in attendance at all
times to supervise the circuits and investigate fire alarm signals.
