                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

UTILITY SHUT-OFF. (§ 155.09)

  UTILITY SHUT-OFF.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,643, Eff. 5/31/11.)	If UMP is unsuccessful in
obtaining adequate tenant participation to maintain the utility services, DWP
will be notified by the Department.  DWP shall consequently notify all
interested parties of the termination of utility services in accordance with its
utility termination procedures.
