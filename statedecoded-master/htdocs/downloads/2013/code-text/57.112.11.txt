                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPRESSED GASES AND LIQUEFIED FLAMMABLE GASES. (§ 57.112.11)

  COMPRESSED GASES AND LIQUEFIED FLAMMABLE GASES.	No person shall store or use
compressed gases or liquefied flammable gases within a residential
occupancy.	EXCEPTION:	Permanent outside installations of containers and
equipment when installed in compliance with Division 42 of this article.
