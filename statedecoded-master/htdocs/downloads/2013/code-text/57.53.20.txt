                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTRIC LIGHTING AND EQUIPMENT. (§ 57.53.20)

  ELECTRIC LIGHTING AND EQUIPMENT.	All electrical equipment within any such
storage bin, vault, or storage room shall be installed and maintained in
accordance with Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Electrical Code) for a dust or lint
hazardous location, depending upon the material handled.
