ย PROOF OF RUBBISH SERVICE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 161,912, Eff. 2/26/87.)	Irrespective of any other provision
of this Code, the manager or person in charge of, or in control of, any refuse
of trash of any hotel, motel, restaurant, office building or commercial business
shall furnish written proof, whether in the form of contracts or receipts, to
any appropriate municipal authority on request that said business establishment
maintains a rubbish service that collects rubbish emanating from said
establishment in a manner in keeping with current health regulations.
