                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SEVERABILITY. (ยง 164.09)

ย SEVERABILITY.	Should any provision, section, paragraph, sentence or word of
this chapter be determined or declared invalid by any final court action in a
court of competent jurisdiction or by reason of any preemptive legislation, the
remaining provisions, sections, paragraphs, sentences or words of this chapter
shall remain in full force and effect.
