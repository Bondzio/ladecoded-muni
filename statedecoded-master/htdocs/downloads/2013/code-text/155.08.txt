                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TENANT OUTREACH AND INFORMATION. (§ 155.08)

  TENANT OUTREACH AND INFORMATION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,643, Eff. 5/31/11.)	The Department, DWP and
organizations authorized by the City Council may contact tenants.  During that
contact, tenants shall be informed of the UMP/REAP and their right to forward
rent payments into the escrow account for the maintenance of utility services,
eviction protection rights, and rights for the release of escrow funds.
