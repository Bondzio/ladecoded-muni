                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VACCINATION OF DOGS REQUIRED. (§ 53.51)

  VACCINATION OF DOGS REQUIRED.	Every person who owns or harbors any dog over
the age of four months in the city shall have such dog vaccinated against rabies
by a duly licensed veterinarian of his choice, or at a Vaccination Clinic
sponsored by the Southern California Veterinary Medical Association, except as
provided in Section 53.52.  (Amended by Ord. No. 127,508, Eff. 6/29/64.)
