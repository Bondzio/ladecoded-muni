                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OPEN FLAME. (§ 57.20.03)

  OPEN FLAME.	A.	No open flame or flame-producing device shall be located,
maintained, or used in any hazardous atmosphere unless specifically approved by
the Chief.	B.	Hand-held open flame devices such as exposed candles shall not be
permitted in any Group A, E, I, or D occupancy except by authority of a Special
Permit from the Chief.
