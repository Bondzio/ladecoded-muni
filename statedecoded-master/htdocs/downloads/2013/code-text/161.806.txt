                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EVICTIONS. (§ 161.806)

  EVICTIONS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	If an order is made under Section
161.805, regardless of whether the building or dwelling unit is subject to the
RSO, the landlord may bring an action to recover possession of the dwelling unit
only upon the grounds set forth in Section 151.09 until the Department
determines that the landlord has complied with the order and for 180 days
following the compliance date.
