SHAFTS, PITS AND SIMILAR EXCAVATION – MISDEMEANOR. (§ 91.6103)

  SHAFTS, PITS AND SIMILAR EXCAVATION – MISDEMEANOR.
	(Sec. 91.6110 Redesignated by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	Every person
owning or having charge of land upon which is located any active or abandoned
mining shaft, test hole, well, pit or similar excavation which exceeds six
inches in any lateral dimension and three feet in depth shall cover, fence
securely or provide some equivalent protection for the hazard and keep it so
protected. Failure to do so shall cause such person to be guilty of a
misdemeanor.
