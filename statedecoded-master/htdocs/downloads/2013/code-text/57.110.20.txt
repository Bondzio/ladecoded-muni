PROJECTION ROOM CONSTRUCTION. (ยง 57.110.20)

ย PROJECTION ROOM CONSTRUCTION.	The projection of cellulose acetate or other
safety film when used in conjunction with electric arc, xenon, or other light
source projection equipment which develops hazardous gases, dust, or radiation
shall be in a room designed and constructed in accordance with requirements of
Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Building Code).
