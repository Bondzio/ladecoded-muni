                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONDUCT OF THE HEARING. (§ 161.804)

  CONDUCT OF THE HEARING.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	Department staff, other governmental
agencies, the owner, tenants, and any interested persons may present any oral or
documentary evidence that is relevant to the case.  The proceedings shall be
recorded by means of audiotape and/or videotape.
