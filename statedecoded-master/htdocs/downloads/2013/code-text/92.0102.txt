                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURPOSE. (§ 92.0102)

  PURPOSE.	(a)	The purpose of this Code is to provide for the safety of life
and limb, and to promote the public welfare.	(b)	Other Limitations.  The
permissive provisions of this Code shall not be presumed to waive any
limitations imposed by other statutes or ordinances of the City of Los Angeles
or State of California.
