                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MISREPRESENTATION AS A DANGEROUS DRUG. (ยง 46.12)

ย MISREPRESENTATION AS A DANGEROUS DRUG.	No person shall represent, either
orally or in writing, or by nonverbal communication, that an over-the-counter
drug is a dangerous drug. This section shall be inapplicable to any
misrepresentation prohibited by either Section 11355, 11382 or 26460 of the
Health and Safety Code of the State of California.
