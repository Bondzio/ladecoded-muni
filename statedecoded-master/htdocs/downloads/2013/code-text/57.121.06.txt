                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OPERATING CONDITIONS. (§ 57.121.06)

  OPERATING CONDITIONS.	A.	The central station shall have a minimum of two
trained persons on duty at all times.  At least one person shall be available
and present at all times to monitor, receive, and give prompt attention to the
emergency receiving equipment.	B.	The central station shall be equipped with the
necessary instruments for automatically receiving and recording all signals. 
The time of receipt of signals shall also be recorded automatically or
manually.  Copies of such records shall be made available to the Department the
business day following such request.  Records shall be kept for a minimum
period of three years and shall be subject to inspection.
