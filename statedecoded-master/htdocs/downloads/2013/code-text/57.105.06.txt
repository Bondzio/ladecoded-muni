                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (ยง 57.105.06)

ย FIRE PROTECTION.	A.	Sprinkler Systems: Handling and storage of large
quantities of waste paper, rags or other combustible materials, shall not be in
a building of combustible construction unless the building is equipped with an
automatic sprinkler system and all vertical openings enclosed.	B.	Portable Fire
Extinguishers: Portable fire extinguishers shall be provided and maintained in
every auto wrecking yard, junk yard, and waste materials handling plant in
accordance with Division 140 of this article.
