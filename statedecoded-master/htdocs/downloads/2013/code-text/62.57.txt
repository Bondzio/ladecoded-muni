                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELEVATED SIDEWALKS – PERMITS. (§ 62.57)

  ELEVATED SIDEWALKS – PERMITS.	No person shall construct or maintain an
elevated sidewalk along or in front of any premises owned or controlled by any
such person without a permit therefor issued by the Board, which Board is hereby
authorized to issue such permits subject to the conditions and restrictions
hereinafter provided in Sections 62.58 to 62.60, inclusive.
