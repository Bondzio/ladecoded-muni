                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GLASS. (§ 57.80.04)

  GLASS.	used in the construction of a spray booth or spray tunnel shall be
wire glass not less than one-fourth inch in thickness or a glass having an equal
fire-resistive rating, set in a metal frame or sash, but in no event shall the
total square foot area of glass in any one ceiling or side of any room, booth or
tunnel be more than 20 percent of the total area of the ceiling or side in which
the glass is mounted.
