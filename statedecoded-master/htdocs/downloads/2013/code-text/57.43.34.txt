                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTOMATICCONTROL EQUIPMENT. (§ 57.43.34)

  AUTOMATIC  CONTROL EQUIPMENT.	The following automatic control equipment
shall be provided:	A.	Suitable controls to govern the compressor operation in
accordance with the pressure in the special vessel.	B.	An alarm system to
function before the pressure in the special vessel rises to the design
pressure.	C.	An alarm and high pressure compressor cut-off shall be actuated by
the condenser system pressure.	D.	Means shall be provided in each liquid line to
stop liquid flow in the event the storage pressure rises above the operating
pressure.
