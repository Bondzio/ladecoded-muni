                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EMERGENCY INSPECTIONS. (ยง 57.05.32)

ย EMERGENCY INSPECTIONS.	The Department may, at its discretion, make emergency
inspections at other than normal working hours when requested to do so by the
permittee. The Board, with the concurrence of the Director of the Office of
Administrative and Research Services (Amended by Ord. No. 173,276, Eff. 6/25/00,
Oper. 7/1/00.), shall determine from the cost of such inspection the fee
therefor for every four hours or fraction thereof of inspection time pursuant to
the provisions of Subsection C of Section 57.04.12 of this article. For the
purpose of this section, normal working hours shall be construed to mean the
hours between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. of any day except Saturdays, Sundays, and
legal holidays.	Inspections of business operations that normally function after
5:00 p.m. and on Saturdays, Sundays, and legal holidays shall not be deemed to
be emergency inspections and shall not be charged the fee specified by this
section.
