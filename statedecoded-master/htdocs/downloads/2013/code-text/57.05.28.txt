                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTHORITY TO STOP WORK. (§ 57.05.28)

  AUTHORITY TO STOP WORK.	Whenever any construction or installation work is
being performed in violation of the plans and specifications as provided by the
Department, a written notice shall be issued to the responsible party to stop
work on that portion of the work which is in violation. The notice shall state
the nature of the violation, and no work shall be done on that portion until the
violation has been corrected.
