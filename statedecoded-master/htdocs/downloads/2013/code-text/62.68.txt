                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EXEMPTION – FRANCHISES. (§ 62.68)

  EXEMPTION – FRANCHISES.	Nothing in Sections 62.62 to 62.67 inclusive, shall
be construed as interfering with any rights granted to any person to string
electric wires, telephone wires, telegraph wires, or wires of any other kind,
which right is exercised under and by virtue of any franchise of the State of
California or of the City, previously obtained.
