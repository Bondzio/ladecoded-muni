                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTRIC WIRES ON STREETS – POWER OF BOARD OVER. (§ 62.63)

  ELECTRIC WIRES ON STREETS – POWER OF BOARD OVER.	The Board shall have the
power and authority to regulate, inspect and supervise the stringing, placing
and attaching of wires and electric lights of every nature whatsoever, now or
hereafter placed over, under, or in any manner directly or indirectly attached
to or in front of any building or structure, or over any street.
