                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

R. (§ 95.220.0)

  
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,327, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	Section 220.0 of
the C.M.C. is adopted by reference with the following additions:	RECLAIMED
REFRIGERANTS are refrigerants reprocessed to the same specifications as for new
refrigerants by means that may include distillation.  These refrigerants have
been chemically analyzed to verify that the specifications have been met. 
Reclaiming implies the use of processes or procedures that are available only at
a reprocessing or manufacturing facility.	RECOVERED REFRIGERANTS are
refrigerants removed from a system in any condition without necessarily testing
or processing them.	REFRIGERATION CAPACITY RATING is expressed as 1 horsepower
or 1 ton or 12,000 Btu/h (3.52 kW).
