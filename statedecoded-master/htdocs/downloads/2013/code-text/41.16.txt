                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SNUFF – THROWING OF. (§ 41.16)

  SNUFF – THROWING OF.	No person shall throw, blow or otherwise scatter on
any street, sidewalk, restaurant, cafe, theatre, place of amusement or other
public place any snuff, or any substance which injuriously affects the olfactory
nerves or which causes sneezing or coughing or otherwise injuriously affects the
person.
