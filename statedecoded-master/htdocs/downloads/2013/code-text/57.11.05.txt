                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PUBLIC GUARANTEE. (ยง 57.11.05)

ย PUBLIC GUARANTEE.	Every person applying fire retardant coatings shall render
to the customer for each separate job location performed, a guarantee of not
less than three years. Such guarantee shall be secured by a surety bond,
acceptable to the Chief and approved by the City Attorney, a copy of which shall
be filed with the Department.
