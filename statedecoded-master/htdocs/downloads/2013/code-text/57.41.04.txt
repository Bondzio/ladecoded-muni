                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONSTRUCTION – PORTABLE CONTAINERS. (§ 57.41.04)

  CONSTRUCTION – PORTABLE CONTAINERS.	Portable containers shall be designed,
constructed, tested and maintained in accordance with:	A.	L.A.F.D. Standard No.
26;	B.	L.A.F.D. Standard No. 19, or	C.	Other design specifications approved by
the Chief.
