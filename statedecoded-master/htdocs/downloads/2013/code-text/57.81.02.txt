LOCATION. (ยง 57.81.02)

ย LOCATION.	No dip tank shall be located, installed, maintained, or used in any
building or room except in an approved location as follows:	A.	Tanks in excess
of 500 gallons shall not be locate in or above basements.	B.	The top of every
dip tank shall extend at least 12 inches above the surrounding floor or grade
level.	C.	Every dip tank shall be separated from every source of ignition by a
distance of 25 feet or more or shall be in a separate room of one-hour
fire-resistive construction with a self-closing fire door.
