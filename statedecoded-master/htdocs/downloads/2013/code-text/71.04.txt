                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AMBULANCE ATTENDANTS – PERMITS REQUIRED. (§ 71.04)

  AMBULANCE ATTENDANTS – PERMITS REQUIRED.	No person shall act as an
ambulance attendant in a private ambulance unless such person holds an ambulance
attendant permit or an ambulance driver permit issued by the Board.  (Amended
by Ord. No. 141,670, Eff. 5/7/71.)
