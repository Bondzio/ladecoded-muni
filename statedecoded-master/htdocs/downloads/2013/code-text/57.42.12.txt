                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GROUPING OF CONTAINERS. (§ 57.42.12)

  GROUPING OF CONTAINERS.	Containers shall be grouped when an installation
consists of more than six containers or when the total storage capacity exceeds
200,000 gallons. Each group shall consist of not more than six containers and
shall have an aggregate capacity of not more than 200,000 gallons. Every such
group shall be separated from other groups or from single containers by a
distance of not less than 25 feet.	EXCEPTIONS:	1.	Containers separated from each
other by a distance of not less than 20 feet.	2.	Single containers having a
capacity of more than 100,000 gallons.
