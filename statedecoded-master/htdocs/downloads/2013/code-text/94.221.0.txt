                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

S. (§ 94.221.0)

  
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,757, Eff. 7/6/11.)	Section 221 of the C.P.C. is hereby
adopted by reference with the following addition:	Self-Closing Faucet.  A
faucet designed to close itself as the activating mechanism is released.
