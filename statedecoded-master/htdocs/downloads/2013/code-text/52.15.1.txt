                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CIVILIAN BADGE – TO WHOM ISSUED. (§ 52.15.1)

  CIVILIAN BADGE – TO WHOM ISSUED.
	(Added by Ord. No. 112,577, Eff. 2/12/59.)	The Board may issue a civilian badge
indicating the civil service classification of the person to whom issued when
the duties of the civilian employee involve the active enforcement of law, or
when he performs other public duties which, in the judgment of the Board or
Chief of Police, are such as to require that he wear or carry a badge for the
purpose of identification.	No person not entitled thereto shall wear, display or
carry a civilian badge.
