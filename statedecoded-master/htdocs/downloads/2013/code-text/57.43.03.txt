                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPROVAL OF DEVICES. (ยง 57.43.03)

ย APPROVAL OF DEVICES.	All devices which are used in connection with special
vessel systems related hereunder shall be of a type and construction suitable
for their intended use. The Department shall approve devices upon satisfactory
evidence that they are designed and constructed for safe operation. The
Department shall classify as approved devices those that have been approved,
listed, or labeled by any approved testing agency.
