                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME ABOARD VESSELS. (§ 57.95.12)

  SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME ABOARD VESSELS.	Smoking, use of matches, lighters, or
open flames and operation of arcing or heat-producing electrical appliances and
equipment shall be prohibited when transferring Grade A, B, C, or liquefied
petroleum products, except that the Master may permit use of such appliances,
boiler fires, galley fires, and smoking in enclosed locations where the same
will not constitute a hazard.  Smoking on deck shall be prohibited at all
times.
