                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OVERCROWDING. (ยง 57.33.05)

ย OVERCROWDING.	A.	No manager or person in control of any assembly occupancy or
premises shall allow an overcrowded condition to exist in that assembly
occupancy or premises.	B.	No person shall remain in any building or premises
that is overcrowded when told to leave by the manager or person in control
thereof or by any authorized enforcement officer.
