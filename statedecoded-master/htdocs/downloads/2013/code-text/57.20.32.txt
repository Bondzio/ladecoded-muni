                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GLUE POTS. (§ 57.20.32)

  GLUE POTS.
	(Sec. 57.20.33 renumbered as Sec. 57.20.32 by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff.
11/16/91.)	A.	Gas Heated:  No person shall install, maintain, or use any gas
heated glue pot unless it is separated or insulated from any combustible
material so as not to create a fire hazard.	B.	Electrically Heated:  In
addition to the requirements set forth in Subsection A of this section,
electrically heated glue pots shall be equipped with an approved electric pilot
lamp to indicate when the heating element is turned on.
