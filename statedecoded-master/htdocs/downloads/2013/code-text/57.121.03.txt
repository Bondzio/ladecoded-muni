                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS REQUIRED. (ยง 57.121.03)

ย PERMITS REQUIRED.	No person shall maintain, operate, conduct, or contract
with others to engage in the operation of any central station signaling system
unless a valid Permit as required by Division 4 of this article has been issued
and the operation of such system is done in compliance with the provisions of
this division.
