                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OPEN FLAME PROHIBITED. (§ 57.25.11)

  OPEN FLAME PROHIBITED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 176,943, Eff. 10/5/05.)	There shall be no open flame or
self-contained device capable of producing flame permitted or located upon any
road, street or fire road within the Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone.
