                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTHORITY TO TAKE PHOTOGRAPHS. (ยง 57.01.25)

ย AUTHORITY TO TAKE PHOTOGRAPHS.	A.	After a notice of a violation of this
article has been issued, sketches may be made and photographs may be taken, as
are found necessary, with permission of the owner or person in charge or control
of the premises.	B.	Subsequent to a fire, explosion, or other emergency,
sketches may be made and photographs may be taken, as are necessary, to
adequately depict the condition for the purpose of investigation. No person
shall interfere with, refuse, or obstruct such sketching or photographing.
