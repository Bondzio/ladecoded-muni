                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GRAVITY FLOW. (§ 57.80.70)

  GRAVITY FLOW.	Not more than 20 gallons of Class I, II and IIIA liquid may be
supplied to each spray booth by gravity flow.  Any container holding such
liquid shall be approved and shall not be over a 10 gallon capacity.  Such
containers shall have closed automatic venting covers and an automatic closing
valve on the discharge outlet.  Containers not resting on floors shall be on
metal supports or suspended by wire cables.
