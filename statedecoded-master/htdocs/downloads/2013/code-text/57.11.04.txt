                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPLICATORS. (§ 57.11.04)

  APPLICATORS.	A.	No person shall apply fire retardant coatings unless such
person is certified to the Department by the manufacturer of such coating as
being fully qualified to make such applications in accordance with the
manufacturer’s specifications.	B.	Any person applying fire retardant coatings
shall apply such coatings in compliance with the manufacturer’s
specifications.	C.	Each manufacturer shall file with the Department complete
specifications for application of such coatings.
