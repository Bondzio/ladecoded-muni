                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INSPECTION FEES. (§ 161.901.1)

  INSPECTION FEES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	If an order to comply is issued and
the violation continues to exist on a date after the order expires, the
Department is hereby authorized to impose an inspection fee pursuant to Section
161.901.2.  Fees may be charged in advance for the third inspection and any
subsequent inspection until compliance is attained.  The fees may be imposed
without a hearing but may be appealed pursuant to Section 161.1001.  Inspection
fees may also be charged in advance for any periodic inspection ordered at the
General Manager’s hearing to take place in less than three years.
