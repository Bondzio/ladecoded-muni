                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.13.01)

  SCOPE.	This division shall set forth the requirements of a fire watch when
the Chief determines that a building or premises presents a hazard to life or
property as the result of a fire or other emergency, or when it is determined
that any fire protection equipment or system is inoperable, defective, or has
been taken out of service.
