                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAND-BILLS – NAME AND ADDRESS OF DISTRIBUTOR. (§ 28.08)

  HAND-BILLS – NAME AND ADDRESS OF DISTRIBUTOR.	It shall be unlawful for any
person to post or otherwise affix on public property, or distribute in any
public place any hand-bills, placard, poster, pamphlet, or other printed matter
that does not bear upon the face thereof the name and address of the person who
caused the same to be distributed. This section does not in any way affect the
provisions of Section 28.01.1 of the Los Angeles Municipal Code.
