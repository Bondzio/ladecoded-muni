                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WAIVER REQUIRED. (ยง 63.20)

ย WAIVER REQUIRED.	Before any permit for any groin, jetty, breakwater, sea
wall, or other work or structure, which may result in the artificial accretion
of land to the adjoining uplands, the Department of Playground and Recreation
will require the applicant for such permit to waive all claims, title and
interest to and in all such artificial accretions of land which may develop as a
result thereof lying beyond or seaward from the line of mean high tide as shown
by the survey accompanying the application and as defined by this article.
