                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

J. (§ 94.212.0)

  	Section 212 of the C.P.C. is hereby adopted by reference with the following
additions and amendments.	Journeylevel Gas Fitter.  A person who has a valid
Certificate of Qualification as provided in this Code, to perform the labor of
gas fitting, when in the employ of and under the supervision of a qualified
installer.	Journeylevel Plumber.  A person who has a valid Certificate of
Qualification, to install, alter, construct or repair any plumbing when in the
employ of and under the supervision of a qualified installer.
