                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HANDLING AND PROCESSING OF CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM. (§ 57.50.07)

  HANDLING AND PROCESSING OF CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM.	Cellulose nitrate film
shall be handled and processed in a room or building conforming to the
requirements of Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Building Code).
