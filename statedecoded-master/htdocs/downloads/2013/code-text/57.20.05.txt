EXHAUST PROTECTION FOR INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINES. (ยง 57.20.05)

ย EXHAUST PROTECTION FOR INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINES.	A.	No person shall use or
operate an internal combustion engine on or near grain, hay, grass, or
brush-covered land, or where flammable fibers are stored, manufactured, or
processed, unless the engine is equipped with an approved spark arrester that
complies with the requirements of L.A.F.D. Standard No. 62.	B.	Spark arresters
and the exhaust systems of engines or vehicles subject to this section shall be
maintained in effective working order and not be affixed or mounted in such a
manner as to allow flames or heat from the exhaust system to ignite any
flammable material
