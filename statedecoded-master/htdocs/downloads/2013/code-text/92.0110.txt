                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TEST OF NEW EQUIPMENT. (§ 92.0110)

  TEST OF NEW EQUIPMENT.	The person who installs or constructs equipment
covered by this Code shall notify the Department before such installation is
covered and shall in the presence of a representative of the Department subject
the equipment and installation to tests of capacity, speed, safety devices,
governors, buffers and general operating conditions. All tests shall be made as
hereinafter provided.
