                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BUILDING MATERIAL – REMOVAL OF. (§ 62.54)

  BUILDING MATERIAL – REMOVAL OF.	No person shall store or keep upon any
street any material or debris taken from any excavation or building, or fail to
remove the same from day to day as it is produced or to wet any dry debris or
rubbish, so as to prevent the same from being blown by the wind, whether in the
course of removal from any building or excavation, or on any street.
