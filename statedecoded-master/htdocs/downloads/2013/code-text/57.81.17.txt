                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTRICAL. (§ 57.81.17)

  ELECTRICAL.	A.	wiring and equipment shall conform to the requirements of
Chapter 9, Article 3, of the L.A.M.C. (Electrical Code)  (Amended by Ord. No.
169,944, Eff. 8/20/94.)	B.	Dip tanks and all equipment and appurtenances thereto
shall be electrically bonded and grounded in a manner to prevent the
accumulation of dangerous static electrical charges.
