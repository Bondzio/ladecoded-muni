                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DECORATIVE MATERIALS. (§ 57.113.06)

  DECORATIVE MATERIALS.	Only flame retardant, flame retardant treated, or
noncombustible decorative materials shall be allowed in institutional
occupancies, in accordance with Division 22 of this article.	EXCEPTION:	The use
of ordinary window curtains, drapes, and cubical curtains in guest rooms,
individual patient rooms, and offices.
