                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ANIMALS – DRIVING ON STREETS – PERMIT. (§ 62.95)

  ANIMALS – DRIVING ON STREETS – PERMIT.	No person shall drive any animal
upon or along any paved street, alley, or public way if such conduct might
reasonably be expected to damage such public property or interfere with public
convenience without having first obtained a special permit from the Board to do
so. The Board shall establish from time to time such regulations regarding said
special permits as it finds are necessary. (Amended by Ord. No. 112,719, Eff.
2/28/59.)
