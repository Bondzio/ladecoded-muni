                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WARNING SIGNS. (§ 57.24.06)

  WARNING SIGNS.	Whenever fumigants are used or stored, conspicuous warning
signs bearing the “Skull and Crossbones” emblem, with the words “DANGER!
POISON GAS! KEEP OUT!,” and designating the name of the fumigant, the
fumigator’s name, address, and telephone number, and any other information
required by the U.S. Public Health Service or State Pest Control Board shall be
posted in such locations as to give adequate warning thereof.
