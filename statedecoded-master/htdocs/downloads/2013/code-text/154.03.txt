                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PENALTIES. (§ 154.03)

  PENALTIES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,761, Eff. 11/30/97.)	Any landlord who does not complete
a City-approved property management training program within 60 days from the
date the landlord is ordered to complete the program as required in Section
154.02 above, shall be guilty of an infraction.  Notwithstanding any provision
of this Code to the contrary, such infraction is punishable by a fine of
$250.00.
