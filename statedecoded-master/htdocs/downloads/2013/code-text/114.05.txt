                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUDIBLE ADVERTISING DEVICES – COMMERCIAL FOOD VENDORS. (§ 114.05)

  AUDIBLE ADVERTISING DEVICES – COMMERCIAL FOOD VENDORS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 164,532, Eff. 4/20/89.)	Notwithstanding the provisions of
Section 114.04, it shall be unlawful for any person, to sound, blow or operate
any music, chimes or bells, or any similar sound device, amplified or otherwise,
within 200 feet of any residential building between the hours of 9:00 p.m. and
7:00 a.m. the next day while operating a catering truck, as that term is defined
in Section 80.73 of the Municipal Code.
