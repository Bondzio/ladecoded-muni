APPLICATION FOR PERMITS. (§ 57.14.04)

  APPLICATION FOR PERMITS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,043, Eff. 7/13/98.)	A.	Filing:	All applications for
Permit shall be filed with the Department, and shall be in writing on forms
provided by the Department.  At the time an application for a Permit is filed,
the Department shall collect the established fees pursuant to this division. 
All applications shall be reviewed for completeness and acceptability for the
purposes of this division.	B.	Contents of Application:	Applications for Permits
required by this division shall, unless otherwise required by the Department,
contain a completed Los Angeles City Fire Department Unified Program application
packet, provided by the Department.
