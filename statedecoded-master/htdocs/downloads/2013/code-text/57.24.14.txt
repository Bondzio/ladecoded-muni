                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMBUSTIBLE WRAPPING. (ยง 57.24.14)

ย COMBUSTIBLE WRAPPING.	Irrespective of the type of fumigant employed, the use
of paper or similar combustible materials to wrap a ship, building, or other
structure in excess of that required for sealing cracks, vents, etc., is
prohibited unless said ship, building, or other structure is located at least 50
feet from the nearest type V Building (excluding a garage or other miscellaneous
structure on the same premises) or unprotected opening in any other type of
building.
