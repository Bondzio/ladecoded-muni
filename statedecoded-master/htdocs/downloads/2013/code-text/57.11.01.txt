                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.11.01)

  SCOPE.	A.	This division shall regulate to all chemical formulations, paint
coatings, or other finishes which are sold, offered for sale, or advertised and
which purport to reduce surface flamespread rate or to reduce or eliminate
combustible characteristics of furnishings or structure. It shall apply only to
existing structures for which a Certificate of occupancy has been issued by the
Department of Building and Safety.	B.	This division shall not be deemed to
regulate any interior or exterior surface application which is regulated by
Title 19 of the California Administrative Code or by Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C.
(Building Code).
