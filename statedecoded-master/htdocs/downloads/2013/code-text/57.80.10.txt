                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DRY TYPE OVER SPRAY COLLECTORS. (§ 57.80.10)

  DRY TYPE OVER SPRAY COLLECTORS.	A.	Filter pads shall not restrict the
required air flow through the booth. Filter pads shall be inspected regularly
and replaced when they become clogged.	B.	Filters shall not be used when
spraying materials known to be highly susceptible to spontaneous heating. 
Discarded filter pads shall be removed to a safe location.	C.	Booths, rooms or
tunnels located in sprinklered buildings shall be protected on both sides of the
filters with automatic sprinklers.
