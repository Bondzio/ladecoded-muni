SOLAR ENERGY COLLECTORS. (ยง 91.1301)

ย SOLAR ENERGY COLLECTORS.	Approved collectors which function as building
components shall comply with the applicable provisions of the Code.	Approved
collectors located above or upon a roof and not functioning as building
components shall not reduce the required fire-resistant or fire-retardant
classification of the roof-covering materials.
