                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONTAINERS. (§ 57.73.11)

  CONTAINERS.	A.	Oxidizing materials shall be stored in containers suitable for
their use.  Where containers do not conform to nationally recognized good
practice, they shall be of an approved type.	B.	Combustible containers which
formerly contained oxidizing materials shall be stored in a separate fire
resistive room or in an approved isolated yard location pending final safe
disposal.
