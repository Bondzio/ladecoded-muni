                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.115.01)

  SCOPE.	This division sets forth fire safety requirements for tents and
membrane structures. The requirements include location, type of construction,
structural stability and flammability. This division also includes general
housekeeping regulations, specific fire protection, fire prevention and exit
requirements. Tents and membrane structures shall comply with all applicable
provisions of this division and Titles 19 and 24, C.A.C.GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
