                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPLIANCE BY EXISTING PERMITTEES. (§ 103.101.2)

  COMPLIANCE BY EXISTING PERMITTEES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,676, Eff. 1/11/04.)	All picture arcade permittees must
comply with the provisions of Section 103.101.1 upon its effective date, except
that permittees must comply with Subsection (c)(3) within 30 days of the
effective date of that section and Subsection (c)(4) within 180 days from the
effective date of that section.
