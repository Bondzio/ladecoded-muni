                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 91.3301)

  GENERAL.	Section 3301 of the CBC is adopted by reference, except Section
3301.1 of the CBC is not adopted and in lieu, Section 91.3301.1 is
added.91.3301.1. Scope.  The provisions of this division shall govern safety
during construction and the protection of adjacent public and private
properties.	This section shall not be construed to waive the requirements of the
Safety Orders of the Department of Industrial Relations of the State of
California, nor the provisions of Section 832 of the Civil Code of California
concerning the rights of coterminous owners as to excavations.	See Division 70
of this Code for all grading, excavation and fill requirements.
