                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PASSAGEWAYS ON ROOFS. (§ 57.12.04)

  PASSAGEWAYS ON ROOFS.	No person shall obstruct required access passageways on
the roof surface. An unobstructed passageway for use by the Fire Department
shall be provided through or around any approved structures or equipment
installations on the roof surface. One access passageway shall be provided for
every 50-feet length or fraction thereof of roof surface. Passageways shall be
at least three feet wide and have at least seven feet of overhead clearance.
