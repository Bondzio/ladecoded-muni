                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITIONS. (§ 57.96.02)

  DEFINITIONS.	The following words or group of words whenever used in this
division shall be defined as follows:	Limited Core Hole – A temporary
geological exploratory core hole limited to 1500 feet in depth and five inches
in diameter.	Unlimited Core Hole – A temporary geological exploratory core
hole unrestricted as to depth or diameter.
