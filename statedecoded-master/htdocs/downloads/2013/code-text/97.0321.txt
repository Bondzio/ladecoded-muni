                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INJURIES – REPORTS – REPAIRS. (§ 97.0321)

  INJURIES – REPORTS – REPAIRS.	Any injury to any person resulting from, or
caused by, any equipment, machinery or apparatus listed in this article shall be
reported immediately to the Superintendent.  No person shall repair or replace
any part of the equipment on which the injury occurred until after the
Superintendent has made an inspection and authorized the removal, repairs or
replacement.  The Superintendent shall inspect within 18 hours after receipt of
the report of any accident or injury.
