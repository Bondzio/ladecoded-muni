                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE OF VACANT PROPERTIES AND ENFORCEMENT OF VACANT PROPERTY MAINTENANCE
REQUIREMENTS. (§ 164.06)

  MAINTENANCE OF VACANT PROPERTIES AND ENFORCEMENT OF VACANT PROPERTY
MAINTENANCE REQUIREMENTS.	If a property subject to this section is found to be
vacant, the provisions of Article 8, Division 7 (Section 98.0701, et seq.), and
all maintenance and security requirements described therein, shall apply. 
Enforcement by the Los Angeles Department of Building and Safety shall be
pursuant to the provisions of Article 8, Division 7.
