                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

H. (§ 94.210.0)

  
	(Amended by Ord. No. 174,826, Eff. 11/2/02, Oper. 11/1/02.)  	Section 210 of
the C.P.C. is adopted by reference with the following additions and
amendments.	Health Officer.  The person representing the Los Angeles County
Health Department.	Helper.  A person who is employed full-time by a qualified
installer and whose primary duty is to assist a qualified installer or
journeylevel plumber in the installation or repair of plumbing
systems.	High-Rise Building.  A building (over 75 feet high) included within
the scope of Section 403 of the California Building Code.
