                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RATING CLASSIFICATIONS. (§ 91.9104)

  RATING CLASSIFICATIONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,324, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	The rating
classification as exhibited in Table No. 91-A is hereby established and each
building within the scope of this division shall be placed in one rating
classification by the Department.  The total occupant load as determined by CBC
Section 1004.1 for the entire building plus the occupant load of any adjacent
building, which interconnects with the subject building or uses the subject
building for exiting purposes, shall be used to determine the rating
classification.
