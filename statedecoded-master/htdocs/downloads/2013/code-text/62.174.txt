                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INJURY TO TREES. (§ 62.174)

  INJURY TO TREES.	No person shall pile building material, or other material,
about any tree, plant or shrub in any street in any manner that will in any way
injure such tree, plant or shrub.  (Added by Ord. No. 153,500, Eff. 4/18/80.)
