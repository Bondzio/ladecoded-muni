                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WAIVER. (§ 47.107)

  WAIVER.	Any waiver by an Owner of any or all of the provisions of this
Article shall be deemed void and unenforceable as contrary to public policy. 
Any attempt by a Mortgage Modification Consultant to induce an Owner to waive
any or all of the rights provided by this Article shall be deemed a violation of
this Article.
