                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LACQUER FINISHES. (§ 57.80.64)

  LACQUER FINISHES.	The interior surfaces of all spray rooms, spray booths and
spray tunnels shall be thoroughly cleaned of all sprayed residue before changing
from the use of a paint or other flammable liquid not containing lacquer to the
use of a lacquer or other liquid containing pyroxylin, or before changing from
one material to another in reverse order of that above set forth.
