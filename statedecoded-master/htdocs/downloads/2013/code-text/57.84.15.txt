                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BRANCH DUCTS. (§ 57.84.15)

  BRANCH DUCTS.	Every branch duct or vent pipe shall be connected to the main
duct or pipe at an angle not exceeding 45 degrees measured from the center line
of the main duct or pipe. Every bend, turn, or elbow in any duct or pipe shall
be a minimum center line radius of not less than one and one-half times the
diameter or minimum width of such turn or elbow.  Every change in cross section
of any such duct or pipe shall be by means of a transition fitting having a
slope or taper of not more than one inch for each four inches in length of the
said duct or pipe.
