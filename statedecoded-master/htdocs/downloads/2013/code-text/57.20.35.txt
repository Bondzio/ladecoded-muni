                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WELDING AND CUTTING. (§ 57.20.35)

  WELDING AND CUTTING.	A.	Minimum fire safety requirements, in accordance with
L.A.F.D. Standard No. 51, shall be maintained wherever welding and cutting
operations are in progress.	B.	Requirements for fire safety concerning Gas
Systems for Welding and Cutting shall be in accordance with L.A.F.D. Standard
No. 50.
