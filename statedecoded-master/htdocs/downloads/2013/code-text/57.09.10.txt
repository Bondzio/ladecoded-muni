ย NOTIFICATION.
	(Former Sec. 57.09.09 Renumbered by Ord. No. 169,944, Eff. 8/20/94.)	Any person
owning, controlling, or otherwise having charge of any Supplemental Fire
Protection equipment or systems as required by the division shall immediately
notify the Fire Department at any time such protection is inoperable or taken
out of service. The Fire Department shall also be notified when Supplemental
Fire Protection is restored to service.
