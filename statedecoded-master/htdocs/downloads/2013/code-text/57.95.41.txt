                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WHARF PIPING. (§ 57.95.41)

  WHARF PIPING.	Pans or buckets shall be placed under all hose couplings when
in use.  Ends of pipe lines on wharf and vessel shall be blind
flanged.	EXCEPTION:	When cargo hoses are left connected to pipe outlets on the
wharf open end of such cargo hoses may be blind flanged, or such cargo hoses may
be attached to another pipe outlet where needed to bypass to another outlet.
