                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITIONS. (§ 21.14.1)

  DEFINITIONS.

	The following words and phrases whenever used in this article shall be
construed as defined in this section:

	(a)	“parcel” shall mean a unit of real property as shown on the last
equalized assessment roll of Los Angeles County,

	(b)	“improvement to property” shall mean a building or other improvement
erected on or affixed to a parcel, and

	(c)	“building” shall mean any structure having a roof supported by columns
or walls, for the housing, shelter or enclosure of persons, animals, chattels or
property of any kind.
