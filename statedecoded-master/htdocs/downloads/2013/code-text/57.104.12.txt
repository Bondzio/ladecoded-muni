                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOCATION STANDARDS. (§ 57.104.12)

  LOCATION STANDARDS.	A.	Spraying area must be designated by the Chief and so
maintained.	B.	A plot plan shall be submitted showing the area so
determined.	C.	Spraying area stall be adequately ventilated.	D.	Spraying area
shall be free of excess equipment and storage of any kind.	E.	Spraying area
shall be such that good housekeeping standards can be readily
maintained.	F.	There shall be no immediate sources of ignition within 25 feet.
