                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION. (§ 91.9109)

  MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 169,341, Eff. 2/4/94.)	All materials permitted by this Code,
including their appropriate allowable stresses and those existing configurations
of materials specified in Division 88 of this Code, may be utilized to meet the
requirements of this division.
