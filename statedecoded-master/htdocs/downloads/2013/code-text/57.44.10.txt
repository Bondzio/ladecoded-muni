                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROHIBITION. (§ 57.44.10)

  PROHIBITION.	The transferring or dispensing of liquefied flammable gas into
any tank or cylinder in any building is prohibited except at a Liquefied
Flammable Gas Cylinder Filling Plant in a building approved for such use by the
Chief, which is open on at least three sides, constructed entirely of
noncombustible material and used exclusively for the filling of liquefied
flammable gas cylinders or tanks.
