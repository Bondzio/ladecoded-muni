                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CLOSURE OF A BUSINESS. (§ 57.08.12)

  CLOSURE OF A BUSINESS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,043, Eff. 7/13/98.)	Whenever a business which handles
hazardous substances intends to cease, or ceases doing business at a location,
the business shall immediately notify the Chief.
