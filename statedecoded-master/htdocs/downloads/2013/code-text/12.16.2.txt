                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ADP ALAMEDA DISTRICT SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE. (§ 12.16.2)

  ADP ALAMEDA DISTRICT SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,130, Eff. 8/6/96.)	The following regulations shall apply
in the “ADP” Alameda District Specific Plan Zone:	A.	Purpose.   The
regulations set forth in this Section are intended to reflect the regulations
contained in the Alameda District Specific Plan Ordinance.	B.	Use.   No
building, structure or land shall be used and no building or structure shall be
erected, structually altered, enlarged or maintained, except as permitted by
Sections 6 and 7 of the Alameda District Specific Plan Ordinance.	C.	Area.  No
building or structure nor the enlargement of any building or structure shall be
erected or maintained unless the yard, area and loading spaces required by
Section 7 of the Alameda District Specific Plan Ordinance are provided and
maintained in connection with the building, structure or enlargement.
