                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 161.351)

  GENERAL.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,108, Eff. 7/15/98.)	All residential rental properties
with two or more dwellings subject to the provisions of this Code will be
subject to regular inspection by the Manager or an authorized representative. 
Inspections may also be complaint-based.
