                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SMOKING PROHIBITED. (§ 57.25.14)

  SMOKING PROHIBITED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 176,943, Eff. 10/5/05.)	It shall be unlawful for any
person to light, ignite or smoke any cigar, cigarette, tobacco in a pipe or
other form of smoldering substance within the Very High Fire Hazard Severity
Zone.
