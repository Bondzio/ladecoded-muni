                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CARRIER AND HOMING PIGEONS – PERMITS. (§ 53.60)

  CARRIER AND HOMING PIGEONS – PERMITS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 127,508, Eff. 6/29/67.)	No person shall liberate, for
exercise or racing, carrier or homing pigeons unless a permit to do so has been
first obtained from the Department.  (Amended by Ord. No. 150,337, Eff.
1/1/78.)
