                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOCATION OF EQUIPMENT. (ยง 57.60.20)

ย LOCATION OF EQUIPMENT.	A.	Varnish kettles, oil stills, sulphur bleachers,
agitators and any other hazardous boiling kettles shall be located as approved
by the Chief.	B.	The installation of the kettle shall be in such a manner that a
fire in the kettle will do no damage beyond the contents.
