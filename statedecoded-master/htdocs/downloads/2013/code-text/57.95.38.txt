                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONNECTION TO SHIP’S PIPING. (§ 57.95.38)

  CONNECTION TO SHIP’S PIPING.	Pans or buckets shall be placed under all hose
connections and maintained during cargo operations.  All hose shall be drained
free of oil or blanketed after loading or discharging operations are
completed.  Extreme care shall be taken to see that oil does not escape from
hose or pipe lines to navigable waters.
