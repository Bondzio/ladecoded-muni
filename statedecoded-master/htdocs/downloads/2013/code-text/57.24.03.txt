                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

USE AND CARE OF TARPAULINS. (§ 57.24.03)

  USE AND CARE OF TARPAULINS.	Where tarpaulins or other covers are used in
fumigation or pest-control operations, such tarpaulins or covers shall be
maintained in a state of good repair, without rips or holes therein, and shall
be carefully wrapped, rolled, and secured to adequately prevent escape of gas in
dangerous quantities.
