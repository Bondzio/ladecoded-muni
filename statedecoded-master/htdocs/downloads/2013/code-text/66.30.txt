                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONSTRUCTION OF ARTICLE. (§ 66.30)

  CONSTRUCTION OF ARTICLE.	Nothing contained in Sections 66.18 to 66.29,
inclusive, shall be deemed to conflict with any section of this Code regulating
the collection, removal or disposal of garbage or combustible rubbish, but any
such sections of this Code and any law shall each be so construed as to give
effect to every provision thereof and each shall be deemed to be independent of
the other.
