                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENCLOSURES. (§ 57.92.03)

  ENCLOSURES.	Every Bulk Distributing Station shall be entirely surrounded by
an approved fence or other enclosure at least five feet high with adequate
gates.  No tank vehicle shall be loaded or unloaded at any Bulk Distributing
Station unless it is entirely within such fenced enclosure.	EXCEPTION:	Tank
vehicles may be unloaded when outside of the fenced enclosure at those Bulk
Distributing Stations where the tank car and tank vehicle unloading facilities
are combined and are located outside of the fenced enclosure.
