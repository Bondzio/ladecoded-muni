                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.71.01)

  SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	In addition to all other
applicable portions of this article, the provisions of this division and LAFD
Standard No. 68 shall apply to the storage, processing, handling, and use of
unstable materials.	If a conflict arises between LAFD Standard No. 68 and the
provisions of this division, the more restrictive shall apply.
