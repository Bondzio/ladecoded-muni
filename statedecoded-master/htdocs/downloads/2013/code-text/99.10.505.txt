                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INDOOR MOISTURE CONTROL. (§ 99.10.505)

  INDOOR MOISTURE CONTROL.99.10.505.1.    Buildings shall meet or exceed the
provisions of Los Angeles Building Code, CCR, Title 24, Part 2, Sections 1203
(Ventilation) and Division 14 (Exterior Walls).  For additional measures not
applicable to low-rise residential occupancies, see Section 99.10.407.2 of this
code.
