                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GRAFFITI DECLARED A PUBLIC NUISANCE. (§ 49.84.6)

  GRAFFITI DECLARED A PUBLIC NUISANCE.	Declaration of Nuisance. The City
Council hereby declares and finds graffiti, which is visible from a street or
other public or private property to be a nuisance subject to abatement according
to the provisions and procedures contained herein.
