                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STREETS – LOADING OR UNLOADING FREIGHT CARS UPON. (§ 56.14)

  STREETS – LOADING OR UNLOADING FREIGHT CARS UPON.	No person shall transfer
any substance, commodity or article from any freight or baggage car to any
vehicle or from any vehicle to any freight or baggage car while such freight or
baggage car or vehicle is standing on any street or upon any private right of
way adjacent to any street.
