                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENFORCEMENT REMEDIES. (§ 98.0704)

  ENFORCEMENT REMEDIES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,782, Eff. 9/30/99.)	Violations of this division may be
prosecuted as misdemeanors subject to the fines and custody provided in Section
11.00(m) of this Code.  The Superintendent may also seek injunctive relief and
civil penalties in the Superior Court pursuant to Section 98.0716 of this Code
or pursue any administrative remedy provided in Chapter IX of this Code.
