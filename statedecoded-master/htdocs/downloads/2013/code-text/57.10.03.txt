                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INSPECTIONS. (§ 57.10.03)

  INSPECTIONS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,176, Eff. 9/20/92.)	The Fire Department and Department
of Building and Safety will, at their discretion, inspect the work sites for
compliance with the requirements contained in this division.  All approvals are
subject to field inspection.
