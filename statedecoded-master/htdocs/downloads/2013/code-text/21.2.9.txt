                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECORDS. (ยง 21.2.9)

ย RECORDS.	It shall be the duty of every person required to pay the tax imposed
by this article to keep and preserve for a period of four (4) years all records
as may be necessary to determine the amount of such tax as such person may have
been liable to pay to the City, which records the Director of Finance shall have
the right to inspect at all reasonable times.
