                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (§ 57.93.10)

  FIRE PROTECTION.	A.	At least two portable fire extinguishers having a 20B:C
classification in accordance with Division 140 of this article shall be provided
at every Marine Service Station, and other water front facilities.	B.	Approved
standpipes and hose shall be provided and located in weather-resistant cabinets
at every Marine Service Station, and at not to exceed 200 foot intervals on
other piers and wharves.
