                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BOARD TO DETERMINE IF REPAIR WILL ALTER THE CURRENT. (ยง 63.21)

ย BOARD TO DETERMINE IF REPAIR WILL ALTER THE CURRENT.	The Board of Playground
and Recreation Commissioners, in the exercise of a reasonable discretion, shall
determine whether or not any contemplated repair will change the drift or alter
the current of the water in a manner different from the effect of such structure
to be repaired on the current or drift prior to the arising of the condition
necessitating such repair.
