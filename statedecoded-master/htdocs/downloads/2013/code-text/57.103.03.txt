                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

YARD MAINTENANCE. (§ 57.103.03)

  YARD MAINTENANCE.	A.	Weeds:  Weeds and other similar vegetation shall be
kept down throughout the entire yard and shall be sprayed as often as needed
with a satisfactory weed killer or cut or grubbed out.  Dead weeds shall be
removed.	B.	Debris : Debris such as sawdust, chips and shorts and combustible
refuse shall be removed at least once a week.  Proper housekeeping shall be
maintained at all times. (Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)
