                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURPOSE. (ยง 14.5.2)

ย PURPOSE.	It is the purpose of this article to establish standards and
approval procedures for the Transfer of Floor Area Rights in the Central City
TFAR Area; to effect maximum coordination between the Community Redevelopment
Agency and the City; to provide for the keeping of records of available Floor
Area Rights within the Central City TFAR Area; to provide for an accounting of
allocations of Public Benefit Payments and TFAR Transfer Payments derived from
the Transfer of Floor Area Rights; and to facilitate those Transfers that
generate Public Benefits.
