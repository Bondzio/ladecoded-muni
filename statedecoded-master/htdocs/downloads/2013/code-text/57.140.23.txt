                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECORD OF HYDROSTATIC TEST. (ยง 57.140.23)

ย RECORD OF HYDROSTATIC TEST.	A.	Each portable fire extinguisher which passes a
hydrostatic test shall be fitted with a decal or record tag of metal or equally
durable material upon which the following shall appear:	1.	Date of test.	2.	Test
pressure.	3.	License registration number of the testing company.	B.	The
hydrostatic test record shall not be stamped or engraved on the extinguisher
shell.	EXCEPTION:	D.O.T. Cylinders.
