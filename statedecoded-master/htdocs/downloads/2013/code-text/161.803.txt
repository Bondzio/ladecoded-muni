                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONTINUANCES. (§ 161.803)

  CONTINUANCES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	Upon a showing of good cause, the
General Manager may grant a continuance of the hearing to the next scheduled
hearing date.  However, prior to granting a continuance, the General Manager
shall consider the extent and seriousness of the conditions, their effect on the
building’s occupants, and the criteria set forth in Section 161.602.1 and any
other criteria set forth by regulation indicating a risk of recurring
violations.  The General Manager shall make written findings to support the
decision.
