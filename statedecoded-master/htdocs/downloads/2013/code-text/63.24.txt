                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HARBOR DEPARTMENT – JURISDICTION. (§ 63.24)

  HARBOR DEPARTMENT – JURISDICTION.	Nothing in this article contained shall
prevent the City Council from granting to the Board of Harbor Commissioners any
tidelands and submerged lands herein mentioned when the City Council shall
determine that the needs and requirements of commerce, navigation and fishery
demand that such territory be placed under the control, supervision and
management of the Harbor Department.
