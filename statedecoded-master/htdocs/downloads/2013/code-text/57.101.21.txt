                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISPENSING HOSE AND NOZZLES. (§ 57.101.21)

  DISPENSING HOSE AND NOZZLES.	A.	Only approved hose which is designed for the
transferring of hydrocarbon liquids will be permitted.	B.	The length of hose
shall be limited to the actual needs of the individual transfer apparatus. 
Such hose shall be equipped with an approved shutoff nozzle.  Fuel transfer
nozzles shall be of the self-closing type, designed to be actuated by hand
pressure only. No notches or other devices shall be used for holding the nozzle
valve handle in the open position.  Such nozzles shall be equipped with a
grounding cable complete with proper attachment for aircraft to be serviced.
