                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PORTS AND DOORS TO QUARTERS. (ยง 57.95.34)

ย PORTS AND DOORS TO QUARTERS.	When loading Grade A, B, or C products, all
ports facing the wharf and cargo decks shall be closed; all offshore ports shall
be closed or screened, and all doors shall be kept closed except for necessary
passage of personnel.	When transferring Grade D and E products, all ports shall
be closed or screened.
