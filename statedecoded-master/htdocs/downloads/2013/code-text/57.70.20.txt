                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MANUFACTURING OF EXPLOSIVE MATERIALS PROHIBITED. (§ 57.70.20)

  MANUFACTURING OF EXPLOSIVE MATERIALS PROHIBITED.	A.	The manufacture of
explosive materials is prohibited.	B.	Every person manufacturing, processing,
assembling, or fabricating articles or devices containing explosive materials
shall comply with the provisions of this article, the Comprehensive Zoning Plan
of the City of Los Angeles, the Los Angeles Building Code, the Los Angeles
Municipal Code, and all other applicable laws.
