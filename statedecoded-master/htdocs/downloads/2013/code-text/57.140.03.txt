                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS – PROHIBITED. (§ 57.140.03)

  PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS – PROHIBITED.	No person, firm, company,
corporation, or association shall use, install, allow to remain installed, sell,
offer for sale, or give away any portable fire extinguisher that employs as an
agent for extinguishing fire any carbon tetrachloride, chlorobromomethane methyl
bromide, or any other material which has a level of vapor toxicity equal to or
greater than any of these materials.
