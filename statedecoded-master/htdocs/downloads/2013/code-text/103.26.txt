                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TRUE NAME, AGE AND ADDRESS. (§ 103.26)

  TRUE NAME, AGE AND ADDRESS.	No person who sells or otherwise disposes of
goods, wares or merchandise to a permittee conducting a business listed in Sec.
103.25 or to said permittee’s employee or agent shall fail or refuse to give
his true name, correct age, and correct address.
