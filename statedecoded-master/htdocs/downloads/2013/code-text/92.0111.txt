                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPROVAL OF PLANS AND EQUIPMENT. (ยง 92.0111)

ย APPROVAL OF PLANS AND EQUIPMENT.	No person shall construct, reconstruct or
install any elevator, dumbwaiter, escalator and other equipment covered in this
Code, without obtaining a permit therefor from the Department prior to the
commencement of such construction, reconstruction or installation. Before any
permit is issued, plan drawings of the equipment intended to be constructed or
installed shall be filed with the Department. If they are in compliance with the
provisions of this Code, the Department shall endorse thereon by stamping
approval, and issuing a permit for the construction, reconstruction or
installation of such equipment.
