                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FENCES REQUIRED. (§ 57.94.01)

  FENCES REQUIRED.	In addition to all applicable sections of Divisions 40, 41,
42 and 44; every liquefied flammable gas Bulk Distributing Station shall be
entirely surrounded by a substantial fence at least five feet high with adequate
gates.  No cargo tank vehicle or railroad liquefied flammable gas tank car
shall be loaded or unloaded at any Bulk Distributing Station, unless it is
completely within such fenced enclosure.
