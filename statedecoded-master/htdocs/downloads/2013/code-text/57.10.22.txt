                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SMOKING. (§ 57.10.22)

  SMOKING.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,176, Eff. 9/20/92.)	shall not be permitted inside any
asbestos abatement area or within 25 feet of any asbestos abatement area. 
Approved “NO SMOKING” signs shall be conspicuously posted in accordance with
Division 23 of this article.  It shall be the responsibility of the asbestos
abatement contractor to enforce this prohibition.
