                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TRANSFER HOSES. (§ 57.44.06)

  TRANSFER HOSES.	A.	Transfer hoses or manifold outlets for hose connections
used with cargo tanks shall not exceed two in number.  The connection between
the tank and the meter shall not be considered as one of these outlets. Transfer
hoses shall not be longer than 100 feet.	B.	Each transfer hose shall be equipped
with a shutoff valve at the discharge end.  Hose used for the transfer of
liquid shall be equipped with an approved safety relief valve.	C.	Bleeding of
piping systems and transfer hoses in other than approved locations is
prohibited.
