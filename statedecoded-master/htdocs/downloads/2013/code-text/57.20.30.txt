                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EXHAUST SYSTEMS FOR COOKING EQUIPMENT. (§ 57.20.30)

  EXHAUST SYSTEMS FOR COOKING EQUIPMENT.	Ventilation of restaurant type cooking
equipment shall be designed, constructed, installed, and maintained in
accordance with Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Heating, Ventilating and Air
Conditioning). Such systems shall be equipped with approved automatic fire
extinguishing systems in accordance with the provisions of Division 141 of this
article. Hoods, ducts, and fan housings shall be cleaned at sufficient intervals
to prevent the accumulation of grease therein.
