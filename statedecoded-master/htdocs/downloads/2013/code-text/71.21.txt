                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OTHER SIGNS – PERMIT REQUIRED. (§ 71.21)

  OTHER SIGNS – PERMIT REQUIRED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 165,491, Eff. 3/19/90.)	No person shall display any sign
other than those provided in Sections 71.19 and 71.20 without first obtaining
the written permission of the Board to do so.
