ย PESTICIDES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 166,004, Eff. 6/28/90.)	No person shall store, mix or
transport a pesticide or insecticide at any airport except:	(a)	in connection
with the application of such pesticide or insecticide within the limits of
airport facilities, or	(b)	when such pesticide or insecticide constitutes air
freight and is stored and transported in conformance with all applicable State
and Federal regulations.
