                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REMOVAL OF POSTED NOTICES OR ORDERS. (§ 161.701.3)

  REMOVAL OF POSTED NOTICES OR ORDERS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	It shall be unlawful for any person
to remove, alter, deface, tamper with or alter the visibility of any notice or
order posted at the premises pursuant to this Code.  If any posting is removed
or tampered with, it shall be the responsibility of the owner or the owner’s
agent to replace promptly the notice or order.
