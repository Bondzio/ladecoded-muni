                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TREE PLANTING RECORDS – PLANS. (§ 62.164)

  TREE PLANTING RECORDS – PLANS.	The Board, through its authorized officers
and employees, shall prepare and keep records, maps or plans, showing the kinds
of trees which have been planted, from time to time, upon the streets of the
City, under the Board’s supervision.  (Added by Ord. No. 153,500, Eff.
4/18/80.)
