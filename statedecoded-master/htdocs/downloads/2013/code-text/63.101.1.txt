55 MPH SPEED LIMIT BUMPER STICKERS ON CITY VEHICLES. (ยง 63.101.1)

ย 55 MPH SPEED LIMIT BUMPER STICKERS ON CITY VEHICLES.	Notwithstanding any
provisions of this article to the contrary, the City Council may at its
discretion authorize and direct that there be affixed to the front or rear
bumper of vehicles assigned to any department or office a sticker urging
compliance with the State of California maximum speed limit of 55 miles per
hour. (Added by Ord. No. 152,456, Eff. 6/7/79.)
