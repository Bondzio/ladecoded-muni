                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SANDBLASTING. (§ 91.8115)

  SANDBLASTING.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	No building or other structure shall
be sandblasted except by a wet process precluding the creation of dust and dry
debris.	Inspection shall be made by the Department after scaffolding and
protective enclosure are in place and before work is started.	EXCEPTION:  Dry
sandblasting may be permitted by the Department only when evidence is submitted
that this process is necessary for the proper cleaning of the building or
structure.  No permission may be granted unless it can be shown that the use of
this process will not be detrimental to adjoining property or public welfare.
