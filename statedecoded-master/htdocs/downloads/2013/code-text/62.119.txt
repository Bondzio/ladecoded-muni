                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RAILROADS – STREET PAVING. (§ 62.119)

  RAILROADS – STREET PAVING.	No person owning or operating a steam, electric,
interurban or street railroad shall pave that portion of any street used by the
tracks thereof which such person is required by law to pave and keep in repair,
or construct the tracks or the roadbed of such tracks in any other manner than
as prescribed in Section 62.120 to 62.128 inclusive.
