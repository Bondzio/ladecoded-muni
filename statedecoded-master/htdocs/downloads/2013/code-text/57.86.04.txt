                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ROOFING KETTLES ON TRUCKS. (§ 57.86.04)

  ROOFING KETTLES ON TRUCKS.	Ground kettles shall not be fired or used while
mounted on the bed of trucks, except if the truck body is all metal
construction, and the kettle is securely attached to the bed by adequate bolts
or by welding of the legs to body of truck.  Patch kettles shall not be
permitted for use while mounted on the bed of the truck.
