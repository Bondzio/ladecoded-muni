                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FALSE ALARMS – REWARD. (§ 63.122)

  FALSE ALARMS – REWARD.	The City will pay a reward of Twenty-five Dollars
($25.00) to the person furnishing information which leads to the arrest and
conviction of any person making any false fire alarm, by any means, upon such
conviction of any such person who shall make any such false alarm.
