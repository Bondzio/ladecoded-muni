                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME. (§ 57.74.06)

  SMOKING AND OPEN FLAME.	No smoking or open flame shall be allowed in any area
where toxic materials are used, processed, or stored.  Any such area shall have
conspicuously posted therein a sign in letters not less than three inches high
on contrasting background reading, “NO SMOKING OR OPEN FLAME WITHIN 25
FEET.”
