                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EFFECTIVE PERIOD OF REPORT. (§ 96.306)

  EFFECTIVE PERIOD OF REPORT.	No new report need be obtained by a owner for a
residential property for a period of six months after the issuance of a report
under the provisions of this division. During said six-months period the City
may notify the applicant, at no extra charge to him, of new information of City
record that makes the original report obsolete.
