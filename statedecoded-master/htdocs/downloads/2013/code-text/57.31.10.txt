                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONTROL OF SPILLS, LEAKS AND OVERFLOWS. (§ 57.31.10)

  CONTROL OF SPILLS, LEAKS AND OVERFLOWS.	The accidental discharge of hazardous
substances from aboveground tanks shall be diverted by means of a drainage
system as provided in Section 57.31.11 or shall be contained by dikes as
provided in Section 57.31.12. These provisions may be altered at the discretion
of the Chief when the tanks under consideration do not constitute a hazard.
