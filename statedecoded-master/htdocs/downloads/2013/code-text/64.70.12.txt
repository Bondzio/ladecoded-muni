                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONSTRUCTION AND APPLICATION. (§ 64.70.12)

  CONSTRUCTION AND APPLICATION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,176, Eff. 10/1/98.)	This ordinance shall be construed to
assure consistency with the requirements of the Federal Clean Water Act and acts
amendatory thereof or supplementary thereto, applicable implementing
regulations, and NPDES Permit No. CAS614001 and any amendment, revision or
reissuance thereof.
