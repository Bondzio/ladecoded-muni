                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FILLING WITHOUT PERMITS. (§ 57.94.11)

  FILLING WITHOUT PERMITS.	No person shall fill any pressure vessel with
liquefied flammable gas on any premises where such pressure vessel is installed,
unless there is a valid Permit pursuant to the provisions of this article for
the maintenance and use of such pressure vessel.LIQUEFIED FLAMMABLE GAS AUTO
