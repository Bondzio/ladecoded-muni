                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECORDS. (§ 21.3.10)

  RECORDS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173, 587, Eff. 12/7/00.)	Each person required to collect
any tax imposed by this article must keep, for a period of four years, all
records necessary to determine the amount of the tax that he or she was
responsible for collecting.  The Office of Finance has a right to inspect those
records at any reasonable time.
