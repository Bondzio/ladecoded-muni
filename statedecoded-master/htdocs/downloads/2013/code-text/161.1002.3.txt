                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HEARING. (§ 161.1002.3)

.  HEARING.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,325, Eff. 8/10/03.)	Upon receipt of the appeal, the
Board shall set the matter for hearing and give notice by mail of the time,
place and purpose of the hearing to the appellant, the affected residential
units, the General Manager, and to any interested party who has requested in
writing to be so notified.  The notice shall be in writing and shall be served
on the owner by certified United States mail, postage prepaid, or in person. 
Notice to each affected residential unit shall be given in the manner prescribed
by Subdivision (a) of California Health and Safety Code Section 17980.6 and made
available to any person requesting a copy.
