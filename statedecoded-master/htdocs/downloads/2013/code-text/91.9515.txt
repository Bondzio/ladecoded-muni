                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION. (§ 91.9515)

  MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,260, Eff. 8/30/96.)	All materials permitted by this Code
may be used to reduce the story drifts.  Their effective stiffness shall be
determined by a nonlinear analysis using principles of engineering mechanics and
expected material characteristics.
