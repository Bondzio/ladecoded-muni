ย SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	This division and LAFD Standard
No. 68 shall regulate that portion of a property where flammable or combustible
liquids are received by tank vessel, pipeline, tank car, or tank vehicle, and
are stored or blended in bulk for the purpose of distributing such liquids by
tank vessel, pipeline, tank car, tank vehicle, or container.	If a conflict
arises between LAFD Standard No. 68 and the provisions of this division, the
more restrictive shall apply.
