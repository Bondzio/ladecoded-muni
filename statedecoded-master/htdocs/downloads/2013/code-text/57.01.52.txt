                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FACSIMILES AND COUNTERFEIT BADGES. (ยง 57.01.52)

ย FACSIMILES AND COUNTERFEIT BADGES.	No person who is not a regular member of
the Department shall wear, display, or have in his or her possession any
official badge or one which resembles or may be mistaken for the official badge,
or which is calculated to indicate that he or she is a member of the Department.
