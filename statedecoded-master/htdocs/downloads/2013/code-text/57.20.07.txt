                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROTECTION FROM VEHICULAR DAMAGE. (ยง 57.20.07)

ย PROTECTION FROM VEHICULAR DAMAGE.	The owner, operator, or person in charge or
control of any building or premises shall provide collision barriers adequate to
protect control meters, regulators, and piping for hazardous materials when such
piping or equipment is exposed to probable vehicular damage due to proximity to
alleys, driveways, or parking areas.
