                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PRESENT AT GAMBLING. (§ 43.13.2)

  PRESENT AT GAMBLING.
	(Added by Ord. No. 111,074, Eff. 5/3/58).	It shall be unlawful for any person
knowingly to visit, frequent, or be present at or within any house, room,
apartment, stand or place used in whole or in part as a gambling house, or any
place where any game is played, conducted, dealt or carried on with cards, dice,
or other device for money, checks, chips, credit, pennants, cigars, candy,
merchandise or other valuable thing, or representative of value; provided,
however, this ordinance shall not apply to any police officer while in the
exercise of his duties as such officer, nor to any person whose presence in any
such place is necessary in the course of his lawful business.
