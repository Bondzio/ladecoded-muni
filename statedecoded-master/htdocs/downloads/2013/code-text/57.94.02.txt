                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BACKING VEHICLES PROHIBITED. (§ 57.94.02)

  BACKING VEHICLES PROHIBITED.	No cargo tank vehicle shall be propelled
backwards from such enclosure into any street, alley or public way.  All
driveways used in connection with any such liquefied flammable gas Bulk
Distributing Station shall be so arranged that vehicles other than railroad cars
can travel into or out of the fenced enclosure of such Bulk Distributing Station
only in a forward direction.
