                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RETALIATORY ACTION PROHIBITED. (§ 184.03)

  RETALIATORY ACTION PROHIBITED.	A.	 No Hotel Employer employing Hotel Workers
shall discharge, reduce in compensation, or otherwise discriminate against any
Hotel Worker for opposing any practice proscribed by this article, for
participating in proceedings related to this article, for seeking to enforce his
or her rights under this article by any lawful means, or for otherwise asserting
rights under this article.
