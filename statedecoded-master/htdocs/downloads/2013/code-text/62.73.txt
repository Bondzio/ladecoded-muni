                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OIL PIPE LINES – PERMITS – REVOCABILITY OF. (§ 62.73)

  OIL PIPE LINES – PERMITS – REVOCABILITY OF.	Any permit granted pursuant
to the provisions of Sections 62.69 to 62.72 inclusive, and any permit for the
erection, maintenance or operation of any pipe, casing or other appliance or
apparatus, or of any truss or standard granted prior to the adoption of this
Code, shall be deemed and construed to be a revocable license and the same may
be revoked at any time by the Board.
