                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LIVE THEATRICAL PRODUCTIONS. (§ 57.110.16)

  LIVE THEATRICAL PRODUCTIONS.	When the occupant load is 100 or more, no person
shall operate or maintain any live theatrical production utilizing scenery
and/or settings, lighting, or other stage dressing or paraphernalia, in any
motion picture theater, theater or little theater except by authority of a
Permit in accordance with Division 5 of this article.
