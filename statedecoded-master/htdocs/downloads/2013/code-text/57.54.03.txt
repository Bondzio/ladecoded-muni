                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PICKING ROOMS. (§ 57.54.03)

  PICKING ROOMS.	Picking rooms shall be provided with not less than a one-hour
occupancy separation and shall be constructed as specified in Chapter 9 of the
L.AM.C. (Building Code).  Picking rooms shall be provided with an approved dust
collecting system of sufficient capacity to adequately remove dust and lint from
the air.
