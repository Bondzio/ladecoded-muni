                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STREET ADDRESS NUMBERS – ODD AND EVEN NUMBERS. (§ 63.115)

  STREET ADDRESS NUMBERS – ODD AND EVEN NUMBERS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 144,527, Eff. 4/19/73.)	The street address numbers on the
northerly and westerly sides of the streets shall be odd numbers, the street
address numbers on the southerly and easterly sides of the streets shall be even
numbers.
