                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOCATION. (§ 57.84.02)

  LOCATION.	The location of every drying oven shall be approved by the Chief. 
Such ovens shall not be located in any basement and shall be entirely surrounded
(top and sides) by a clear space of at least 18 inches.  Posts, beams, trusses,
and other structural members of the building shall not pass through any drying
oven.
