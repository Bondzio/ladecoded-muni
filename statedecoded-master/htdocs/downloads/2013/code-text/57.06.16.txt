                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FEE EXEMPT CERTIFICATE OF FITNESS. (§ 57.06.16)

  FEE EXEMPT CERTIFICATE OF FITNESS.	Fee exempt Certificate of Fitness, without
the payment of prescribed by this division shall be issued to the following:
City of Angeles, County of Los Angeles, Los Angeles City School Districts, Los
Angeles Community College District, or any municipal corporation, department, or
office thereof.
