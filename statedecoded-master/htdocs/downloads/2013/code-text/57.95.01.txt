                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.95.01)

  SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	In addition to all other
applicable provisions of this article, flammable and combustible liquids and
flammable gases shall be handled and stored at Marine Oil Terminals in
accordance with this division and LAFD Standard No. 68.	If a conflict arises
between LAFD Standard No. 68 and the provisions of this division, the more
restrictive shall apply.
