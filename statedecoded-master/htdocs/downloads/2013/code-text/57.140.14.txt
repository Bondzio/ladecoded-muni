                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PUBLIC OR PRIVATE HELIPORTS. (§ 57.140.14)

  PUBLIC OR PRIVATE HELIPORTS.	At least two dry chemical type portable fire
extinguishers with individual ratings of 80B:C shall be located not less than 10
feet nor more than 50 feet from each heliport pad, and positioned adjacent to
exitways.	EXCEPTION:	Emergency helicopter landing facility.
