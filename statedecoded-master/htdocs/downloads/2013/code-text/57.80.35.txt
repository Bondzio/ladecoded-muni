                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BRANCH DUCTS. (§ 57.80.35)

  BRANCH DUCTS.	Every branch duct or pipe shall be connected to the main duct
or pipe at an angle not exceeding 45 degrees measured from the center line of
the main duct or pipe. Every bend, turn or elbow in any duct or pipe shall be
constructed as not to reduce the cross sectional area of the duct or pipe. 
Every change in cross section of any such duct or pipe shall be by means of a
transition fitting having a slope or taper of not more than one inch for each
four inches in length of the said duct or pipe.
