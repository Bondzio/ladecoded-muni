DECLARATION OF PURPOSE. (§ 16.00)

  DECLARATION OF PURPOSE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173,268, Eff. 7/1/00, Oper. 7/1/00.)

	It is the purpose and objective of this Article to establish reasonable and
uniform regulations to protect the public welfare and to provide a streamlined
method for consideration of applications for temporary use approvals and other
land use approvals in an emergency, such as fire, storm, severe earthquake,
civil disturbance, or other disaster declared by the Governor.
