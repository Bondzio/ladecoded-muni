                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL. (§ 57.80.68)

  COMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL.	Combustible material shall not be stored on top of or
above any spray booth or spray tunnel, or within 12 inches horizontally of any
spray booth or tunnel.  Paper and similar combustible material shall not be
used as lining for spray rooms, booths, or tunnels.QUANTITY OF CLASS I, II, AND
IIIA LIQUIDS AT SPRAY BOOTHS, ROOMS, OR TUNNELS
