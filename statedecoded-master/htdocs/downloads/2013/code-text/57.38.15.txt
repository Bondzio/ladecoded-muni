ย PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.	Each cargo tank or tank vehicle shall be
equipped and maintained with at least one approved portable fire extinguisher
having a 2A, 20B:C classification in accordance with Division 140 of this
article. Portable fire extinguishers shall be kept in good operating condition
at all times, and shall be located in an accessible place on each tank
