DISPLAY AND SALE OF GLASS ETCHING PRODUCTS. (§ 47.16)

  DISPLAY AND SALE OF GLASS ETCHING PRODUCTS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 174,361, Eff. 1/13/02.)	(a)	Every person who owns, conducts,
operates or manages a retail commercial establishment selling glass etching
cream, or any commercially available glass etching product, whether sold
separately or in a kit, shall store or cause to be stored such glass etching
cream or glass etching product under lock and key in an area viewable by, but
not accessible to the public in the regular course of business without employee
assistance, pending legal sale or disposition of such glass etching cream or 
glass etching product.	(b)	No person who owns, conducts, operates or manages a
retail commercial establishment selling glass etching cream or any commercially
available glass etching product, whether sold separately or in a kit, shall 
sell or caused to be sold any such glass etching cream or glass etching product
to any person who has not yet attained the age of 18 years.
