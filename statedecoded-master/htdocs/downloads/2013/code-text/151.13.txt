                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MINOR ERRORS IN PAYMENT. (§ 151.13)

  MINOR ERRORS IN PAYMENT.	If a discrepancy exists between the amount of the
registration fee paid and the amount due under this chapter which results in the
underpayment or overpayment of the fee in an amount of $5.00 or less, then the
Department may accept and record such underpayment or overpayment without other
notification to the landlord.  (Title and Section Amended by Ord. No. 156,597,
Eff. 5/20/82, Oper. 5/15/82.)
