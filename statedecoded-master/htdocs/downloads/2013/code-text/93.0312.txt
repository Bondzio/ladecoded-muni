                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ABANDONED WIRING. (§ 93.0312)

  ABANDONED WIRING.	When a circuit is abandoned or discontinued, its conductors
shall be removed from the raceways, or shall be insulated and maintained in
wiring enclosures as if in use.  (Added by Ord. No. 154,234, Eff. 9/8/80.)
