                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SOURCES OF IGNITION. (§ 57.76.10)

  SOURCES OF IGNITION.	No smoking, open flame, unapproved heating device, or
other source of ignition shall be permitted in any room or area where organic
peroxides are stored, mixed, or applied.  The owner, operator, or person in
charge or control shall post “NO SMOKING” signs in each location in which
smoking shall be prohibited.  Such signs shall have lettering not less than
three inches in height on a contrasting background.
