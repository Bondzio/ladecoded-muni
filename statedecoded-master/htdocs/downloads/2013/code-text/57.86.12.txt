                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONTAINERS FOR FUEL. (ยง 57.86.12)

ย CONTAINERS FOR FUEL.	A.	Containers for fuel shall be located a minimum of 10
feet from the burner flame or at least two feet when protected by a
noncombustible insulated shield from burner flame, secured in an upright
position, and protected from mechanical damage.	B.	Fuels Prohibited: No person
shall use solid fuel or Class I flammable liquids as fuel for a roofing kettle.
