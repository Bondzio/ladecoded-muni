                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NEW MATERIALS AND METHODS OF CONSTRUCTION. (§ 93.0303)

  NEW MATERIALS AND METHODS OF CONSTRUCTION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,561, Eff. 3/22/11.)	New or alternate materials and
methods of construction may be approved by the Department in accordance with the
provisions of Section 98.0502, Division 5 of Article 8, Chapter IX, of the Los
Angeles Municipal Code.
