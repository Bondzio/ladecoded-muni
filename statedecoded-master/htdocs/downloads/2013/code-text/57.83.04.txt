                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE. (§ 57.83.04)

  MAINTENANCE.	A.	Electrostatic Spraying:	1.	Insulators:  All insulators shall
be kept clean and dry.	2.	Drip Plates:  Drip plates and screens subject to
paint deposits shall be removable and shall be taken to a safe place for
cleaning.	B.	Powder-Coating Dust	1.	All areas shall be kept free of accumulation
of powder-coating dust, particularly horizontal surfaces such as ledges, beams,
pipes, hoods, booths, and floors.	2.	Surfaces shall be cleaned in such manner as
to avoid scattering dust to other places or creating dust clouds. Cleaning
equipment shall be approved for use in hazardous locations.
