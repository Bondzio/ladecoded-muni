                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENFORCEMENT. (§ 92.0117)

  ENFORCEMENT.	The Superintendent of Building shall have the authority to make
rules and regulations and to fix safety standards, and to provide for the proper
enforcement thereof, as to any equipment or apparatus mentioned in this Code and
violation of or failure to comply with any of said rules and regulations or
safety standards shall be deemed to be a violation of this Code and shall be
punishable as a misdemeanor.
