                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DOCKING. (§ 57.95.46)

  DOCKING.	Tank vessels shall not be moored at other than marine oil terminals
or other specially designated places except when gas-free.  Whenever
practicable, tankships will berth headed out or to seaward.	EXCEPTION:	By
permission of the Los Angeles Fire Department and concurrence of Harbor
Department authority.
