                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOCATION. (§ 57.80.81)

  LOCATION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	A.	Spray booths, spray tunnels or
spray rooms shall be located in Group B-2 or H occupancies.	EXCEPTION:	Spray
booths and spray tunnels may be located outside in zones approved by the LAMC
(Zoning Code) and shall comply with all other requirements of this
section.	B.	Spray booths and spray tunnels located outside shall not be adjacent
to nonrated buildings or within ten feet of openings into buildings or within
five feet of property lines.
