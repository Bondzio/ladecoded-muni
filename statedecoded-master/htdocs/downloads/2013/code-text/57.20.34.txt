                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SWEATING PIPES. (§ 57.20.34)

  SWEATING PIPES.
	(Added by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	In addition to all provisions of
57.20.33, when sweating pipes, combustible material in close proximity to the
open flame shall be protected against ignition by shielding, wetting, or other
means.
