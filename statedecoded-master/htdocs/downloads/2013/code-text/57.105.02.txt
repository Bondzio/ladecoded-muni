                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STORAGE. (ยง 57.105.02)

ย STORAGE.	The storage of materials, whether combustible or not, in any auto
wrecking plant, junk yard, or waste materials handling plant, shall be in
accordance with Division 58 of this article for outside storage, aisles, and
Fire Department access.	EXCEPTION:	The storage of flammable fibres shall be in
accordance with Division 54 of this article.
