                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAZARDOUS MATERIALS ROOM. (ยง 57.30.15)

ย HAZARDOUS MATERIALS ROOM.	A.	Every used for the storage or dispensing of
flammable or combustible liquids shall be constructed in accordance with the
requirements of Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Building Code).	B.	No combustible
materials, oxidizers, explosives, acids, or compressed gases shall be stored in
a Hazardous Materials Room with Class I, II, or III liquids, except such
combustible materials as may be incidental to the use of the room.
