                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OBSTRUCTIONS. (§ 57.95.40)

  OBSTRUCTIONS.	Bulk cargo transfer operations shall not be obstructed by
equipment stores, freight and other materials not essential to cargo operations
being performed.  Ship stores or supplies shall not be loaded or discharged
while transferring Grade A, B, C, or liquefied petroleum products, except when
the Terminal Superintendent and officer in charge of the vessel shall agree that
such handling of stores will not obstruct transfer operations nor create a fire
hazard.
