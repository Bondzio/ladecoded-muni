                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OWNERSHIP OF BADGES AND INSIGNIA. (§ 57.01.54)

  OWNERSHIP OF BADGES AND INSIGNIA.	All badges and uniform insignia shall
remain the property of the City. Every person receiving such property shall be
required to personally sign a receipt therefor, and the receipt shall set forth
an agreement to reimburse the City in the event of loss for the standard cost
price as determined from time to time by the Board.
