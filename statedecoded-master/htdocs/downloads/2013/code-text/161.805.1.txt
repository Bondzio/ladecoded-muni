                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NOTICE OF DECISION. (§ 161.805.1)

  NOTICE OF DECISION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,112, Eff. 4/8/03.)	A copy of the decision shall be
served on the owner via certified United States mail, postage prepaid, or in
person, and a notice shall be provided to each affected residential unit in the
manner prescribed by Subdivision (a) of California Health and Safety Code
Section 17980.6 and made available to any person requesting a copy.  If the
decision orders a rent reduction or inclusion into REAP, the decision shall
comply with the notice provision of Section 162.04 of this Code.  If the
decision includes a requirement for relocation assistance, the decision shall
comply with Section 161.805(7) of this Code.	The decision shall also advise the
owner of any additional potential consequences as a result of non-compliance
including but not limited to, civil penalties and fines pursuant to Section
161.905, criminal penalties and fines pursuant to Section 161.906, liens
pursuant to Section 161.904, eviction protections pursuant to Section 161.806,
prohibition of rent increases pursuant to Section 161.807 and rent withholding
pursuant to Section 161.903.3 for the owner’s failure to pay any fees owed to
the Department.	The General Manager’s decision shall state that an appeal may
be filed within ten calendar days after the date the decision is sent via
certified United States mail, postage prepaid, or after the date of personal
service.  (Amended by Ord. No. 175,325, Eff. 8/10/03.)
