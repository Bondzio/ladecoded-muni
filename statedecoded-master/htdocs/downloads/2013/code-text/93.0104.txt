                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROHIBITED ACTS. (§ 93.0104)

  PROHIBITED ACTS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,593, Eff. 6/28/99, Oper. 7/1/99.)	No person shall
install, operate or maintain electrical systems or equipment which do not comply
with all of the applicable provisions of this Code.  Nor shall any person offer
for sale, rent or lease, or distribute any electrical equipment which does not
comply with all of the applicable provisions of this Code.
