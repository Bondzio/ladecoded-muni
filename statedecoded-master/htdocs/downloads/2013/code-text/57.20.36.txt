                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE OF MATERIALS. (ยง 57.20.36)

ย MAINTENANCE OF MATERIALS.	No person owning or having control of any building
or premises, shall store, keep, maintain, or allow to remain, any debris,
including debris generated as the result of a fire, hazardous refuse, or
hazardous material, in such a manner as to cause a fire hazard.
