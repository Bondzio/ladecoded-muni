OBSTRUCTIONS ON FIRE ROADS AND FIREBREAKS. (ยง 57.20.09)

ย OBSTRUCTIONS ON FIRE ROADS AND FIREBREAKS.	No person shall install or
maintain a radio or television aerial, or the guy wires thereto, on any fire
road or firebreak. Antenna wire shall be not less than 16 feet above a fire road
or firebreak.
