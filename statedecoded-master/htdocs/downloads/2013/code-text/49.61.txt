                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITION. (§ 49.61)

  DEFINITION.	For the purpose of this Article 11 the term “newsletter or mass
mailing” shall mean identical or nearly identical pieces of mail in an amount
which exceeds 200 pieces, but does not include a form letter or other mail which
is sent in response to a letter or inquiry or an official mailing required by
law.
