                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FORFEITURE OF GRANTS. (§ 63.07)

  FORFEITURE OF GRANTS.	The Board of Playground and Recreation Commissioners
shall have the power to declare a forfeiture of any grant made under this
article, upon the neglect, failure or refusal by the grantee thereof to comply
with any of the terms or conditions of the grant, and that upon such forfeiture,
any and all buildings, structures and improvements of whatsoever character
erected, installed or made under, through or because of, or pursuant to the
terms of the grant, shall immediately, ipso facto become the property of the
City, and every such grant shall so provide.
