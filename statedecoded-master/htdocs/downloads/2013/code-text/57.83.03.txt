                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VENTILATION. (§ 57.83.03)

  VENTILATION.	A.	Electrostatic: The spraying area shall be adequately
ventilated to insure safe conditions from fire and life
hazards.	B.	Powder-Coating: Nondeposited air-suspended powders shall be safely
removed from a spray operation to an approved powder-recovery system.
