                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ILLEGAL BUILDINGS, CONSTRUCTION AND USE. (§ 91.8105)

  ILLEGAL BUILDINGS, CONSTRUCTION AND USE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	Every existing building, structure
or portion thereof constructed without a building permit shall be made to
conform to the provisions of this Code or shall be demolished and removed.  Any
use or occupancy existing in a building without authorization of a building
permit shall be discontinued and removed or shall be made to conform to the
provisions of this Code.
