ย HANDLING OF OXIDIZING MATERIALS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	No person shall manufacture,
store or transport oxidizing materials in a manner that will give rise to an
unreasonable danger to life or property by reason of fire or explosion.	In
addition to all other applicable provisions of this article, oxidizing material
shall be stored and processed in accordance with the provisions of this division
and LAFD Standard No. 68.	If a conflict arises between LAFD Standard No. 68 and
the provisions of this division, the more restrictive shall apply.
