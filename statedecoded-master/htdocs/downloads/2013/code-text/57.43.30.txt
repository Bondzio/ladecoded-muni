                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REFRIGERATION LOAD. (ยง 57.43.30)

ย REFRIGERATION LOAD.	The total refrigeration load shall be computed as the sum
of the following:	A.	Heat transfer through the insulation and special vessel
wall.	B.	Heat input resulting from:	1.	Cooling the stored material from the
maximum incoming temperature to storage temperature.
	2.	Condensing of vapor returned during discharge operations of the refrigerated
special vessel.	3.	Cooling recycle vapor from the special vessel when stored
material is used as the refrigerant.
