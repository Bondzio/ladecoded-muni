                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NOTICE OF FEE INCREASES. (ยง 21.17.4)

ย NOTICE OF FEE INCREASES.	If the State determines that the fee to be collected
by the Enterprise Zone Administration is to be increased above the ten dollars
($10.00) currently authorized, the General Manager of the Community Development
Department or an authorized representative shall issue adequate public notice of
the fee increase and shall adjust its collection of the fee in conformity with
the State requirements.
