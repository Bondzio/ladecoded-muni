                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HISTORICAL BUILDINGS. (§ 91.9107)

  HISTORICAL BUILDINGS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,592, Eff. 6/28/99, Oper. 7/1/99.)	Qualified historical
buildings may utilize alternate building standards or deviations from this
division to preserve their original or restored architectural elements and
features.  See Section 91.8119 of this Code.
