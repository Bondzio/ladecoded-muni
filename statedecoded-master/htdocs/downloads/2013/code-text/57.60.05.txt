                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MELTING THE BATH. (§ 57.60.05)

  MELTING THE BATH.	In externally heated furnaces which do not melt from the
top to the bottom, a cast iron or steel wedge shall be suspended in center of
bath before it solidifies, for use in remelting.  The wedge shall touch the
bottom of the pot and extend above the surface.
