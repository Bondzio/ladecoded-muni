                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SPECIAL POLICE – FEES. (§ 52.05)

  SPECIAL POLICE – FEES.	The Chief of Police shall make a charge of Two
Dollars for the issuance of every special police officer’s badge and for every
badge issued to replace a lost badge which sum shall be considered as rental for
the use of said badge, which shall at all times be and remain the property of
this City.
