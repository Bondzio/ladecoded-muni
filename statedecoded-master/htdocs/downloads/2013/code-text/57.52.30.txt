                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (§ 57.52.30)

  FIRE PROTECTION.	A.	Only portable fire extinguishers suitable for use on
Class D fires shall be used, in accordance with Division 140 of this
article.	B.	When required by the Chief, fire extinguishing agents suitable for
extinguishment or containment of combustible metal fires shall be stored in
sufficient quantity in sealed containers in all areas where combustible metals
are used or stored.  Shovels and hand scoops shall be kept readily available
adjacent to the sealed containers.  Extinguishing agents shall be clearly
identified and shall be available within 75 feet of the hazard.
