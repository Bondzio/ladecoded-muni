                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OCCUPANCY RESTRICTIONS. (ยง 57.24.05)

ย OCCUPANCY RESTRICTIONS.	Except in approved atmospheric or vacuum chambers,
the use of unabsorbed hydrogen cyanide, carbon disulfide, ethylene oxide, or
other highly flammable substances in fumigation is prohibited in, or within 100
feet of any residential, institutional or assembly occupancy, and in any other
type of occupancy unless the building, ship, or other structure wherein the
fumigation is to be conducted is located at least 50 feet from any other ship or
inhabited structure not under the control of the person having the fumigation
done.	EXCEPTION:	Such distance separation is not required for the use of
absorbed hydrogen cyanide in dosages not exceeding one and one-half pounds per
1,000 cubic feet.
