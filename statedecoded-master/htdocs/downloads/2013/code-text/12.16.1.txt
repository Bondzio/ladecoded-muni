                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

“CW” CENTRAL CITY WEST SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE. (§ 12.16.1)

  “CW” CENTRAL CITY WEST SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 166,704, Eff. 4/3/91.)	The following regulations shall apply
in the “CW” Central City West Specific Plan Zone:	A.	Purpose.  The
regulations set forth in this section are intended to reflect the regulations
contained in the Central City West Specific Plan Ordinance.	B.	Use.  No
building, structure or land shall be used and no building or structure shall be
erected, structurally altered, enlarged or maintained, except as permitted by
Section 5 of the Central City West Specific Plan Ordinance.	C.	Area.  No
building or structure nor the enlargement of any building or structure shall be
erected or maintained unless the yard, area and loading spaces required by
Section 5 of the Central City West Specific Plan are provided and maintained in
connection with the building, structure or enlargement.
