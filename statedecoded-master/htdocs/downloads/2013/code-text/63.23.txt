                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COUNCIL TO APPROVE CERTAIN GRANTS. (§ 63.23)

  COUNCIL TO APPROVE CERTAIN GRANTS.	Before the Board of Playground and
Recreation Commissioners shall make any grant on any beach property for a period
of over five (5) years, the same must be submitted to and approved by the City
Council, and without such approval the Board of Playground and Recreation
Commissioners shall have no power to make such grant.
