                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE DISTRICT REQUIREMENTS. (§ 91.8106)

  FIRE DISTRICT REQUIREMENTS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,758, Eff. 8/8/11.)91.8106.1.  Fire Sprinklers.  In
an existing building in Fire District No. 1, every story which has a floor
surface elevation more than four feet lower than the highest elevation of the
floor landing or tread of any required exit from the story, and is used for
keeping, storing, manufacturing, repairing or processing any combustible
material, shall be sprinklered.	EXCEPTION:  Building that is occupied only as a
single-family dwelling.
