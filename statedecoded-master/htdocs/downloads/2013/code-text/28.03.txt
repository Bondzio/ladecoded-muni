                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAND-BILLS, SIGNS – BUILDINGS PERMISSION. (§ 28.03)

  HAND-BILLS, SIGNS – BUILDINGS PERMISSION.	No person shall paint, mark or
write on or post or otherwise affix or attach any hand-bill or sign to or upon
any building, wall or part thereof, or upon any private property without the
consent of the owner, agent or occupant thereof.
