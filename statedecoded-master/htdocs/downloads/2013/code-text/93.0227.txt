                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FEES FOR CHANGING ADDRESS. (§ 93.0227)

  FEES FOR CHANGING ADDRESS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 166,584, Eff. 1/23/91.)	Whenever it shall become necessary
to make an extra inspection trip because the applicant has given an incorrect or
wrong location when obtaining a permit required by the Code, a fee as specified
in Section 98.0415(a) of this Code shall be paid for correcting the address
location in such permit.
