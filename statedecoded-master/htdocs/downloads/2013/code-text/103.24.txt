                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOURS OF BUSINESS. (§ 103.24)

  HOURS OF BUSINESS.	No person engaging in, managing, conducting or carrying on
any business requiring a permit under this article shall purchase or receive any
article, or carry on or transact such business at other than the hours or days
the Board may allow by rule or regulation.  All permittees engaging in more
than one of the businesses requiring a permit shall observe the hours of
business that are the most restrictive.
