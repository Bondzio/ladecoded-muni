                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GRAVITY DISTRIBUTION. (ยง 57.39.15)

ย GRAVITY DISTRIBUTION.	A.	Gravity distribution systems are prohibited unless
specifically approved by the Chief.	B.	When such lines lead into buildings,
anti-siphon valves shall be installed at the highest point in the supply line
where it leaves the top of the tank. Approved substitute protection may be
provided for highly volatile liquids.
