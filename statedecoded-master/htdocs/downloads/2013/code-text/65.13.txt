  FAILURE TO COMPLY WITH ORDER – BUREAU OF STREET MAINTENANCE MAY REPAIR
PRIVATE STREETS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,596, Eff. 12/7/03.)	Whenever an order to repair any
private street has not been complied with within the time set by the Board or
Director, or within any additional time the Director extends for good cause, the
Board may cause the private street to be repaired by the means the Board deems
advisable.
