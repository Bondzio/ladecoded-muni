                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

UNREINFORCED MASONRY BEARING WALL BUILDINGS. (§ 91.8110)

  UNREINFORCED MASONRY BEARING WALL BUILDINGS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	Existing unreinforced masonry
bearing wall buildings constructed or under construction prior to October 6,
1933, shall conform to the requirements of Division 88 of this Code.
