SOLICITATIONS ON BEHALF OF OTHER PERSONS. (§ 44.08)

  SOLICITATIONS ON BEHALF OF OTHER PERSONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,060, Eff. 3/4/03.)	(a)	No person shall conduct a
solicitation for charitable contributions on behalf of another person without
written authorization from the person on whose behalf the solicitation is
made.	(b)	The written authorization required by Subsection (a) shall be provided
to the Department with the Notice of Intention filed by the person conducting
the solicitation.	(c)	Subsections (a) and (b) shall not apply where the person
conducting the solicitation is  an officer, director, bonafide employee, or
bonafide member of a charitable organization that is operating in compliance
with this article.
