                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CLEANING OF DIP TANKS. (ยง 57.81.20)

ย CLEANING OF DIP TANKS.	All floors and other portions of the premises around
the dip tank and all drain boards, hoods, covers, conveyors, and other
appurtenances used in connection with the dip tank, including the exterior
surface of such tank, shall receive such periodical cleaning as is necessary to
maintain them free from deposits of residue.
