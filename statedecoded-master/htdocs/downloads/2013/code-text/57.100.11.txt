                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EMPTY DRUMS AND CONTAINERS. (ยง 57.100.11)

ย EMPTY DRUMS AND CONTAINERS.	No person shall store upon the premises of any
Auto Fueling Station or Public Garage any container in excess of five gallons
capacity which has contained a Class I liquid, except as required for
transportation or refilling, unless all flammable vapors are removed.
