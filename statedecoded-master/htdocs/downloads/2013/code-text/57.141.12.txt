                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LICENSE REQUIRED. (ยง 57.141.12)

ย LICENSE REQUIRED.	No person shall engage in the business of servicing or
testing automatic fire extinguishing systems without a license issued by the
State Fire Marshal.	EXCEPTIONS:	1.	Persons who engage in the business of
servicing or testing fire alarm devices, water flow alarm devices, or the
supervisory signaling components of automatic fire extinguishing systems shall
not be subject to the licensing requirements.	2.	Systems may be serviced or
tested by, or under the supervision of, an qualified person employed by the
private entity who shall not be subject to the licensing requirement unless such
service or testing is performed for a fee outside of the employment
relationship.
