                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TIME OF REPORTING AND PAYMENT. (§ 21.2.3)

  TIME OF REPORTING AND PAYMENT.	On or before the last day of the calendar
month following the close of each calendar month, every person required to pay
the tax imposed by this article shall render a statement to the Director of
Finance, in the manner provided in Section 21.2.4, of all gross receipts
attributable to the distribution of tickets of admission to or attendance at
Olympic events during the calendar month preceding such statement. At the time
each statement is filed, the full amount of tax, plus any interest and penalties
due under this article for the period covered by such statement shall be paid to
the Director of Finance.
