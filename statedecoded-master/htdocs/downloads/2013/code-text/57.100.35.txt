                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOUSEKEEPING IN GARAGES. (§ 57.100.35)

  HOUSEKEEPING IN GARAGES.	Floors shall be kept clean and free from oil. 
Clear aisle spaces shall be maintained.  Class I or II liquids shall not be
used for cleaning floors.  Metal receptacles with metal covers shall be used
for the storage of oily waste and rubbish.
