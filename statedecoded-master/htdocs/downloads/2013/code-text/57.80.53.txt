                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INFRARED DRYING. (§ 57.80.53)

  INFRARED DRYING.	Automobile refinishing spray booths or enclosures otherwise
installed and maintained in full conformity with this division may alternately
be used for drying with portable infrared drying apparatus when conforming to
the requirements of Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Electrical Code).CLEANING AND
MAINTENANCE
