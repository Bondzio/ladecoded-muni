                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RE-SALE CERTIFICATE. (ยง 21.5.13)

ย RE-SALE CERTIFICATE.	The Director of Finance may at his option accept a State
of California re-sale certificate as evidence that any sale is not a sale for
use or consumption, or he may in his discretion require an affidavit from the
buyer setting forth such information respecting such sale as he deems necessary
to determine the nature of such sale.
