                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 91.7002)

  SCOPE.
	(Title and Section Amended by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	All grading
shall be performed in accordance with the provisions of this division and with
rules and regulations as established by the Superintendent of Building, and
shall be in accordance with the zoning, private street and division of land
regulations contained in Chapter I of the Los Angeles Municipal Code, and the
requirements of the approved General Plan for the area in which the grading is
to be done.
