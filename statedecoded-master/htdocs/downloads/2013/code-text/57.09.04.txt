                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE LANE MAINTENANCE. (§ 57.09.04)

  FIRE LANE MAINTENANCE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	Any person owning or having
control of any facility, structure, group of structures or premises, shall
maintain all fire lanes in an unobstructed manner.	EXCEPTION:	Gates and Gate
Devices approved by the Department.
