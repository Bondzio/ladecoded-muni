                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LASED LOS ANGELES SPORTS AND ENTERTAINMENT DISTRICT SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE. (§
12.16.3)

  LASED LOS ANGELES SPORTS AND ENTERTAINMENT DISTRICT SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 174,225, Eff. 10/13/01.)	The following regulations shall
apply in the “LASED” Los Angeles Sports and Entertainment District Specific
Plan Zone:	A.	Purpose.  The purposes set forth in the Los Angeles Sports and
Entertainment District Specific Plan Ordinance are incorporated by this
reference into these regulations.	B.	Use.  No building, structure or land shall
be used and no building or structure shall be erected, structurally altered,
enlarged or maintained, except as permitted by the Los Angeles Sports and
Entertainment District Specific Plan Ordinance.	C.	Area.  No building or
structure, nor the enlargement of any building or structure, shall be erected or
maintained unless the requirements of the Los Angeles Sports and Entertainment
District Specific Plan Ordinance are met and maintained in connection with the
building, structure or enlargement.
