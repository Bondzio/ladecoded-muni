                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (§ 57.60.11)

  FIRE PROTECTION.	Only carbon dioxide or dry chemical portable fire
extinguishers shall be permitted in the area of baths. Where wet standpipe hoses
or extinguishers employing the use of liquid extinguishing agents are located
within 100 feet of the bath, they shall be labeled with a conspicuous sign,
“WARNING – DO NOT USE ON FIRES IN MOLTEN SALT”.
