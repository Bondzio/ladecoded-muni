                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECORD SEARCH – CHARGE AUTHORIZED. (§ 103.43)

  RECORD SEARCH – CHARGE AUTHORIZED.	The Police Department may furnish
information shown upon records kept pursuant to this article to persons
asserting legal title to the property forming the subject matter of such
records.  A charge of $1.10 shall be collected as to each article of property
for which such record information is requested by any person engaged in the
business of selling property on conditional sales contract basis or by other
deferred payment plan where such request is made in the regular course of such
business and is an incident thereto. (Amended by Ord. No. 137,137, Eff.
9/29/68.)
