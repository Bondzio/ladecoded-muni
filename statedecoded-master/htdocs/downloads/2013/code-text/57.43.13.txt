                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VACUUM RELIEF DEVICE. (§ 57.43.13)

  VACUUM RELIEF DEVICE.	A.	Every special vessel which may be subjected to
vacuum conditions shall be equipped and maintained with one or more
vacuum-relief devices unless constructed to withstand full vacuum in accordance
with the requirements of the Standard under which the special vessel is
constructed.	B.	The capacity of the vacuum relief device shall be sufficient to
compensate for the maximum compressor displacement or the maximum volumetric
liquid withdrawal rate, whichever is the greater.LOCATION AND INSTALLATION OF
