                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FACILITY INSPECTION AND RECORDS. (§ 57.31.40)

  FACILITY INSPECTION AND RECORDS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	The owner, operator, or permittee
of a facility shall conduct or cause to be conducted monthly inspections and
tests of the facility, including the monitoring systems of that facility. 
Records of these inspections and tests shall be maintained for at least three
years and shall be available for inspection by the Chief.  Facilities shall be
inspected annually by the Fire Department.  Every three years, a facility
inspection which includes performance testing of monitoring systems shall be
conducted.  This test shall be witnessed by the Department.
