                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HEATING SPRAY BOOTHS AND SPRAY ROOMS. (§ 57.80.52)

  HEATING SPRAY BOOTHS AND SPRAY ROOMS.	No heating device shall be located,
installed, maintained or used in any spray room.  For comfort of persons
working within the spray room or spray booth, heating appliances of indirect
type may be installed on the outside of such booths or rooms for the purpose of
introducing heat inside thereof, provided the temperature does not exceed
120°F. at the warm air inlet to the booth or tunnel and that location and
arrangement of the heating appliance and the method of heat introduction into
said room is first approved.
