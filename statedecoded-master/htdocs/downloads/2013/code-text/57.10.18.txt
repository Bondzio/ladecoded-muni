ย RUBBISH AND DEBRIS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,176, Eff. 9/20/92.)	All combustible rubbish and debris,
including, but not limited to, properly bagged asbestos, shall be removed and
properly disposed of at the end of each working day, or 24-hour period if work
is continuous 24 hours a day.
