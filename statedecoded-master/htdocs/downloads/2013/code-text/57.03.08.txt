                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ACTING WITHOUT A PERMIT PROHIBITED. (§ 57.03.08)

  ACTING WITHOUT A PERMIT PROHIBITED.	No person shall sell, offer for sale,
install, operate, maintain, or use any appliance, device, equipment, or system
which requires a Permit, a Special Permit, or a General Approval by provisions
of this article, unless such Permit, Special Permit, or General Approval is then
in effect.
