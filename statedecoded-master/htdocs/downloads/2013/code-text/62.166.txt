                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STREET OBSTRUCTIONS BY TREES – JURISDICTION. (§ 62.166)

  STREET OBSTRUCTIONS BY TREES – JURISDICTION.	The Board, through its
authorized officers or employees, shall have charge of enforcing and carrying
out the rules and regulations prescribed under Section 56.08 of this Code. 
(Added by Ord. No. 153,500, Eff. 4/18/80.)
