                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUCTIONEER. (§ 21.55)

  AUCTIONEER.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 166,204, Eff. 10/11/90, Oper. 1/1/91.)	For every
individual person who as an employee or as an independent contractor engages in
the business of auctioneer, the tax shall be $886.88 per year or fractional part
thereof.
