                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BONDING AND GROUNDING OF PIPE LINES. (§ 57.95.21)

  BONDING AND GROUNDING OF PIPE LINES.	Pipe lines on petroleum wharfs shall be
adequately bonded and grounded for protection against stray electrical
currents.  Bonding and grounding connections on all pipe lines shall be located
on wharf side of insulating flanges, if used, accessible for inspection.
