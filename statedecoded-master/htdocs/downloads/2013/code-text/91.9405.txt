                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PRE-DESIGN INVESTIGATION. (§ 91.9405)

  PRE-DESIGN INVESTIGATION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,258, Eff. 8/30/96.)	The engineer or architect shall
prepare a pre-design field investigation report in accordance with Department
guidelines and shall file the report when the plans are filed for permit.  The
plans shall include a description of the existing lateral force resisting system
at and below the base.
