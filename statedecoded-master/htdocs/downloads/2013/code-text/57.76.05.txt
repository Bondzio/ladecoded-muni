                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAZARDOUS MATERIALS CABINET – CONSTRUCTION AND USE. (§ 57.76.05)

  HAZARDOUS MATERIALS CABINET – CONSTRUCTION AND USE.	A.	Hazardous Materials
Cabinets, as referred to herein, shall be constructed in accordance with
L.A.F.D. Standard No. 40.	B.	Not more than 50 pounds of solid or 20 gallons of
liquid organic peroxides shall be stored in a Hazardous Materials
Cabinet.	C.	Not more than one Hazardous Materials Cabinet containing organic
peroxides shall be allowed within a building and such cabinet shall be used
exclusively for the storage of organic peroxides.	D.	The location of Hazardous
Materials Cabinets shall be determined by the Chief.
