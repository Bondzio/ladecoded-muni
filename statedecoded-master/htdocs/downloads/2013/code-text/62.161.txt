                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PLANTING, MAINTENANCE AND CARE OF PLANTS IN CITY STREETS – JURISDICTION OF
BOARD. (§ 62.161)

  PLANTING, MAINTENANCE AND CARE OF PLANTS IN CITY STREETS – JURISDICTION OF
BOARD.	The Board, through its authorized officers and employees, shall exercise
jurisdiction and control over the planting, maintenance and care of trees,
plants and shrubs in all streets of the City.  (Added by Ord. No. 153,500, Eff.
4/18/80.)
