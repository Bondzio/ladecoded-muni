                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REQUIRED ELEVATORS. (§ 92.0109)

  REQUIRED ELEVATORS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 174,861, Eff. 11/14/02, Oper. 11/1/02.)	Every inhabited
building provided with a passenger elevator after the effective date of this
Section shall maintain at least one operational passenger elevator serving all
occupied levels of the building, regardless of the number or
stories.	EXCEPTION:  The building may be inhabited for a limited period of
time, subject to the  approval of the Department, without an operational
elevator during periods of elevator maintenance, repair or replacement.
