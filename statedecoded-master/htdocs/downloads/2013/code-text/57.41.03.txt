                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPROVAL OF DEVICES. (§ 57.41.03)

  APPROVAL OF DEVICES.	All devices which are used in connection with portable
container systems regulated hereunder shall be of a type and construction
suitable for their intended use and approved by the Chief. The Chief shall
approve devices upon satisfactory evidence that they are designed and
constructed for safe operation. The Chief shall classify as approved those
devices that have been approved, listed, or labeled by any approved testing
agency.
