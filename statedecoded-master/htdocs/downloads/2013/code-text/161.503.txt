                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS. (§ 161.503)

  PERMITS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	No building or structure regulated
by this article shall be erected, constructed, enlarged, altered, repaired,
moved, improved, removed, converted or demolished unless a separate permit for
each building or structure has first been obtained from the Department of
Building and Safety in the manner and according to the applicable conditions
prescribed in Chapter IX of this Code.
