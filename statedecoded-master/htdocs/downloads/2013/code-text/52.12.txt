                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

UNAUTHORIZED WEARING OF OFFICIAL POLICE OFFICER’S BADGE PROHIBITED. (§ 52.12)

  UNAUTHORIZED WEARING OF OFFICIAL POLICE OFFICER’S BADGE PROHIBITED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 177,374, Eff. 4/9/06.)	No person who is not a Regular
Police Officer or Reserve Police Officer shall wear, display or carry an
Official Police Officer’s Badge.
