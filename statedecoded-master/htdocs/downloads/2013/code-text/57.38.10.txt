                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INTERCONNECTION OF VEHICLES. (§ 57.38.10)

  INTERCONNECTION OF VEHICLES.	A.	No tank vehicle used for transporting
flammable or combustible liquids shall be connected with a vehicle transporting
any other material unless specifically approved by the Chief.	B.	Tank vehicles
and tank trailers shall not be interconnected by a transfer or discharge hose
while in transit.
