                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOUSE MOVING – PERMIT. (§ 62.172)

  HOUSE MOVING – PERMIT.	No person shall move any building or other object
along any street in such manner as to injure any tree or shrub in such street
without first having obtained a permit therefor from the Board, and said Board
may, if deemed advisable, require any person moving a building or any other
object along a street in said City to furnish a bond in an amount sufficient to
cover the damage or destruction of trees or shrubs on any street.  (Added by
Ord. No. 153,500, Eff. 4/18/80.)
