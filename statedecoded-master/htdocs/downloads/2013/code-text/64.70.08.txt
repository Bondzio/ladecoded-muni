  REMEDIES NOT EXCLUSIVE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,176, Eff. 10/1/98.)	Remedies provided for the
enforcement of this article are in addition to and do not supersede or limit any
and all other remedies provided by law.  The remedies provided herein are
cumulative and not exclusive.
