                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FAN SHAFTS. (§ 57.80.43)

  FAN SHAFTS.	Fans shall be mounted on a shaft sufficiently heavy to maintain
perfect alignment even when the blades of the fans are coated with residue. 
Bearing shall be of the self-lubricating type or shall be lubricated from
outside of the duct or pipe.
