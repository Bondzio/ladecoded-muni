                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

Q. (§ 95.219.0)

  	Section 219.0 of the C.M.C. is adopted by reference with the following
addition:	QUICK DISCONNECT DEVICE is a hand-operated device which provides a
means for connecting and disconnecting an appliance or an appliance connector to
a gas supply.  The device is equipped with an automatic means to shut off the
gas supply when the device is disconnected.
