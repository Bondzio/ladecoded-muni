                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INSULATION. (§ 57.84.05)

  INSULATION.	The walls, roof panels, or other exposed surfaces of every drying
oven shall be sufficiently insulated as to prevent the outside surface
temperature from exceeding 160°F.  In lieu of such insulation, the Chief may
approve for existing ovens, guards, or barriers designed to protect personnel
and combustible materials from contact with such heated surface.
