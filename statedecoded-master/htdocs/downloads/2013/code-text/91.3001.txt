                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 91.3001)

  GENERAL.	Section 3001 of the CBC is adopted by reference, except that
Sections 3001.1, 3001.2 and 3001.4 of the CBC are not adopted and in lieu,
Sections 91.3001.1, 91.3001.2 and 91.3001.4 are added.91.3001.1.  Scope.  This
division governs the design, construction, installation, alteration and repair
of elevators and conveying systems and their components.  All elevators shall
comply with the additional requirements of the Elevator Code.  Whenever a
conflict exists between this Division and the Elevator Code, the more
restrictive of the two codes shall apply. 91.3001.2.  Referenced Standards. 
Except as otherwise provided for in this Code, the design, construction,
installation, alteration, repair and maintenance of elevators and conveying
systems and their components shall conform to the Elevator Code, ASME A90.1,
ASME B20.1, ALI ALCTV, and ASCE 24 for construction in flood hazard areas
established in CBC Section 1612.3.91.3001.4.  Change in Use.  A change in use
of an elevator from freight to passenger, passenger to freight, or from one
freight class to another freight class shall comply with the Elevator Code.
