                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.52.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall regulate the storage, handling,
and use of combustible metals. Included in this division are requirements
regulating Permits, fire protection, and other measures and general precautions
relating to combustible metals.  Unless otherwise regulated by this division,
compliance with L.A.F.D. Standard No. 64 is required.MAGNESIUM
