                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.84.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall regulate drying ovens which are
not an integral part of a building or room.  Drying ovens, not specifically
regulated in this division shall be constructed, installed, and maintained, in
compliance with the provisions of L.A.F.D. Standard No. 7 of this article.
