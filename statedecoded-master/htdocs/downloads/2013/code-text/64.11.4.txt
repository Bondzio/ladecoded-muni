                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISPOSITION OF SEWERAGE FACILITIES CHARGE. (§ 64.11.4)

  DISPOSITION OF SEWERAGE FACILITIES CHARGE.	All sewerage facilities charges
collected July 1, 1979, and thereafter, for the City shall be placed and
deposited in the Sewer Construction and Maintenance Fund established by Section
64.19.2 of this Code, such monies to be expended for the purposes set forth in
said section and to pay for any refunds, billing, collection and administration
costs relating to the sewerage facilities charge.  (Added by Ord. No. 151,859,
Eff. 2/19/79.)
