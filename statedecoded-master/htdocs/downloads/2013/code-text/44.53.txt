                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REGULATION OF GAMES. (§ 44.53)

  REGULATION OF GAMES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 181,289, Eff. 10/3/10.)	All bingo games, and all
activities in connection therewith shall be conducted in accordance with the
provisions of Sections 326.3 and 326.5 of the Penal Code of the State of
California, as amended from time to time.
