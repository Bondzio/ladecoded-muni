                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TITLE. (§ 93.0101)

  TITLE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,593, Eff. 6/28/99, Oper. 7/1/99.)

	This ordinance shall be known as the “Los Angeles Electrical Code”, a
portion of the “Los Angeles Municipal Code”.  Whenever the word “Code”
is used in this ordinance, it shall mean the “Los Angeles Electrical Code”
and whenever “LAMC” is used, it shall refer to the “Los Angeles Municipal
Code”.  References to the “C.E.C.” and the “C.B.S.C.” shall mean the
“California Electrical Code” and the “California Building Standards
Codes” respectively.
