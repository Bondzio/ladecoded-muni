                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (§ 57.92.16)

  FIRE PROTECTION.	A.	An approved fire protection system shall be provided in
every Bulk Distributing Station building in which Class I or II liquids are
handled outside of closed systems.	B.	Portable fire extinguishers shall be
maintained and distributed at every Bulk Distributing Station as specified in
Division 140 of this article.  At least one extinguisher with a minimum
classification of 20B:C shall be provided at each loading or unloading facility.
