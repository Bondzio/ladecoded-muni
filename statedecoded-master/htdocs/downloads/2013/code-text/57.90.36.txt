                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION FOR OIL WELLS. (§ 57.90.36)

  FIRE PROTECTION FOR OIL WELLS.	At all times when conducting drilling
operations at an oil well, there shall be available within 100 feet of such well
not less than two portable fire extinguishers, each having a 20B:C
classification in accordance with Division 140 of this article.
