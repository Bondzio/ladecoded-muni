                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OIL – SPILLING ON STREETS. (§ 62.79)

  OIL – SPILLING ON STREETS.	No person shall pour, spill, or permit to drip
from any tank vehicle, upon any asphalt or bituminous pavement laid upon any
street, any oil, petroleum kerosene, benzine or other similar oil or oily
substance or liquid.
