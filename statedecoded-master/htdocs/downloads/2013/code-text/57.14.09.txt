                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS – VALIDITY. (§ 57.14.09)

  PERMITS – VALIDITY.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,043, Eff. 7/13/98.)	Unless otherwise set forth on the
face of the Permit, every Permit issued in accordance with the provisions of
this division shall be valid until voided, revoked, or suspended.
