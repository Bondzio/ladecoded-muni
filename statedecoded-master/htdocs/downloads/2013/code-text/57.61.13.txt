                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS. (§ 57.61.13)

  OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS.	Operating instructions provided by the equipment
manufacturer shall be posted at the equipment for reference.  These
instructions shall include schematic piping and wiring diagrams, and the
following:	1.	Start-up procedures.	2.	Shutdown procedures.	3.	Emergency
procedures.	4.	Maintenance procedures.
