                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.140.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall regulate the installation,
servicing, maintenance, testing, and use of all portable fire extinguishers.
Unless otherwise specifically provided in this division, the provisions of
L.A.F.D. Standard No. 1 and Title 19, C.A.C., shall apply.  This division does
not apply to automatic fire extinguishing systems.
