                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FAN MOTORS. (§ 57.80.41)

  FAN MOTORS.	The fan motors shall not be installed inside of spray rooms,
spray booths or spray tunnels nor shall any belt or chain-driven apparatus be
inside any such rooms, booths, tunnels, ducts or pipes unless such belt or chain
or any pulley in connection therewith is entirely enclosed and grounded, or
unless otherwise approved for the specific application and location by the
Chief.
