                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EXCESS CHARGES. (§ 71.24)

  EXCESS CHARGES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 165,491, Eff. 3/19/90.)	No person shall charge, collect,
demand, receive or arrange for any compensation for the service of any vehicle
defined in Section 71.00 in this City used for the conveyance of passengers, any
amount, rate, or compensation different than the charges or rates established by
the Board.
