                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPLIANCE WITH TRAFFIC CONTROL MANUAL. (§ 61.06)

  COMPLIANCE WITH TRAFFIC CONTROL MANUAL.
	(Added by Ord. No. 142,123, Eff. 7/31/71.)	All work involving City property or
rights of way shall be performed in accordance with the provisions of the latest
edition of the manual entitled “Work Area Traffic Control” adopted by the
Board.
