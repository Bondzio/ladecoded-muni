FOAM EQUIPMENT – ARRANGEMENT. (§ 57.95.09)

  FOAM EQUIPMENT – ARRANGEMENT.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	Where wet solution foam systems
are used, there shall be a sufficient number of foam solution outlets and hose
on the wharf to provide a minimum of one effective foam line for all portions of
the wharf or tank vessel.  The hose shall be immediately available on the wharf
or adjacent thereto when any tank vessel is being loaded.  The hose shall be on
