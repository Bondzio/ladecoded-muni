                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SELLING GOODS NEAR PRIVATE PARKS. (§ 41.43)

  SELLING GOODS NEAR PRIVATE PARKS.	No person shall expose, sell or offer for
sale, any goods, wares or merchandise of any kind whatsoever in, upon or along
any street or sidewalk, within 200 feet of any entrance to any private or
amusement park.  (Added by Ord. No. 111,348, Eff. 7/4/58.)
