                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.122.01)

  SCOPE.	This division regulates the design and installation of all fire
protective signaling systems operating at 600 volts or less.  Unless otherwise
specifically provided in this article, the provisions of Title 19 and Part 2 –
Title 24, C.A.C., and L.A.F.D. Standard No. 39 shall apply.  When provisions of
this division are in conflict with L.A.F.D. Standard No. 39, the requirements of
this division shall apply.
