                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE OF REVIEW. (§ 161.1002.4)

.  SCOPE OF REVIEW.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	The Appeals Board review of the
General Manager’s decision shall be limited to those alleged errors of law or
fact occurring at the hearing specified in the Notice of Appeal.  The Board
shall consider any briefs submitted by the parties and any oral argument.  The
Board shall not consider any evidence not presented at the General Manager’s
hearing, unless it is newly discovered evidence which could not, with due
diligence, have been discovered and produced at the General Manager’s
hearing.  Compliance with orders to comply subsequent to the General
Manager’s hearing shall not be considered by the Board.  The Appeals Board
shall have no authority relative to interpretation of the administrative
provisions of this article and the Board shall not be empowered to waive
requirements of this article.	The decision of the General Manager to refer the
property to the City Attorney for criminal prosecution is not subject to appeal.
