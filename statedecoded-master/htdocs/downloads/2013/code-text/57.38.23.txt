                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONSTRUCTION OF AIRCRAFT FUEL SERVICING TANK VEHICLES. (§ 57.38.23)

  CONSTRUCTION OF AIRCRAFT FUEL SERVICING TANK VEHICLES.	The design and
construction of aircraft fuel servicing tank vehicles shall conform to the
provisions of Section 57.38.03 of this Division and L.A.F.D. Standard No. 60.
