                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VALVES. (§ 57.95.19)

  VALVES.	Pipe lines carrying petroleum products shall be equipped with a
minimum of three approved valves located as follows:	One on the wharf, one
within 200 feet of the shore bulkhead line, outside of any diked area,
accessible for emergency use and one at the source of supply.  The valve at the
shore bulkhead line shall normally be open.
