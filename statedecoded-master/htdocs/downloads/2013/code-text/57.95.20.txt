                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ACCESS TO VALVES AND PIPE LINES. (§ 57.95.20)

  ACCESS TO VALVES AND PIPE LINES.	Access shall be provided to valves and
connections of pipe lines below wharf deck.  No freight or other material shall
be placed on petroleum wharfs in such a manner as to interfere with the opening
of trap doors which provide access to valves and pipe connections below wharf
deck.  If trap doors are used, they shall be conspicuously marked.
