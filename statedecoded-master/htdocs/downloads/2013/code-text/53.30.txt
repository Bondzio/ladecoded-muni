                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

KEEPING OF DISEASED OR CRIPPLED ANIMALS. (ยง 53.30)

ย KEEPING OF DISEASED OR CRIPPLED ANIMALS.	No person shall have, keep, or
harbor any animal which is known or believed by him to be infected with any
dangerous or communicable disease, or which is in an incurable crippled
condition, or which is afflicted with any painful disease which is believed by
such person to be incurable, except as in this article otherwise provided.
