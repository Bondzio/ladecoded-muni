                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

UNATTENDED PARKING – PROHIBITED. (§ 62.139)

  UNATTENDED PARKING – PROHIBITED.
	(Added by Ord. No. 82.963, Eff. 7/7/40.)	It shall be unlawful to park or to
leave an unattended overload in and upon any street, alley or other public way
in the City of Los Angeles between sunset and sunrise. It shall be moved off the
traveled way or pavement so as to interfere in no way with passing traffic.
