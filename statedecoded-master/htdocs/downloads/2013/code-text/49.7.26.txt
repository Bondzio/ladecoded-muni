                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NOTICE REGARDING EXPENDITURE CEILINGS. (§ 49.7.26)

  NOTICE REGARDING EXPENDITURE CEILINGS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 182,362, Eff. 1/30/13.)	A candidate shall notify the
Ethics Commission on the day the candidate raises more than 100 percent of the
applicable expenditure ceiling and again on the day the candidate spends more
than 100 percent of the applicable expenditure limit.  The Ethics Commission
shall notify all other candidates for the same office within one business day of
receiving the candidate’s notice.
