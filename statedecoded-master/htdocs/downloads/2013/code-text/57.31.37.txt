INTEGRITY TESTING OF UNDERGROUND TANKS. (§ 57.31.37)

  INTEGRITY TESTING OF UNDERGROUND TANKS.
	(Title and Section amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	Existing
underground tanks and piping shall be precision tested for tightness by an
approved method capable of meeting the requirements of NFPA 329 (1987 Edition),
on an annual basis.	Precision tests shall be performed by qualified technical
personnel experienced in the use of the test procedure and licensed by the State
of California in accordance with CCR Section 2751, Title 23.	Whenever a leak is
detected, the Chief shall be notified in accordance with Section 57.31.47 B of
this article.
