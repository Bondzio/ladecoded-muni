                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMMUNICATIONS SYSTEMS – POSSESSION OF KEYS FOR. (§ 63.117)

  COMMUNICATIONS SYSTEMS – POSSESSION OF KEYS FOR.	No person shall have in
his possession or under his control any key belonging to or fitted to open the
lock of any city communications box subject to the jurisdiction of the
Department of General Services, or to make or fit any such key without the
permission of the Board of Fire Commissioners, the Chief Engineer of the Fire
Department, the Board of Police Commissioners, the Chief of Police or the
Department of General Services. (Amended by Ord. No. 152,426, Eff. 6/29/79,
Oper. 7/1/79.)
