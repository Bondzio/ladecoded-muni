                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NEW PERMIT APPLICATION AFTER DENIAL OR REVOCATION. (§ 103.38)

  NEW PERMIT APPLICATION AFTER DENIAL OR REVOCATION.	When the permit of any
person is revoked for cause, no new or other application for a permit from the
same person shall be accepted within one year after such revocation.  When an
application for a permit is denied for cause, no new or other application for a
permit from the same person shall be accepted within one year after denial,
unless the applicant can show a material change in his situation which would
justify the issuance of such permit.
