                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TAXICAB – MISREPRESENTING IDENTITY. (§ 43.18)

  TAXICAB – MISREPRESENTING IDENTITY.	No owner, driver or agent soliciting
patronage for any automobile for hire or taxicab shall misrepresent by word,
sign, hat band, insignia I or badge the true identity of the vehicle for which
such patronage is sought
