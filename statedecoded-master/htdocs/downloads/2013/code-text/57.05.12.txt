                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

L.A.F.D. LABEL. (§ 57.05.12)

  L.A.F.D. LABEL.	Every appliance, device, equipment, or system for which a
General Approval has been granted shall have attached thereto a metal label, or
other approved means of identification, containing the following
information:	A.	The name of the manufacturer thereof.	B.	The letter
“L.A.F.D.” together with the General Approval number.	C.	If a tank or
container, the gauge of thickness of metal of which it is constructed and the
capacity thereof in gallons.	D.	Such other information as may be required as a
condition of the General Approval.
