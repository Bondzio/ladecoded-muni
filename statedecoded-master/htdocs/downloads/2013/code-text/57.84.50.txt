                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR. (§ 57.84.50)

  MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR.	All drying ovens, equipment, and any ducts or pipes
in connection therewith shall be maintained at all times in good repair.  No
person shall make any alterations or repairs thereon or thereto, nor permit them
to be made before any such ovens and all equipment, ducts and pipes in
connection therewith shall have been thoroughly cleaned of residue and made gas
and vapor free.
