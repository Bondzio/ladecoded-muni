                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GATE ENTRANCES TO SCHOOL GROUNDS. (§ 57.111.07)

  GATE ENTRANCES TO SCHOOL GROUNDS.	Any school ground that is entirely enclosed
by fences or walls shall provide one or more gates in such fences or walls in a
location as approved by the Chief.  The gates shall be not less than 20 feet in
width to permit the entrance of emergency apparatus.  Gates, when equipped with
locking devices, shall be readily openable by chain or bolt cutting devices.
