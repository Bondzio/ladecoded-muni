                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONDITIONS POSING SERIOUS RISK. (§ 161.704.4)

  CONDITIONS POSING SERIOUS RISK.
	(Added by Ord. No. 173,011, Eff. 1/30/00.)	If the condition poses a serious
risk to the health or safety of the occupants or the public, the inspector shall
order the condition to be abated in not more than 14 days.  No extensions of
time shall be granted except by the General Manager or his or her designee.
