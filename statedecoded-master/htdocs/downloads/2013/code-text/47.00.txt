POSTING OF TELEPHONE NUMBER OF THE FIRE DEPARTMENT EMERGENCY AMBULANCE SERVICE
IN EATING ESTABLISHMENTS. (§ 47.00)

  POSTING OF TELEPHONE NUMBER OF THE FIRE DEPARTMENT EMERGENCY AMBULANCE
SERVICE IN EATING ESTABLISHMENTS.
	(Added by No. 148,123, Eff. 4/11/76.)

	Every person conducting, operating or managing any restaurant, cafeteria,
coffee shop, food stand or other eating establishment where food is sold to be
consumed on the premises thereof shall display near each telephone located upon
said premises the local telephone number to summon the Fire Department Emergency
Ambulance Service for the area involved. Said telephone number shall be set
forth as follows:

“EMERGENCY CALLS
AMBULANCE (telephone number)”
