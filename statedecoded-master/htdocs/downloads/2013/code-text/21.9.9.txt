                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECORDER ADMINISTERS. (§ 21.9.9)

  RECORDER ADMINISTERS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 166,976, Eff. 7/3/91, Oper. 7/1/91.)	The County Recorder
shall administer this article in conformity with the provisions of Part 6.7 of
Division 2 of the Revenue and Taxation Code and the provisions of any County
ordinance adopted pursuant thereto.  The Director of Finance of the City of Los
Angeles is authorized to negotiate and enter into a contract with the County of
Los Angeles or one of its officials for the administration of this article and
payment to the County for its costs of administration.
