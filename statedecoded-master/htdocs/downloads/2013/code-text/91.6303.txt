                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SERVICE STATIONS. (§ 91.6303)

  SERVICE STATIONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 180,619, Eff. 5/12/09.)	Every service station located
within 660 feet of an accessible right-of-way of any interstate or primary
highway, as defined in Sections 5215 and 5220 of the Business and Professions
Code, shall provide, during business hours, public restrooms for use by its
customers.  The public restroom shall not be temporary or portable but shall be
permanent and shall include separate facilities for men and women, each with
toilets and sinks suitable for use by disabled persons in accordance with
Division 11 of this article and shall be maintained in a clean and sanitary
manner.  This section shall not apply to service stations which are fully
operational prior to January 1, 1990.
