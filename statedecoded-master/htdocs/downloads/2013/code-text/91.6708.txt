                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DOORS – GENERAL. (§ 91.6708)

  DOORS – GENERAL.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 171,858, Eff. 1/23/98.)	Every door in a security opening
shall be constructed, installed and secured as set forth in Sections 91.6709,
91.6710, 91.6711 and 91.6712.  Glazing in doors shall comply with Section
91.6713.	Every door in a security opening for an apartment house shall be
provided with an incandescent light bulb (minimum of 60 watts) at a maximum
height of eight feet (2438 mm) or lights on the exterior side of the unit that
have a minimum surface illumination of 0.2 footcandles (2.15 lx) at the security
opening.  Where, in any specific case, different sections of the Los Angeles
Municipal Code specify different requirements, the most restrictive shall
govern.
