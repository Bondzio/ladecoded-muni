                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPRESSORS. (§ 57.43.32)

  COMPRESSORS.	A.	The refrigeration system shall include at least two
compressors, each of which shall be capable of handling the refrigeration load
imposed by Subsection A of Section 57.43.30.  The total compressor capacity
shall be not less than the total refrigeration load.	B.	shall be suitable for
continuous refrigeration service at the pressures and temperatures to be
encountered.	C.	Compressors shall be equipped with suction and discharge shutoff
valves, discharge relief valve and suction and discharge pressure gauges.
