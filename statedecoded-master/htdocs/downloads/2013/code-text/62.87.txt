                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOUSE MOVER – MUST PAY LICENSE. (§ 62.87)

  HOUSE MOVER – MUST PAY LICENSE.	No permit shall be issued to any house
mover pursuant to the terms of Sections 62.84 to 62.93 inclusive, unless such
house mover shall have taken out the necessary license as provided for in
Chapter II Article 1 of this Code.
