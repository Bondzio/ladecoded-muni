                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OIL CABLES – PERMIT CONTENTS. (§ 62.76)

  OIL CABLES – PERMIT CONTENTS.	Each permit issued by the Board pursuant to
Section 62.74 shall state the exact location where the rope, wire or cable for
which such permit is issued shall be erected, maintained or operated. No such
permit shall be granted until after the application therefor shall have been
referred to the Oil Inspector for his report thereon.
