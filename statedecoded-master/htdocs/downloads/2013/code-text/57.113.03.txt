                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE CLEARANCES. (§ 57.113.03)

  FIRE CLEARANCES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 169,944, Eff. 8/20/94.)	When all applicable provisions of
this article and  the California Code of Regulations are complied with, the
Chief may, upon request, issue a Fire Clearance to appropriate licensing
agencies for any institutional occupancy governed by this article.
