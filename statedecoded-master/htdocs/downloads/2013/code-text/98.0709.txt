ย ENTRY OR INTERFERENCE WITH NOTICE PROHIBITED.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,782, Eff. 9/30/99.)	(a)	It is unlawful for any person to
enter or occupy any structure or premises which has been posted pursuant to
Section 91.8903.1.5, 91.8904.1, or 98.0708(e) of this Code, except to repair or
demolish the structure under proper permit or for a purpose authorized by the
owner.	(b)	It is unlawful for any person to remove or deface any notice posted
pursuant to Section 91.8903.1.5, 91.8904.1, 98.0708(e) or 98.0714 of this Code.
