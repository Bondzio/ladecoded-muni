                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISTANCE – BARRIER SEPARATIONS. (§ 57.51.05)

  DISTANCE – BARRIER SEPARATIONS.	Work areas where hazardous plastics are
handled in a dry state shall be separated by a distance of not less than five
feet from other such areas, or by a noncombustible or fire resistive barricade
so that a fire in one work area will not involve adjacent work areas.
