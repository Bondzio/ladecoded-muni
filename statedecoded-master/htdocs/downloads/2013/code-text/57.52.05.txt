                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FABRICATED MAGNESIUM STORAGE. (ยง 57.52.05)

ย FABRICATED MAGNESIUM STORAGE.	The size of storage piles of magnesium
articles, light castings, and sheets, in foundries and processing plants shall
not exceed 1,250 cubic feet and shall be separated by aisles not less in width
than one-half the height of the piles.
