                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONCEALED WEAPONS – PERMITS – FEE. (§ 55.03)

  CONCEALED WEAPONS – PERMITS – FEE.	The Chief of Police shall keep a
record of all permits authorized by the Board of Police Commissioners under this
article. No permit required by Section 55.01 shall be issued to any person until
such person shall have paid to the Chief of Police a registration fee of Three
($3.00) Dollars. On Monday of each week the Chief of Police shall pay to the
City Treasurer all such registration fees received during the preceding week.
