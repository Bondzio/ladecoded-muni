                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REINSPECTION FEE. (§ 98.0715)

  REINSPECTION FEE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,782, Eff. 9/30/99.)	The Superintendent may periodically
reinspect Vacant Structures to ensure compliance with the provisions of this
division and all applicable court and administrative orders.  The
Superintendent may impose a reinspection fee against the Responsible Person for
actual costs of each reinspection and continuous monitoring of the structure and
premises as is reasonably necessary to determine compliance with the standards
and procedures in this division.  The Superintendent shall follow the
procedures set forth in Sections 91.8904.3, 98.0411 or 98.0412 of this Code.
