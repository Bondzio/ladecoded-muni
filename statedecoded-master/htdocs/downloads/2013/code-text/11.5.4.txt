                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CITY PLANNING COMMISSION. (§ 11.5.4)

  CITY PLANNING COMMISSION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173,268, Eff. 7/1/00, Oper. 7/1/00.)	In addition to the
duties set forth in the Charter, the shall adopt guidelines for the
administration of the provisions of this chapter if it determines that
guidelines are necessary and appropriate.  Authority to adopt guidelines for
the administration of the provisions of this chapter may be delegated to others
by adoption of a resolution by Council.  Existing provisions of this chapter
that delegate authority for the adoption of guidelines to others shall continue
to apply with respect to those provisions.
