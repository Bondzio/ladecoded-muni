VACCINATION CERTIFICATES, PROOF OF STERILIZATION AND OTHER PROOF TO BE RETAINED
BY OWNER. (§ 53.54)

  VACCINATION CERTIFICATES, PROOF OF STERILIZATION AND OTHER PROOF TO BE
RETAINED BY OWNER.
	(Title and Section Amended by Ord. No. 181,882, Eff. 10/30/11.)	A copy of the
vaccination certificate or other proof of vaccination and proof of sterilization
or proof of exemption from sterilization, in a form acceptable to the
Department, shall be retained by the owner or custodian of any dog and where
applicable, for any cat, for inspection by the authorized representatives of the
Department.
