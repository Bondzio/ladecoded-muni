ย FAN BLADES.	All fan blades shall be rigid and of a non-sparking electrical
conducting material, or the fan shall have inlet nose and drive side of cone
lined with non-sparking material, with ample clearance to avoid creating fire by
friction, necessary allowance being made for ordinary expansion and loading or
coating to prevent contact between any moving part of the spray room, spray
