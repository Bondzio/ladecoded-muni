                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PIPING TO ROOF. (§ 57.86.07)

  PIPING TO ROOF.	A.	Piping or tubing used to transfer heated material to the
roof shall be Schedule 40 or equivalent.  Flexible steel piping shall be of an
approved type.  Flexible piping shall not be used anywhere in the system except
at the connection immediately adjacent to the pump or kettle and shall not
exceed six feet in length.	B.	Remote operated bypass valves used in a single
supply line system shall be of a fail-safe type which opens automatically if the
control line or lanyard fails.	C.	All piping shall be firmly supported at not
more than 12-foot intervals.
