VEHICLE THEFT ALARM SYSTEMS. (§ 114.06)

  VEHICLE THEFT ALARM SYSTEMS.
	(Former Sec. 114.05, Renumbered by Ord. No. 164,532, Eff. 4/20/89.)	It shall be
unlawful for any person to install, operate or use any vehicle theft alarm
system that emits or causes the emission of an audible sound, which is not, or
does not become, automatically and completely silenced within five minutes. The
time period shall be calculated based upon the emission of the first audible
sound and shall end five minutes thereafter notwithstanding any variation or
stoppage in the emissions of audible sound.  Violation of this section shall
constitute an infraction.
