INVESTIGATION FEE. (ยง 93.0212)

ย INVESTIGATION FEE.	Whenever any work, for which a permit is required under
the provisions of this Code, has been commenced without the authorization of
such permit, a special investigation shall be made before a permit may be issued
for such work. In addition to the permit fee, an investigation fee shall be
collected. (For fee charge, see Los Angeles Municipal Code Section 98.0402 in
the appendix.)
