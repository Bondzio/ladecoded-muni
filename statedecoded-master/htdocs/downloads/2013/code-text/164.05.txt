                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF MAINTENANCE REQUIREMENTS. (§ 164.05)

  MAINTENANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF MAINTENANCE REQUIREMENTS.	Properties shall be
maintained by the beneficiary/ trustee in accordance with the standards set
forth in Section 91.8104.	The provisions of Article 1, Division 89 (Section
91.8901, et seq.) and all requirements described therein shall apply to
properties subject to this section.  Should a property be deemed a nuisance,
hazardous, or substandard by the City, the abatement procedures described in
Article 1, Division 89 may be initiated against beneficiary/trustee by the City.
