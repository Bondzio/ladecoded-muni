                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION OF LICENSES. (§ 97.0208)

  SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION OF LICENSES.	The Department shall have the power and
authority to suspend any license for a maximum of 60 days, or to revoke the
license, for the violation of any of the provisions of this Code.	Any suspension
or revocation shall be in accordance with the provisions of Article 8, Chapter
IX of this Code.
