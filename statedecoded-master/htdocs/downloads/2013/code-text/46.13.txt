                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISCLOSURE OF HAZARDS. (§ 46.13)

  DISCLOSURE OF HAZARDS.	No over-the-counter drug containing caffeine,
ephedrine or phenylpropanolamine shall be displayed or offered for sale, or sold
unless there appears on the package or container in which said drug is displayed
or offered for sale, or sold, a label stating “WARNING, DO NOT EXCEED THE
RECOMMENDED DOSAGE FOR THIS DRUG AS STATED ON THE LABEL”. Such warning shall
be in red lettering.
