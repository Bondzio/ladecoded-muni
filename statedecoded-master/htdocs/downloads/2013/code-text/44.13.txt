                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOURS OF SOLICITATION. (ยง 44.13)

ย HOURS OF SOLICITATION.	(a)	No person shall solicit any charitable
contribution door-to-door between the hours of 8:00 p.m. and 8:00 a.m.	(b)	No
child under the age of 10 years shall solicit any charitable contribution unless
accompanied by a responsible person who is 18 years of age or older and who
shall at all times keep such child within the range of his or her sight and
hearing.	(c)	No child under the age of 16 years shall solicit any charitable
contributions between the time of sunset and 9:00 a.m.
