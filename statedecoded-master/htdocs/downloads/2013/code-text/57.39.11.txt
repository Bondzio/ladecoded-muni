                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RESTRICTED MATERIALS – TEMPERATURE OR PRESSURE. (§ 57.39.11)

  RESTRICTED MATERIALS – TEMPERATURE OR PRESSURE.	A.	Malleable iron pipe and
fittings shall not be used in systems designed to operate at temperatures in
excess of 500°F. or at pressures in excess of 400 psig.	B.	Cast iron pipe and
fittings shall not be used in systems designed to operate at temperatures in
excess of 300°F. or at pressures in excess of 400 psig.	C.	Low melting point
materials shall not be used in systems designed to operate at temperatures in
excess of 400°F.	D.	Plastic pipe and fittings shall not be used at temperatures
or pressures which the Chief determines would constitute a fire hazard.
