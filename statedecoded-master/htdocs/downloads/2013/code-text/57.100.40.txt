                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SIGNS – NO SMOKING – STOP MOTOR. (§ 57.100.40)

  SIGNS – NO SMOKING – STOP MOTOR.	“NO SMOKING – STOP MOTOR” signs
having letters at least three inches high on sharply contrasting backgrounds
shall be conspicuously posted in every Auto Fueling Station and in every Public
Garage where Class I, II and IIIA liquids are used or dispensed.
