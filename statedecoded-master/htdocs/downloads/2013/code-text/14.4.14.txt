                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WINDOW SIGNS. (§ 14.4.14)

  WINDOW SIGNS.	A.	Area.  The total area of all window signs shall not exceed
ten percent of the area of the window.	B.	Combined Area.  The combined sign
area of wall signs, projecting signs, monument signs, illuminated architectural
canopy signs, pole signs, roof signs and window signs shall not exceed four
square feet for each foot of street frontage.
