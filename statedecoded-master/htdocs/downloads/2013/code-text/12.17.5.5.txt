                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

“CCS” CENTURY CITY SOUTH SPECIFIC PLAN STUDIO ZONE. (§ 12.17.5.5)

  “CCS” CENTURY CITY SOUTH SPECIFIC PLAN STUDIO ZONE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,861, Eff. 8/7/93.)	The following purpose and regulations
shall apply in the CCS Century City South Specific Plan Studio
Zone:	A.	Purpose.  The regulations set forth in this section are intended to
reflect the regulations contained in the Century City South Specific Plan
Ordinance.	B.	Use.  No building, structure or land shall be used and no
building or structure shall be erected, structurally altered, enlarged or
maintained, except as permitted by Section 6B of the Century City South Specific
Plan Ordinance.	C.	Area.  No building or structure, nor the enlargement of any
building or structure, shall be erected or maintained unless the yard and area
requirements of Section 6B of the Century City South Specific Plan are provided
and maintained in connection with the building, structure or enlargement.
