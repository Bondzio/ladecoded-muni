INSPECTION FEES FOR OFF-SITE INSPECTIONS. (§ 93.0243)

  INSPECTION FEES FOR OFF-SITE INSPECTIONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No 166,524, Eff. 1/29/91.)	Upon a written request, the
Department may, at its discretion make inspections at locations other than the
site upon which a building will be located provided such location is within 60
miles of the Los Angeles City Hall.	A fee as specified in Section 98.0412(e), in
addition to fees charged elsewhere in this Code,  shall be charged for such
inspections.  The time shall include travel to and from the place of
inspection.
