                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ABATEMENT COST. (§ 98.0711)

  ABATEMENT COST.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,782, Eff. 9/30/99.)	(a)	Abatement costs shall include
the cost to perform the actual work and the City’s cost to administer any
abatement.	(b)	Once the abatement is complete, the Superintendent shall recover
all abatement costs pursuant to the procedure found in Division 89 of Article 1
of Chapter IX of this Code.
