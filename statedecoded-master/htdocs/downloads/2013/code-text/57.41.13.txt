                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SAFETY RELIEF VALVE. (ยง 57.41.13)

ย SAFETY RELIEF VALVE.	A.	Safety relief valve shall be installed in compliance
with the conditions prescribed by the L.A.F.D. Standard under which such
container was designed and constructed.	B.	Unless otherwise permitted by
Subsection A of this section, fusible plugs or rupture discs shall not be
permitted for containers used for the storage of liquefied materials.
