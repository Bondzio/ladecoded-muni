BOARD OF RECREATION AND PARK COMMISSIONERS AND DEPARTMENT OF RECREATION AND
PARKS DEFINED. (§ 63.00.1)

  BOARD OF RECREATION AND PARK COMMISSIONERS AND DEPARTMENT OF RECREATION AND
PARKS DEFINED.
	(Added by Ord. No. 121,319, Eff. 4/7/62.)	Whenever the terms Board of Park
Commissioners, Board of Playground and Recreation Commissioners, Department of
Parks, Playground and Recreation Department, Park Department or Recreation
Department are used in this article, they shall be deemed to mean and refer to
the Board of Recreation and Park Commissioners or the Department of Recreation
and Parks, as the case may be.
