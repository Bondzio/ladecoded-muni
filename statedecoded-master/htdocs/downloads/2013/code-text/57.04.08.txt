                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS – POSTING AND KEEPING. (§ 57.04.08)

  PERMITS – POSTING AND KEEPING.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,043, Eff. 7/13/98.)	Posting and Keeping.  Each valid
Permit issued pursuant to the provisions of this division shall be posted in a
conspicuous place on the premises for which the same is issued.  Persons
engaged in the business of operating or maintaining any oil well shall keep the
Permit available for inspection at the main office or principal place of
business of the permittee.
