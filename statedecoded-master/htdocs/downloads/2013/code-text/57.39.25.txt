                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAZARDOUS MATERIALS STORAGE TANKS INSIDE BUILDINGS. (ยง 57.39.25)

ย HAZARDOUS MATERIALS STORAGE TANKS INSIDE BUILDINGS.	Tanks for the storage of
hazardous materials in liquid form inside buildings shall be provided with
either:
	A.	A normally closed remotely-activated valve.	B.	An automatic-closing
heat-activated valve.	C.	An approved device on each liquid transfer connection
below the liquid level that shall provide a quick cut-off of flow in the event
of fire.
