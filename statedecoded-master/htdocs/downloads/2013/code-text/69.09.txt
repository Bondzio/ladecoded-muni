                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS, SUSPENSIONS, OR REVOCATIONS. (§ 69.09)

  PERMITS, SUSPENSIONS, OR REVOCATIONS.	All permits granted under the authority
of this ordinance shall be valid only for such period as may be determined by
the Director. A violation of the provisions of this ordinance, or of any other
applicable ordinance, by any permittee shall be grounds for suspension or
revocation of such permit or permits.
