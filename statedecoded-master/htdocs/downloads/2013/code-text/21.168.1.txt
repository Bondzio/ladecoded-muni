                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OUT OF STATE SALES. (§ 21.168.1)

  OUT OF STATE SALES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 178,101, Eff. 1/9/07.)	Nothing defined in Section 21.41,
as Multimedia Business, in Section 21.42, as Wholesale Sales or in Section
21.44, as Retail Sales, shall be construed to require the inclusion in the
computation of the amount of the tax due under these sections the gross receipts
of the sales of goods, which are shipped to the purchasers of the goods by the
seller to points outside of the State of California.
