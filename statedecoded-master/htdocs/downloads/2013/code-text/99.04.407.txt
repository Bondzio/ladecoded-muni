                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WATER RESISTANCE AND MOISTURE MANAGEMENT. (§ 99.04.407)

  WATER RESISTANCE AND MOISTURE MANAGEMENT.99.04.407.3.  Flashing Details. 
Provide flashing details on the building plans which comply with accepted
industry standards or manufacturer’s instructions at the following
locations:	1.	Around windows and doors;	2.	Roof valleys;	3.	Chimneys to roof
intersections.99.04.407.4.  Material Protection.  Protect building materials
delivered to the construction site from rain and other sources of moisture.
