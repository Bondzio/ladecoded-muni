                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ASSESSMENT – ADMINISTRATIVE REMEDY. (§ 21.15.10)

  ASSESSMENT – ADMINISTRATIVE REMEDY.	Whenever the Director of Finance
determines that any tax is or may be due to the City under the provisions of
this article, the Director of Finance may make and give notice of an assessment
for such tax. The manner of making and providing notice of such assessment, the
right to a hearing, the manner of providing for and conducting such hearing, the
preparation and service of the decision, and the manner of filing and passing
upon exceptions shall be the same as are provided in Section 21.16 of this
chapter.
