                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELEVATED SIDEWALKS – WHERE PERMITTED. (§ 62.59)

  ELEVATED SIDEWALKS – WHERE PERMITTED.	No permit to erect or maintain an
elevated sidewalk shall be granted except where a railroad switch or spur track
is situated along and adjacent to the curb line of a sidewalk and within five
(5) feet thereof, or upon or along the portion of a cul-de-sac between the
closed end thereof and the first street intersecting such cul-de-sac on the same
side thereof on which it is desired to construct such elevated sidewalk. No such
elevated sidewalk shall be more than three (3) feet in height, and before any
work or construction of any elevated sidewalk is done the complete plans and
specifications thereof shall be submitted to the Board for approval.
