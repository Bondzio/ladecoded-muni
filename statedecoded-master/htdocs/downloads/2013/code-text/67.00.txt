                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMMEMORATIVE SIGNS. (§ 67.00)

  COMMEMORATIVE SIGNS.

	Nothing contained in this article shall prevent the erection of signs or
structures over streets containing words or figures commemorating a historical,
cultural, or artistic event or location in accordance with Section 67.29 hereof.
Such signs or structures shall be erected, constructed and maintained in a safe
and secure manner approved by the Board. All such signs or structures shall be
constructed to withstand from any direction a wind pressure of 30 pounds per
square foot of exposed surface. (Added by Ord. No. 135,775, Eff. 1/13/68.)
