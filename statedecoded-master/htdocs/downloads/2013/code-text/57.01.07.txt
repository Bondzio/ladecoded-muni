                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPLICABILITY. (ยง 57.01.07)

ย APPLICABILITY.	The provisions of this article shall apply to all buildings,
structures, or premises located within this City including the buildings,
structures, or premises owned or directly controlled by the City of Los Angeles,
or any County or other municipal or quasi-municipal corporation or government or
any department, commission, board of office thereof.	The provisions of this
article, in so far as they are substantially the same as existing provisions
relating to the same subject matter, shall be construed as restatements and
continuations thereof and not as new enactments.
