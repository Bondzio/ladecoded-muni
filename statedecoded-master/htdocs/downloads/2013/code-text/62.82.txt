                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PUBLIC UTILITY DEFINED. (§ 62.82)

  PUBLIC UTILITY DEFINED.	For the purposes of Sections 62.84 to 62.93
inclusive, “Public Utility” shall mean and include the Department of Water
and Power of this City, the Department of General Services, Department of
Transportation, and any person owning, operating or maintaining overhead light,
power, trolley, telephone, telegraph, signal or other wires, street railway
tracks or underground structures necessary for public service. (Amended by Ord.
No. 152,426, Eff. 6/29/79, Oper. 7/1/79.)
