                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DOG TAG – MUST BE OFFICIAL. (§ 53.22)

  DOG TAG – MUST BE OFFICIAL.	(a)	No person shall attach to or keep upon any
dog any tag provided for in this article except a tag issued for which dog under
the provisions of this article.	(b)	Dog Tags – Imitation of.  No person shall
attach to or keep upon any dog or make or have in his possession any counterfeit
or imitation of any tag provided for in this article.
