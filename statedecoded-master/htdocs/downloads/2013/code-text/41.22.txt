                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOITERING – RIVER BED. (§ 41.22)

  LOITERING – RIVER BED.	No person shall camp, lodge, make or kindle a fire,
wash any clothes or bedding, bathe, sleep, lay any bed or any blanket, quilt,
straw or branches for the purpose of resting or sleeping thereon, or remain or
loiter in the official bed of the Los Angeles River.
