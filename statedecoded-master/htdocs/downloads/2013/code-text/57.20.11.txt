                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HAZARDOUS SWEEPINGS. (ยง 57.20.11)

ย HAZARDOUS SWEEPINGS.	The use of sawdust or similar combustible material or
compound for the absorption of drippings or spills of hazardous materials from
machinery or processes on any floor is prohibited.	EXCEPTION:	The use of sawdust
as a sweeping compound is permissible when it is applied, immediately swept up,
and properly disposed of in an approved manner.
