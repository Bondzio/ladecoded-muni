                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CHANGE IN OWNER INFORMATION. (§ 103.21)

  CHANGE IN OWNER INFORMATION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,676, Eff. 1/11/04.)	Whenever any change in owner
information occurs, the owner shall file a verified report with the Board within
30 days after the change occurs.  Matters that shall be reported include, but
are not limited to, name changes, change of business address, criminal
convictions, the association of one or more new partners, associates, members,
directors, or officers, or a substantial change of stock ownership, unless the
change requires a new permit pursuant to Section 103.08.
