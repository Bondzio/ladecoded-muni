                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ADDITIONS TO BUILDINGS. (§ 91.7202)

  ADDITIONS TO BUILDINGS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,324, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	An addition may be
made to any building in a fire district if the added portion conforms to this
division, and also if the entire building, including the addition, is within the
height and area limits of CBC Section 503 for building of like type and
occupancy.	EXCEPTION:  An addition to a provisional structure shall not be
permitted.
