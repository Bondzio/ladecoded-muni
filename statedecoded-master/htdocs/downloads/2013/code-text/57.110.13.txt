                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NO SMOKING SIGNS. (§ 57.110.13)

  NO SMOKING SIGNS.	The person in control of every projection room, theatre, or
little theatre shall install and maintain “NO SMOKING” signs in accordance
with Division 23 of this article.	EXCEPTION:	When smoking is a part of the play
or production then being produced, a Special Permit may be issued by the Chief
allowing smoking on the stage or platform.
