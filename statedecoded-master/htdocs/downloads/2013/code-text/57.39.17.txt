57.39.17)

  NEW FACILITY UNDERGROUND PIPING  MONITORING AND SECONDARY CONTAINMENT.
	(Added by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	A.	Suction delivery systems shall
be provided with secondary containment, and be provided with an approved
monitoring system for the piping, or be installed according to Sec.
57.31.45.	B.	Pressure delivery systems shall be provided with secondary
containment, and be provided with an approved monitoring system for the piping,
or be installed according to Sec. 57.31.46.
