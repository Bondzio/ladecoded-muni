                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION. (§ 93.0503)

  MAINTENANCE CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION.	(a)	A Maintenance Certificate of
Registration may be issued to an owner or occupant of an existing building for
the sole purpose of the repair, addition or alteration of existing
facilities.	(b)	The owner or occupant shall register a Maintenance Supervisor
who is the holder of a valid Certificate of Qualification in the proper
classification.	(c)	An annual registration fee shall be paid for premises
covered by a Maintenance Certificate of Registration as required by Section
93.0240. (Amended by Ord. No. 154,234, Eff. 9/8/80.)
