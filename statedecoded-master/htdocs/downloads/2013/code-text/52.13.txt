                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SPECIAL POLICE – RETURN OF BADGES. (§ 52.13)

  SPECIAL POLICE – RETURN OF BADGES.	It shall be the duty of any person after
he has served his employment with any private patrol or bank, or who has ceased
to perform the duties which require the issuance of a special police officer’s
badge, to return the special police officer’s badge to the Chief of Police.
