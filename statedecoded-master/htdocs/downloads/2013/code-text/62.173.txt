                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

TREE STAKES OR GUARDS. (§ 62.173)

  TREE STAKES OR GUARDS.	Tree stakes or guards may be placed around trees,
shrubs or plants by the Board, by its authorized officers or employee, or by
property owners, provided the same are merely placed near a tree, shrub or
plant, for the purpose of protecting or training such trees, shrubs or plants. 
(Added by Ord. No. 153,500, Eff. 4/18/80.)
