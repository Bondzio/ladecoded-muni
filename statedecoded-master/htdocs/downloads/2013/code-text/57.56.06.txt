                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROHIBITED TYPES. (§ 57.56.06)

  PROHIBITED TYPES.	No person shall manufacture, distribute, sell, offer for
sale, or possess any exploding match, “trick” match, blazer or wind type
match, or any type of match which will ignite spontaneously when subjected for
eight consecutive hours to a temperature of 200°F. in a standard laboratory
test.
