                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTRIC WIRES ON STREETS – PERMIT REQUIRED. (§ 62.62)

  ELECTRIC WIRES ON STREETS – PERMIT REQUIRED.	No person shall string, place
or attach any electric wires or electric lights of any nature whatsoever over,
under, or in any manner directly or indirectly attached to or in front of any
building or structure, or over any street, without first obtaining a written
permit from the Board so to do.
