                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

QUANTITY OF CLASS I, II AND IIIA LIQUIDS. (§ 57.80.74)

  QUANTITY OF CLASS I, II AND IIIA LIQUIDS.	The quantity of Class I, II and
IIIA liquids in a spray area shall not exceed the amount permitted by Section
57.30.30 of this article.  Bulk storage of portable containers of Class I, II
and IIIA liquids at or near a spray area, not otherwise permitted by this
Division, is prohibited.
