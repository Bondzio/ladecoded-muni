                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.121.01)

  SCOPE.	This division regulates the installation, maintenance and use of
central station signaling systems as they relate to fire protective signaling
systems or devices.  Unless otherwise specifically provided in this article,
the provisions of the L.A.F.D. Standard No. 67 shall apply.  When provisions of
this division are in conflict with L.A.F.D. Standard No. 67, the requirements of
this division apply.
