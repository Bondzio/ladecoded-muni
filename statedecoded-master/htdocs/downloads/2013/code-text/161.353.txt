                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FREQUENCY OF INSPECTION. (§ 161.353)

  FREQUENCY OF INSPECTION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,108, Eff. 7/15/98.)	The frequency with which a building
is inspected over a three year period shall be determined by the General Manager
or an authorized representative based on its condition, however, each building
shall be inspected at least once every three years.
