                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FAN LOCATION. (§ 57.53.15)

  FAN LOCATION.	Systems collecting highly flammable or explosive dust shall,
wherever practicable, be so arranged that the fan is on the clean air side of
the collector.  Systems shall be operated with approved equipment for removing
stock from the air stream before it reaches the fan.  Readily ignitable
materials shall not pass through the fan unless the fan is constructed and
installed in a manner approved therefor.
