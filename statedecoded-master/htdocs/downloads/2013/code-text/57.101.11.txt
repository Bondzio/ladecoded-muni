                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMBUSTIBLE STORAGE. (§ 57.101.11)

  COMBUSTIBLE STORAGE.	No person shall store combustible materials, flammable
liquids, flammable or liquefied compressed gas cylinders (other than fire
protection equipment) or other hazardous materials in an aircraft factory or
hangar, except in Hazardous Materials storage rooms or as otherwise permitted by
this article.
