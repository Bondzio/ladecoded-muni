POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE DEPARTMENT. (§ 95.108)

  POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE DEPARTMENT.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,327, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)95.108.1. 
GENERAL.  The powers of the Department and the Board are enumerated in Section
98.0403.1 of the Los Angeles Municipal Code.95.108.2.  INTERPRETATION.	1.	If
two or more pertinent limitations are not identical, those limitations shall
prevail that provide the greater safety to persons, property or public
welfare.	2.	The Superintendent of Building or a duly authorized representative
is empowered to make all interpretations concerning the provisions of this
Code.95.108.3.  RIGHT OF ENTRY.  Section 108.3 of Chapter 1, Division II of
the C.M.C. is adopted by reference.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff.
5/2/11.)95.108.4.  STOP ORDERS.  Section 108.4 of Chapter 1, Division II of
the C.M.C. is adopted by reference.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff.
5/2/11.)95.108.5.  AUTHORITY TO DISCONNECT UTILITIES IN EMERGENCIES.  Section
108.5 of Chapter 1, Division II of the C.M.C. is adopted by reference. 
(Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff. 5/2/11.)95.108.6.  AUTHORITY TO CONDEMN
EQUIPMENT.  Section 108.6 of Chapter 1, Division II of the C.M.C. is adopted by
reference.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff. 5/2/11.)95.108.7.  CONNECTION
AFTER ORDER TO DISCONNECT.  Section 108.7 of Chapter 1, Division II of the
C.M.C. is adopted by reference.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff.
5/2/11.)95.108.8.  LIABILITY.  Section 108.8 of Chapter 1, Division II of the
C.M.C. is adopted by reference.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,685, Eff.
5/2/11.)95.108.9.  COOPERATION OF OTHER OFFICIALS AND OFFICERS.  Section 108.9
of Chapter 1, Division II of the C.M.C. is adopted by reference.  (Amended by
Ord. No. 181,685, Eff. 5/2/11.)
