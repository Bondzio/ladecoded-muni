                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

IMPOUNDING. DUTY OF POLICE. (§ 53.05)

  IMPOUNDING. DUTY OF POLICE.	It shall be the duty of the General Manager or
his authorized representatives to take up and impound in the City pound, and it
shall be the duty of members of the Police Department on duty during the hours
when the Municipal Pound is open, to take up and immediately deliver to the
Department those animals which are authorized and directed to be taken up and
impounded by this article or which are found or kept contrary to the provisions
of this article.
