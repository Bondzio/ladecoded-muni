                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REPLACING FALLEN MATERIAL. (§ 66.24)

  REPLACING FALLEN MATERIAL.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 127,508, Eff. 6/29/64.)	No person removing or conveying
any refuse, garbage, food plant waste, market waste or rubbish shall fail,
refuse or neglect to replace immediately in any container any refuse, garbage,
food plant waste, market waste or rubbish that shall have fallen therefrom, in
or upon any street or in or upon any premises.	No person other than the owner or
operator of the premises on which market waste containers are located, or
persons authorized by such person, including such person’s authorized hauler
of market waste, shall tamper with or remove any material from a market waste
container.
