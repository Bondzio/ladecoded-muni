                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WATER SHIELDS. (§ 57.72.22)

  WATER SHIELDS.	Tanks containing corrosive materials which react with water
shall be of closed type, or shall be equipped with a lid which shall be kept
closed during intervals of non-use and at the end of each working shift.  A
hood or shield shall be provided over open tanks containing corrosive materials
which react with water, if located beneath automatic sprinklers.
