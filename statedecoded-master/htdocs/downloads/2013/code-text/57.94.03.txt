                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REMOTE CONTROL SWITCH. (§ 57.94.03)

  REMOTE CONTROL SWITCH.	A.	An electrical power control switch shall be located
at every transfer apparatus where electric power is used in connection with any
such transfer apparatus pump for loading or unloading.  In addition thereto, a
control switch shall be located at a remote point.	B.	Both power control
switches shall be conspicuously labeled with a legible sign indicating the
location and function thereof.
