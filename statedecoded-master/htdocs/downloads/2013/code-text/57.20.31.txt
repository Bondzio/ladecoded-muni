ย FORGES.
	(Sec. 57.20.32 renumbered as Sec. 57.20.31 by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff.
11/16/91.)	No person shall maintain or use any portable forge on a combustible
surface unless the same is equipped with a protective metal shield suspended
thereunder and such shield is at least eight inches above said surface.
