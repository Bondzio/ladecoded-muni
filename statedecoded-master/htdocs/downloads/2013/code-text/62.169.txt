                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMIT REQUIRED TO PLANT IN STREETS. (§ 62.169)

  PERMIT REQUIRED TO PLANT IN STREETS.	No person shall plant, remove, destroy,
cut, prune or deface or in any manner injure any tree or shrub in any street in
the City, without first obtaining a permit to do so from the Board.  (Added by
Ord. No. 153,500, Eff. 4/18/80.)
