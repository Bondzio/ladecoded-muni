                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS. (§ 57.94.25)

  PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.	At least two portable fire extinguishers having
not less than a 20B:C classification shall be provided at every liquefied
flammable gas Bulk Distributing Station.  At least two portable fire
extinguishers having not less than a 10B:C classification shall be provided at
every Auto Fueling Station dispensing liquefied flammable gas and at every
Cylinder Filling Plant.  One such portable fire extinguisher shall be located
within 25 feet of every dispenser.
