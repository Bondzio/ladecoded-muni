                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SIGNS. (§ 57.09.05)

  SIGNS.	A.	Fire lanes shall be posted with signs not less than 17 inches by 22
inches in size, with lettering not less than one inch in height, stating “NO
PARKING — DESIGNATED FIRE LANE. VIOLATORS WILL BE CITED VEHICLE CODE SECTION
22500.1. VEHICLES PARKED IN VIOLATION WILL BE TOWED AWAY AT OWNER’S
EXPENSE.” shall also contain a telephone number of the Los Angeles Police
Department which may be called by the person owning the vehicle to find out
where it has been towed. Signs shall be in plain view at all entrances to
required fire lanes and the spacing of signs shall be as required by the Chief.
The bottom of such signs shall be six feet above the adjacent ground surface. 
(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	B.	The installation of approved
fire lane signs on private roadways is the responsibility of the owner of the
property.
