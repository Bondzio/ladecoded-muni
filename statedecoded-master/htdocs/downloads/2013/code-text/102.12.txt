                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AMENDMENT OF ACCUSATION AFTER SUBMISSION. (§ 102.12)

  AMENDMENT OF ACCUSATION AFTER SUBMISSION.	The Board or hearing examiner may
order amendment of the accusation after submission of the case for decision.
Each party shall be given notice of the intended amendment and opportunity to
show that he will be prejudiced thereby unless the case is reopened to permit
the introduction of additional evidence in his behalf.  If such prejudice is
shown, the Board or hearing examiner shall reopen the case to permit the
introduction of additional evidence.
