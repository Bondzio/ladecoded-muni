                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OVERLOADS – ASSIGNMENT OF INSPECTORS. (§ 62.147)

  OVERLOADS – ASSIGNMENT OF INSPECTORS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 82,963, Eff. 7/7/40.)	(a)	Whenever any overload is required
by law to be accompanied by an inspector, and when a valid permit has been
issued therefor, and when all fee and deposits have been paid to the Board, the
Board shall assign an inspector to accompany the overload.	(b)	It shall be the
duty of the mover of such overload to notify the Board at least twenty-four (24)
hours in advance of the time the move is to be started.
