ย DISCARDING FLAMING OR GLOWING OBJECTS.
	(Title Amended by Ord. No. 170,954, Eff. 4/16/96.)	No person shall throw or
place, or cause to be thrown or placed, any lighted match, cigar, cigarette,
ashes, or other flaming or glowing substances, or thing in any place where it
may directly or indirectly start a fire.
