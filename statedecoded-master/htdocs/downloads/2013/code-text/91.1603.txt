                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CONSTRUCTION DOCUMENTS. (§ 91.1603)

  CONSTRUCTION DOCUMENTS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 181,758, Eff. 8/8/11.)	Section 1603 of the CBC is adopted by
reference, except CBC Section 1603.1.9 is not adopted and in lieu Section
91.1603.1.9 is added.  91.1603.1.9 Systems and Components Requiring Special
Inspections for Seismic resistance. Construction documents or specifications
shall be prepared for those systems and components requiring special inspection
for seismic resistance as specified in Section 1707.1 by the registered design
professional responsible for their design and shall be submitted for approval in
accordance with Section 91.106.3.3.  Reference to seismic standards in lieu of
detailed drawings is acceptable.
