                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NON-RESPONSIBILITY OF CITY. (§ 92.0139)

  NON-RESPONSIBILITY OF CITY.	Neither the City of Los Angeles, nor any
department, board, commission, officer or employee thereof shall be held liable
or responsible for any damage or injury caused by or resulting from the issuance
of any permit, or any inspection or approval made under the provisions of this
Code.
