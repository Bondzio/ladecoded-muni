                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

D. (§ 94.206.0)

  	Section 206 of the C.P.C. is hereby adopted by reference with the following
additions and amendments.	Department.  The Department of Building and
Safety.	Dual Flush Toilet.  A toilet that has two flush modes, one at 1.1
gallons per flush or less and one at 1.6 gallons per flush or less with an
effective 1.28 gallons per flush.   (Added by Ord. No. 181,757, Eff. 7/6/11.)
