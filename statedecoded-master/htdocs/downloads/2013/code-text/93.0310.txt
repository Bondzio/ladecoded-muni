                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

POWER TO STOP WORK. (§ 93.0310)

  POWER TO STOP WORK.	(a)	The Department is hereby authorized to stop the
installation of electric wiring or the concealment thereof, whenever an
installation is being done in violation of this Code, or where a hazard to life
and property is created until the hazard or violation has been eliminated. 
(Amended by Ord. No. 161,316, Eff. 7/7/86.)	(b)	The notice to stop work shall be
in writing, served as provided in the Los Angeles Municipal Code, upon the
person violating or responsible for the violation of this Code.	(c)	No person
shall fail, neglect or refuse to comply with a stop work notice.
