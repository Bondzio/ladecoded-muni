                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CERTIFICATES OF FITNESS – CHANGE OF ADDRESS. (§ 57.06.11)

  CERTIFICATES OF FITNESS – CHANGE OF ADDRESS.	Each person holding a
Certificate of Fitness shall notify the Department in writing of any change of
his business, residential, or notification address within 10 days after such
change. Failure on the part of said person to give such notification shall
constitute grounds for revocation of said Certificate.
