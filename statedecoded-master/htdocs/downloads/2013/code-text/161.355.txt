                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REINSPECTION. (§ 161.355)

  REINSPECTION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,108, Eff. 7/15/98.)	The General Manager or an authorized
representative shall reinspect the building after the time specified in the
notice and order has passed to determine whether the violation has been
corrected.  If it has not been corrected, then the General Manager may schedule
a General Manager’s hearing or take any other enforcement action the General
manager deems appropriate.
