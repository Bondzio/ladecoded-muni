                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VALVES AT TANK. (§ 57.39.22)

  VALVES AT TANK.	Every gravity flow pipe leading out of any tank shall be
equipped with a manually operated shutoff valve at or near such tank, and there
shall be no branch or connection to any such gravity flow pipe between such
shutoff valve and such tank except a bypass line equipped with a check valve or
a relief  valve.
