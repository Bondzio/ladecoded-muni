                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

QUALITY CONTROL. (§ 91.9408)

  QUALITY CONTROL.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,258, Eff. 8/30/96.)91.9408.1.  General.  All hillside
building construction shall comply with the requirements specified in this
section.91.9408.2.  Department Called Inspections.  (Amended by Ord. No.
172,592, Eff. 6/28/99, Oper. 7/1/99.)  All anchors installed in accordance with
Sections 91.9406.2, 91.9406.3 and 91.9406.4 shall be inspected by the Department
prior to installation of any construction which might restrict access to the
anchors or prevent a visual inspection from the floor level above the
anchors.91.9408.3.  Structural Observation by the Engineer or Architect of
Record.  (Amended by Ord. No. 181,758, Eff. 8/8/11.)  The owner shall employ
the engineer or architect of record, or other engineer or architect designated
by the engineer or architect of record, to perform structural observations as
required by CBC Section 1710.91.9408.4.  Anchor Installation.  No installed
anchor shall be covered prior to all required Department framing inspections and
structural observation by the architect or engineer.
