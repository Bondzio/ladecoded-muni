                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VEHICLES – FIRE SIGNS ON. (§ 63.97)

  VEHICLES – FIRE SIGNS ON.	No person shall place upon any vehicle, other
than a vehicle owned by or under the control or management of this City, any
sign or plate bearing the words “Department of Fire, City of Los Angeles” or
“Fire Department, City of Los Angeles” or any similar sign, or drive or
operate any vehicle bearing such sign.
