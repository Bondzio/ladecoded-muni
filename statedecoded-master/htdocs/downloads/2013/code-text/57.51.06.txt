                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCRAP MATERIALS. (§ 57.51.06)

  SCRAP MATERIALS.	All waste hazardous plastic scraps, chips, turnings,
edgings, trimmings, and dust shall be gathered up at frequent intervals and
shall be stored in metal containers with self-closing metal covers, which shall
be removed from work areas at the close of each working day.  Until removed
from the premises for final disposal, scrap materials which have been removed
from work areas shall be kept in an approved film cabinet or film vault.
