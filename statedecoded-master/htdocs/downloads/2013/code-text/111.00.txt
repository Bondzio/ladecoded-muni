                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DECLARATION OF POLICY. (§ 111.00)

  DECLARATION OF POLICY.

	It is hereby declared to be the policy of the City to prohibit unnecessary,
excessive and annoying noises from all sources subject to its police power.  At
certain levels noises are detrimental to the health and welfare of the citizenry
and in the public interests shall be systematically proscribed.
