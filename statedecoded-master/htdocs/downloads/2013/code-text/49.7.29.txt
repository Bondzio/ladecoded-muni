                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAXIMUM MATCHING FUNDS. (§ 49.7.29)

  MAXIMUM MATCHING FUNDS.	A.	The following maximum amounts may be paid to
qualified participating candidates in a primary election:	1.	$100,000 for City
Council candidates;	2.	$267,000 for Controller candidates;	3.	$300,000 for City
Attorney candidates; and	4.	$667,000 for Mayoral candidates.	B.  The following
maximum amounts may be paid to qualified participating candidates in a general
election:	1.	$125,000 for City Council candidates;	2.	$300,000 for Controller
candidates;	3.	$350,000 for City Attorney candidates; and	4.	$800,000 for
Mayoral candidates.
