                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PREVIOUS ASSESSMENTS, PAYMENTS, AND LITIGATION. (§ 21.12.4)

  PREVIOUS ASSESSMENTS, PAYMENTS, AND LITIGATION.	The tax penalty shall apply
and no refund or credit shall be granted of any penalty if, prior to the time
the taxpayer makes a request for tax penalty amnesty pursuant to Section 21.12.5
of this article, the penalty has been paid by the taxpayer.
