                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DRAWING AND SPINNING. (ยง 57.52.10)

ย DRAWING AND SPINNING.	The devices for heating of magnesium for drawing or
spinning shall provide for reliable means to prevent overheating. Trimmings
shall be collected at frequent intervals and placed in clean, dry, covered
noncombustible containers. If such devices produce fine particles, the
provisions of Section 57.52.08 shall apply.
