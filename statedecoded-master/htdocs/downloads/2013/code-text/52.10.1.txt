                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FUNERAL ESCORT OFFICERS. (§ 52.10.1)

  FUNERAL ESCORT OFFICERS.	Any person acting as a funeral escort officer within
the City of Los Angeles shall possess a valid license issued by the County of
Los Angeles under the provisions of Section 1253 of Los Angeles County Ordinance
No. 5860. (Amended by Ord. No. 154,186, Eff. 8/22/80.)
