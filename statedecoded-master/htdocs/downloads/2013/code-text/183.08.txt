                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NO WAIVER OF RIGHTS. (§ 183.08)

  NO WAIVER OF RIGHTS.	Except for bona fide collective bargaining agreements,
any waiver by a Hotel Worker of any or all of the provisions of this article
shall be deemed contrary to public policy and shall be void and unenforceable. 
Any attempt by an Incumbent or Successor Hotel Employer to have a Hotel Worker
waive rights given by this article shall constitute a violation of this article.
