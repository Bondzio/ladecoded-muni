                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EXEMPTION-EXCHANGES WITH THE CITY. (§ 21.9.12)

  EXEMPTION-EXCHANGES WITH THE CITY.
	(Added by Ord. No. 170,326, Eff. 3/10/95.)	Any deed, instrument or writing by
which a person acquires real property in an exchange with the City shall be
exempt from the tax imposed pursuant to this article where the real property
received by the City is of equal or greater value than the real property
received by the other person.
