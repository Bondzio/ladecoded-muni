                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FILLING TERMINALS. (§ 57.42.25)

  FILLING TERMINALS.	The filling pipe inlet connections for tanks filled on
consumer’s premises shall not be located less than 15 feet from any opening
into or under a building where such opening is below the level of the filling
connections; and in no case shall the filling pipe inlet connection be located
less than 10 feet from any building, street, or alley.VAPORIZERS FOR LIQUID
