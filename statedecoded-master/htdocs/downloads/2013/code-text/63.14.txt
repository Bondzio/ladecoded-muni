                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BOARD – POWER TO GRANT PERMIT. (§ 63.14)

  BOARD – POWER TO GRANT PERMIT.	The Board of Playground and Recreation
Commissioners, in the exercise of sound and reasonable discretion based upon
compliance with the provisions of this article and based upon the effect the
proposed or existing structure may have or has had upon any of the rights or
interests of the public, and of its effect on the public beach land, may grant
or deny any and all applications to construct, maintain, repair or operate any
of the structures mentioned in Section 63.08 for which an application has been
made; provided, however, that the Board of Playground and Recreation
Commissioners must grant or deny any application within sixty days after the
date of the filing of the same.
