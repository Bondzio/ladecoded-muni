                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ANIMALS IN LOS ANGELES RIVER BED. (§ 53.08)

  ANIMALS IN LOS ANGELES RIVER BED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 150,337, Eff. 1/1/78.)	No person shall stake out, herd or
graze any animal in or upon the bed of the Los Angeles River unless such animal
is under the immediate control of some person over the age of fifteen (15) years
who is at all times within fifty (50) feet of the animal. Any horse, mule, ass
or ox which is harnessed or saddled, at the time, in the actual custody and
control of some person is exempted from the operation of this section.
