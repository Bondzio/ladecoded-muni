                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROHIBITION AGAINST DISCRIMINATION. (§ 45.95.02)

  PROHIBITION AGAINST DISCRIMINATION.	A.	It shall be unlawful for a club which
is not distinctly private to deny to any person entry to facilities at,
membership in, or the full enjoyment of said club or organization on the basis
of sex, race, color, religion, ancestry, national origin, sexual orientation, or
disability.	B.	The provisions of this article shall not apply to an institution
organized and operated exclusively for religious purposes as defined in 26
U.S.C. Section 501(c)(3).
