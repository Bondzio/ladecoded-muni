                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EFFECTIVE DATE. (§ 93.0112)

  EFFECTIVE DATE.	Electric wiring installed under a permit issued before the
effective date of this Code may be installed in accordance with the laws in
effect when the permit was issued; and all work begun before the expiration of
the permit may be done in accordance with such laws.  (Added by Ord. No.
154,234, Eff. 9/8/80.)
