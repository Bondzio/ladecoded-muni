                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURPOSE. (§ 91.7101)

  PURPOSE.	This division sets forth the minimum requirements of the City of Los
Angeles for control of methane intrusion emanating from geologic formations. 
The requirements do not regulate flammable vapor that may originate in and
propagate from other sources, which include, but are not limited to, ruptured
hazardous material transmission lines, underground atmospheric tanks, or similar
installations.
