                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCHOOL GROUNDS – TRESPASS ON. (§ 63.94)

  SCHOOL GROUNDS – TRESPASS ON.	No person shall enter the grounds or any
property or buildings of a public school or remain on such grounds or property
or building in violation of posted rules regarding the use of school property.
Any such conduct shall constitute a trespass upon school property.  (Amended by
Ord. No. 158,173, Eff. 9/1/83.)
