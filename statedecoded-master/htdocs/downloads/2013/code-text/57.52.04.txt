                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HEAVY CASTINGS STORAGE. (§ 57.52.04)

  HEAVY CASTINGS STORAGE.	Large magnesium castings (25 pounds or more) having
walls of heavy cross section at least 1/4 inch, shall be stored outside, or in
one-story buildings without basements or floor depressions of noncombustible
construction.  Individual piles shall be limited to 1,250 cubic feet.
