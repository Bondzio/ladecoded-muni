                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPLICATION OF REQUIREMENTS. (§ 49.5.14)

  APPLICATION OF REQUIREMENTS.	The requirements imposed by this article on
officers and employees shall not apply to any officer or employee who terminated
his or her City service prior to the effective date of this article; provided,
however, that a person who returns to City service on or after the effective
date of this article shall be subject to the requirements of this article.
