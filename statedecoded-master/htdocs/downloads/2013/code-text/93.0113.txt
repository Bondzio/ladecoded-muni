                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REFERRED DOCUMENTS – COPIES. (§ 93.0113)

  REFERRED DOCUMENTS – COPIES.	Three copies of every law, ordinance,
regulation, and specification, or portion thereof which is specifically referred
to, or which is mentioned by title and serial designation, or by date of
publication, approval or adoption in this Code, shall be kept on file and
available for public inspection in the office of the Superintendent of
Building.  (Added by Ord. No. 154,234, Eff. 9/8/80.)
