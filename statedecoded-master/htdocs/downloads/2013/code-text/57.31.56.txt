                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

METHANOL COMPATIBILITY. (§ 57.31.56)

  METHANOL COMPATIBILITY.
	(Added by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	At least one tank and all
associated pipes and equipment shall be compatible with methanol in accordance
with AQMD rule 1170 when underground motor vehicle fuel storage tanks are
installed or replaced.
