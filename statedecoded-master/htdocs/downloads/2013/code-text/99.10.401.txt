                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 99.10.401)

  GENERAL.99.10.401.1.  Scope.  The provisions of this division shall outline
means of achieving material conservation and resource efficiency through
protection of buildings from exterior moisture, construction waste diversion,
employment of techniques to reduce pollution through recycling of materials, and
building commissioning or testing, adjusting and balancing.
