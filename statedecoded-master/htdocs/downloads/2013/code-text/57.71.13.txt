                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

STATIC ELECTRICITY. (ยง 57.71.13)

ย STATIC ELECTRICITY.	A.	All filling or withdrawal lines on tanks or vessels
containing liquid unstable materials shall enter through or extend to the bottom
of the tank or vessel to minimize the possible generation of static
electricity.	B.	All tank, vessels, and pipe lines shall be grounded or bonded in
an approved manner to prevent the accumulation of static electricity.
