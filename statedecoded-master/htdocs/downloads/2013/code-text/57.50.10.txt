                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCRAP CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM. (§ 57.50.10)

  SCRAP CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM.	Scrap cellulose nitrate film shall be kept
submerged under water in metal containers with self-closing covers, and shall be
kept separate from waste paper, safety film and all other rubbish.  It shall be
collected from the work rooms at least once daily and stored in approved
containers in film vaults until safely disposed of.
