                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MAINTENANCE OF VEHICLES. (§ 57.70.46)

  MAINTENANCE OF VEHICLES.	Vehicles used for the transportation of explosive
materials shall be properly maintained, including brakes, headlights, warning
devices, and other parts thereof.  The chassis, motor, body, and all other
parts of the vehicle shall be clean and free from surplus oil and grease.
Gasoline tank and piping shall be secure and without leaks. Electric wiring
shall be completely insulated and properly secured.
