                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.83.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall regulate the safe operation,
ventilation, maintenance, and fire protection necessary for electrostatic
spraying and powder-coating. Approval for installation of electrostatic spraying
and powder-coating apparatus and devices shall be in accordance with Division 5
of this article (Spray Booths) and the requirements of Chapter 9 of the
L.A.M.C.  (Electrical Code).
