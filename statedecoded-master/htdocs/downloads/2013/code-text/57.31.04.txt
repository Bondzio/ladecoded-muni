                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROTECTION FOR TANKS. (ยง 57.31.04)

ย PROTECTION FOR TANKS.	Every owner or operator having charge of any
atmospheric tank which has an opening exceeding six inches in any lateral
dimension or which has a depth in excess of three feet, shall secure the tank
from unauthorized entry. Provisions shall be made to secure unauthorized
discharge from any atmospheric tank by locking valves in a closed position or by
providing and maintaining equivalent protection as approved by the Chief.
