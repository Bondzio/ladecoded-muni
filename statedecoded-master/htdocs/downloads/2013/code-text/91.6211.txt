                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WALL SIGNS. (§ 91.6211)

  WALL SIGNS.91.6211.1. Materials.  Wall signs that have an area exceeding 40
square feet (3.72 m2) shall be constructed of metal or other approved
noncombustible material, except for nailing rails and as provided for in
Sections 91.6206.1.1 and 91.6207.1 of this Code.91.6211.2. Section H111.2 of
Appendix H of the CBC is adopted by reference.91.6211.3. Section H111.3 of
Appendix H of the CBC is adopted by reference.
