                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

BREEDING ANIMALS – ENCLOSURES. (§ 53.47)

  BREEDING ANIMALS – ENCLOSURES.	No person shall let to a mare any stallion
or jack, or let to a cow any bull, or let to a sow any boar, or let to a bitch
any dog, or let to a nanny goat any goat, except within an enclosure so arranged
as to obstruct such animals entirely from the view of all persons outside of
such enclosure.
