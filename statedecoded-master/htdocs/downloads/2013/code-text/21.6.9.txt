                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SEVERABILITY. (§ 21.6.9)

  SEVERABILITY.	If any section, subsection, sentence clause, phrase or portion
of this ordinance, including but not limited to any exemption, is for any reason
held to be invalid or unconstitutional by the decision of any court of competent
jurisdiction, such decision shall not affect the validity of the remaining
portions of this ordinance. The Council of the City of Los Angeles hereby
declares that it would have adopted this ordinance and each section, subsection,
sentence, clause, phrase or portion thereof, irrespective of the fact that any
one or more sections, subsections, sentences, clauses, phrases or portions be
declared invalid or unconstitutional.
