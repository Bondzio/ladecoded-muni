                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 91.8802)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall apply to all buildings
constructed or under construction prior to October 6, 1933, or for which a
building permit was issued prior to October 6, 1933, which on the effective date
of this ordinance have unreinforced masonry bearing walls as defined
herein.	EXCEPTION:  This division shall not apply to detached one- or
two-family dwellings and detached apartment houses containing less than 5
dwelling units and used solely for residential purposes.
