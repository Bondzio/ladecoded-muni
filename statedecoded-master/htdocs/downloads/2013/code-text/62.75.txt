                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

OIL CABLES – PERMIT APPLICATIONS. (§ 62.75)

  OIL CABLES – PERMIT APPLICATIONS.	Any person desiring to obtain the permit
required in Section 62.74 shall sign and file a written application therefor
with the Board, which application shall contain:	(a)	The name and address of the
applicant;	(b)	The exact location where it is proposed to erect, maintain or
operate the rope, wire or cable;	(c)	A statement that the applicant agrees to
conform in all respects to and obey all provisions of this Code and ordinances
of this City in force at the time of filing the application, or that may
thereafter be adopted relative to the erection, maintenance or operation of any
ropes, wires or cables over or across any street.
