DAMPPROOFING AND WATERPROOFING. (§ 91.1805)

  DAMPPROOFING AND WATERPROOFING.
	(Title and Section Amended by Ord. 181,758, Eff. 8/8/11.)	Section 1805 of the
CBC is adopted by reference, except Section 1805.4.3 of the CBC is not adopted
and in lieu Section 91.1805.4.3 is added.  91.1805.4.3. Drainage Discharge. 
The floor base and foundation perimeter drain shall discharge by gravity or
mechanical means into an approved drainage system that complies with the
Plumbing Code.
