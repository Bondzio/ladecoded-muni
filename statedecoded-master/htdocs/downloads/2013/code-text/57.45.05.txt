                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SHUT-OFF VALVES FOR REMOTE CYLINDER STORAGE. (§ 57.45.05)

  SHUT-OFF VALVES FOR REMOTE CYLINDER STORAGE.	When cylinder supply storage is
not readily accessible or the entry to the cylinder storage room is on the
exterior of the treatment facility, a manual shutoff valve accessible inside of
the treatment facility shall be installed downstream from main supply lines and
prior to first use point.  This valve is for use in an emergency and shall be
labeled, “CAUTION, OXYGEN VALVE – NITROUS OXIDE VALVE – DO NOT CLOSE
EXCEPT IN EMERGENCY”.
