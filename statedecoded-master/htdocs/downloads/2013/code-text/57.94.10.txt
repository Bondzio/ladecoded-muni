                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FILLING PRESSURE VESSELS. (§ 57.94.10)

  FILLING PRESSURE VESSELS.	No person shall fill any pressure vessel with
liquefied flammable gas unless such pressure vessel is approved for such use,
nor shall any such pressure vessel be filled with liquefied flammable gas having
a vapor pressure in excess of the rated design pressure of such pressure vessel;
nor shall any person fill any such pressure vessel in excess of its rated
capacity.
