                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE EXTINGUISHERS. (ยง 57.86.06)

ย FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.	There shall be at least one approved portable fire
extinguisher with a rating of 20B:C located not less than 10 feet nor more than
30 feet from every roofing kettle during the period that the roofing kettle is
in use, and there shall be one additional 20B:C portable fire extinguisher
located on the roof being covered.
