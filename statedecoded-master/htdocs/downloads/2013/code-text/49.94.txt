                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROMULGATION OF RULES AND REGULATIONS. (§ 49.94)

  PROMULGATION OF RULES AND REGULATIONS.	The Rent Adjustment Commission of the
City of Los Angeles and the Los Angeles Housing Department shall have the
authority to adopt policies, rules and regulations to effectuate the purposes of
this Article, and to implement, administer, and enforce the provisions of this
Article.
