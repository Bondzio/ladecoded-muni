                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RULES AND REGULATIONS. (§ 53.58)

  RULES AND REGULATIONS.	The General Manager may adopt and enforce reasonable
rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of this article. The General
Manager may amend such rules and regulations from time to time. Such rules and
regulations and amendments thereto shall take effect upon publication in a
newspaper of general circulation in this City. Violation of such rules and
regulations shall be a misdemeanor. (Amended by Ord. No. 133,983, Eff. 4/10/67.)
