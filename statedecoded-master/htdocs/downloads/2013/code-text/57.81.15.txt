                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROTECTION OF FLOORS. (§ 57.81.15)

  PROTECTION OF FLOORS.	A.	Dip tanks exceeding 20 gallons capacity shall not be
used or maintained on any combustible floor.	B.	Dip tanks of 20 gallons capacity
or less shall not be used or maintained on combustible flooring unless the floor
under such tanks, and for a distance of five feet on all sides of dip tanks,
drain boards, and objects which have been dipped is protected with a covering of
noncombustible material in such a manner as to prevent the floor from becoming
saturated or covered with flammable or combustible liquids used in the dipping
process.
