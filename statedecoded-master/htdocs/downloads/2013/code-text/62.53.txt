                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMIT TO BE EXHIBITED. (§ 62.53)

  PERMIT TO BE EXHIBITED.	No person shall fail to keep the permit granted for
maintaining any protection fence, protection canopy, equipment, building
material or debris at all times at the place where such work is being done: nor
fail, upon demand therefor, to exhibit such permit to any member or inspector of
the Board, or any public officer.  (Amended by Ord. No. 128,226, Eff.
10/12/64.)
