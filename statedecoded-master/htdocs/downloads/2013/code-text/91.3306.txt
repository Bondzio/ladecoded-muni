                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROTECTION OF PEDESTRIANS. (§ 91.3306)

  PROTECTION OF PEDESTRIANS.	Section 3306 of the CBC is adopted by reference,
except Section 3306.1 of the CBC is not adopted and in lieu, Section 91.3306.1
is added.91.3306.1.  Protection Required.  Pedestrians shall be protected
during construction, remodeling and demolition activities as required by this
division and CBC Table 3306.1.  Signs shall be provided to direct pedestrian
traffic.	Nothing contained in this section shall be construed to grant
permission to use, construct or place a canopy over any street or public place
without first obtaining the necessary permits from the Department of Public
Works.
