                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPROVALS. (ยง 92.0133)

ย APPROVALS.	All elevator equipment, including controls, doors, interlocks,
machinery, governor, safety devices, switches and any other devices designed for
the operation of the equipment governed by this Code shall be approved by the
Superintendent of Building before they may be in stalled or used. The
Superintendent of Building shall have the authority to establish rules and
regulations in connection therewith.
