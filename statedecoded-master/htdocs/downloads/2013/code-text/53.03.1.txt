                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MINIMUM AGE – DUTIES OF EMPLOYEES. (§ 53.03.1)

  MINIMUM AGE – DUTIES OF EMPLOYEES.	In addition to his duties as otherwise
provided, each employee of the Department is hereby given the power and duty of
collecting dog license fees.	Persons under the age of twenty-one (21) years may
be appointed to a position within said Department for the purpose of performing
clerical, stenographic or kennel duties. All other positions shall be filled by
persons over the age of twenty-one (21) years.
