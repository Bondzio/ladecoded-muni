                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SOURCES OF IGNITION. (§ 57.80.12)

  SOURCES OF IGNITION.	Smoking, open flames or other sources of ignition shall
not be located within 25 feet of any open face spray booth unless separated by a
partition or vapor barrier wall of at least the height of the spray
booth.	EXCEPTION.	Hanging space heaters located a minimum of 12 feet above the
floor, a minimum of 3 feet above the booth, and a minimum of 10 feet from the
face of the booth.EXHAUST SYSTEMS
