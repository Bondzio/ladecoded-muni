                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NOTICES AND ORDERS. (§ 161.354)

  NOTICES AND ORDERS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,108, Eff. 7/15/98.)	If, upon inspection, the General
Manager or an authorized representative discovers one or more violations of this
Code, the General Manager shall cause to be issued a notice and order pursuant
to Health and Safety Code Sections 17980 and 17980.6.  To the extent the
General Manager deems feasible and consistent with state law, the General
Manager or an authorized representative may utilize the procedures outlined in
Chapter 11 of the Uniform Housing Code.
