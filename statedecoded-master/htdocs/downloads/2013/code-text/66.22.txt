                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS – REVOCATION OF. (§ 66.22)

  PERMITS – REVOCATION OF.	The Board may, in addition to the penalties
provided for in this Code for the violation of any provisions of this article,
revoke the permit issued pursuant to Sec. 66.18 when any condition of said
permit or any law or ordinance, or any rule of the Board relating to, or
regulating the removal, disposal or conveyance of noncombustible rubbish has
been violated.	No permit shall be revoked until a hearing shall have been had by
the Board in the matter of the revocation of such permit in the manner provided
for in Section 22.02 of this Code.
