                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS – VALIDITY. (§ 57.04.10)

  PERMITS – VALIDITY.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 172,043, Eff. 7/13/98.)	Unless otherwise set forth on the
face of the Permit, every Permit issued in accordance with the provisions of
this division shall be valid for the year stated, or until voided, revoked, or
suspended.
