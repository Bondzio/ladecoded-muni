                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SEPARATIONS. (§ 57.40.11)

  SEPARATIONS.	Unless otherwise provided in this article, gasholders used for
the storage of flammable, toxic, or oxidizing gases shall be separated from
buildings, adjacent property lines which may be built upon, streets, alleys,
public ways, aboveground flammable liquid storage tanks, and other similar
gasholders in accordance with the distances set forth in Table 40-A, in relation
to the diameter of the gasholder.
