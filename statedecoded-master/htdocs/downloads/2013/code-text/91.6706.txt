                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENTRY VISION. (§ 91.6706)

  ENTRY VISION.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 171,175, Eff. 7/25/96.)	In residential occupancies, all
entry doors to dwelling units or guest rooms shall be arranged so that the
occupant has a view of the area immediately outside the door without opening the
door.  Such view may be provided by a door viewer, through windows located in
the vicinity of the door or through view ports in the door or adjoining wall. 
Such windows or view ports shall be constructed in compliance with the
provisions of Section 91.6713.
