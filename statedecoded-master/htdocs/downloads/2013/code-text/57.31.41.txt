                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LOCATION OF UNDERGROUND TANKS. (ยง 57.31.41)

ย LOCATION OF UNDERGROUND TANKS.	A.	Tanks which are located in areas where they
may become buoyant due to a rise in the water table, or in areas which are
subject to flooding, shall be anchored in a manner to prevent their floating or
unsafe movement.	B.	Tanks shall be so located with respect to existing building
foundations and supports that the loads carried by these foundations and
supports cannot be transmitted to the tank. The distance from any part of a tank
to the nearest wall or any basement, pit, or cellar shall not be less than one
foot and not be less than three feet from any property line that may be built
upon.	C.	Underground tanks shall be separated from each other by at least one
foot.
