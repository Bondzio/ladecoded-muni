                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTHORITY TO ISSUE PARKING NOTICES. (ยง 57.01.18)

ย AUTHORITY TO ISSUE PARKING NOTICES.	The Chief shall have the authority to
issue parking control notices as provided in Sections 22500, 22500.1, 22514, and
41102 of the State of California Vehicle Code when an illegally parked vehicle
blocks access to a fire hydrant, fire lane, or obstructs access to or from a
fire station.
