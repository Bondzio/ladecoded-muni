                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

JURISDICTION OVER GROINS, ETC. (§ 63.04)

  JURISDICTION OVER GROINS, ETC.	The Department of Playground and Recreation
shall have jurisdiction and control over the construction of any groin, jetty,
breakwater, sea wall, pipe line, sewer, pier, wharf, building or other
structure, except public sewers or storm drains, in, or out from any of the area
over which the said Department has, or may acquire, jurisdiction.
