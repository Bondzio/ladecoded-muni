                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ADDITIONAL COMPLIANCE MONITORING FEES. (§ 192.05)

  ADDITIONAL COMPLIANCE MONITORING FEES.	A.	Any facility that requires
extraordinary compliance monitoring by the Department, in excess of that
performed for other facilities, as determined by the Department, must submit
additional monitoring fees, as set forth in subsection B. below.	B.	After
notification, the applicant will be billed by the Department on a monthly basis
in accordance with the time and materials cost associated with additional
compliance monitoring.  This fee will reflect the cost of staff time utilized
and the cost of City contracted consulting services plus a 10% administrative
fee.
