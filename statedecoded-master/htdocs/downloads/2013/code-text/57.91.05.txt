                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

UNFIRED PRESSURE VESSELS. (§ 57.91.05)

  UNFIRED PRESSURE VESSELS.	Unfired pressure vessels shall be constructed in
accordance with nationally recognized good practice.  When no generally
recognized national or state Code exists for a particular type of pressure
vessel, the Chief may grant approval upon satisfactory proof that such vessel
will withstand a pressure test of four times the designed working pressure.
