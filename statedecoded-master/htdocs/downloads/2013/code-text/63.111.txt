                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ELECTION SUPPLIES – RETURN OF. (§ 63.111)

  ELECTION SUPPLIES – RETURN OF.	No person serving or acting as inspector,
judge, clerk or ballot clerk in any precinct at any election held in this City,
shall fail, refuse, or neglect to deliver to the City Clerk at the same time the
election returns of such precincts are delivered at the office of the City
Clerk, all maps, rubber stamps, ink pads, copies of election laws and all other
election supplies furnished in, to or for such precinct for such election.
