                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION. (§ 91.9605)

  MATERIALS OF CONSTRUCTION.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,261, Eff. 8/30/96.)	All materials permitted by this
Code, including their appropriate allowable stresses and those existing
configurations of materials specified in Division 88 of this Code,  may be
utilized to meet the requirements of this division.
