                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECEIPTS FOR CONTRIBUTIONS. (§ 44.11)

  RECEIPTS FOR CONTRIBUTIONS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,060, Eff. 3/4/03.)	A receipt for the amount donated
shall be given to each contributor requesting one.  Each receipt shall be
signed by the person making the solicitation and shall identify the person on
whose behalf the contribution was received.
