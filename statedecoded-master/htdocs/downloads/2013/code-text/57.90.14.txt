OIL AND GAS SEPARATORS. (ยง 57.90.14)

ย OIL AND GAS SEPARATORS.	At every oil well producing in excess of 20,000 cubic
feet of gas each 24 hours, there shall be installed and maintained an approved
oil and gas separator, unless the gas produced from such oil well is separated
from such oil in tanks equipped with an approved vapor collecting system.
