                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PIPE RACKS. (§ 57.90.11)

  PIPE RACKS.	No portion of any pipe rack or loading platform shall be erected
or constructed unless there is provided a free and unobstructed clearance of not
less than 12 feet from the nearest line of any alley, public street, or
highway.	EXCEPTION:	The provisions of this section shall not apply to any
portable derrick or drilling equipment used for redrilling or servicing any oil
well.
