                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SOLICITING PATRONAGE – MANNER OF. (§ 42.09)

  SOLICITING PATRONAGE – MANNER OF.	No person shall solicit custom or
patronage upon any street or sidewalk in a loud, noisy or boisterous tone of
voice or manner, or cry out or lay hands upon the person or baggage of any
person without the express consent of such person, or obstruct the movement of
any person or follow any person for the purpose of soliciting custom or
patronage.
