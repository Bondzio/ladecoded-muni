                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FEES. (§ 97.0314.1)

  FEES.	(a)	Issuing Fee.  Prior to issuance of a permit pursuant to Division 3
of this Code, the owner shall pay the issuing fee specified in Section
98.0415.	(b)	Minimum Inspection Fee.  The owner shall pay a minimum inspection
fee, as specified in Section 98.0412, to the Department to use any equipment,
machinery or apparatus for which a certificate, license or permit is required by
this Code.  Where the cumulative fees set forth in Section 97.0314 are less
than the minimum inspection fee, the minimum inspection fee shall be paid. 
(First Paragraph Amended by Ord. No. 179,329, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper.
1/1/08.)	EXCEPTION:  (Deleted by Ord. No. 180,868, Eff. 10/22/09.)
