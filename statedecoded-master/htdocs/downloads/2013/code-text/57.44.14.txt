                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

METHODS OF FILLING. (§ 57.44.14)

  METHODS OF FILLING.	A.	No person shall fill any cylinder or tank except by an
approved method of weighing or a liquid level gauging device of approved
design.	B.	The transferring of liquefied flammable gas from a cargo tank to a
cylinder or tank shall be done by pressure differential, pumping, or gravity. An
artificial cooling of cylinders or tanks to reduce pressure below normal
temperature for the purpose of filling is prohibited.	C.	Pressure differential
shall not be obtained by lowering the pressure of the receiving cylinder or tank
by blowing or venting vapor to the atmosphere, except through approved gauging
devices.
