                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SIGNS AT GANGPLANK. (§ 57.95.53)

  SIGNS AT GANGPLANK.	During loading and discharging of tank vessels, a warning
placard carrying letters not less than two inches high on a contrasting
background shall be displayed near the gangway.  This placard shall state as
follows:WARNING
NO OPEN LIGHTS – NO SMOKING
NO VISITORS
