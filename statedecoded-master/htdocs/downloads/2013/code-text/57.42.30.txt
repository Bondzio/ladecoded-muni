                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

VAPORIZERS – GENERAL. (§ 57.42.30)

  VAPORIZERS – GENERAL.	A.	Storage containers for liquid materials shall not
be directly heated with open flames.	B.	Heating coils shall not be installed
inside of storage containers.	C.	Vaporizers may be installed in buildings or
rooms used exclusively for vaporizing or mixing of liquefied
gases.	D.	Vaporizers shall be provided with approved automatic means to prevent
liquid passing from the vaporizer to the gas discharge piping of the vaporizer.
