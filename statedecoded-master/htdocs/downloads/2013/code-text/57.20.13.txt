                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MOTION PICTURE LOCATIONS – TELEVISION REMOTES. (§ 57.20.13)

  MOTION PICTURE LOCATIONS – TELEVISION REMOTES.	No person shall operate any
television broadcasting, videotaping equipment, or motion picture photographic
equipment for commercial or professional purposes, or commercial
still-photographic equipment outside of an approved studio unless notice of such
operation shall have been given to the Fire Department at least 24 hours prior
to the commencement of such operation. Spontaneous news coverage activities are
exempt from this provision.
