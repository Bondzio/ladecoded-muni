                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FILLING AND WITHDRAWING FROM CONTAINERS. (§ 57.92.05)

  FILLING AND WITHDRAWING FROM CONTAINERS.	Containers of Class I or II liquids
shall not be filled or emptied within buildings unless sufficient ventilation is
provided to prevent the accumulation of flammable vapors. Where mechanical
ventilation is required, it shall be kept in operation while flammable vapors
may be present.
