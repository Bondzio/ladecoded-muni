ย REMOVAL OF BENCHES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 112,719, Eff. 2/28/59.)	(a)	After the revocation of the
permit, the Board may remove and store the bench installed or maintained under
such permit, if the permittee fails to do so within 10 days after
notice.	(b)	(Amended by Ord. No. 161,889, Eff. 2/16/87.) The permittee may
recover the bench, if within 60 days after its removal, he pays the cost of such
removal and storage, which shall be the actual cost for removal, and $6.00 per
month for storage, for each such bench. After 60 days from its removal, the
Board may sell, destroy or otherwise dispose of the bench at its discretion. All
of the foregoing shall be at the sole risk of the permittee, and shall be in
addition to any other remedy provided by law for the violation of this section.
