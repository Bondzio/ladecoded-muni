                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIREARMS SALES BY SECONDHAND DEALERS PROHIBITED. (§ 103.311.1)

  FIREARMS SALES BY SECONDHAND DEALERS PROHIBITED.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,268, Eff. 10/6/96.)	All secondhand dealers, as that term
is defined in Section 103.311, are prohibited from vending any weapon defined as
a “Saturday Night Special” as that term is defined in Section 103.314.
