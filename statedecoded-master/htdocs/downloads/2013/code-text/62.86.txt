                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOUSE MOVERS – INTERFERENCE WITH UTILITY PROPERTY. (§ 62.86)

  HOUSE MOVERS – INTERFERENCE WITH UTILITY PROPERTY.	No house mover shall
interfere in any manner whatsoever with any property of any public utility,
notwithstanding anything in the permit granted by the Board pursuant to the
terms of Sections 62.84 to 62.93 inclusive.
