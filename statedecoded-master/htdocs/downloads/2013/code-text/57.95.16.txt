                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPETENCE OF PERSONS. (ยง 57.95.16)

ย COMPETENCE OF PERSONS.	It is the intent of this article that all persons
performing duties and coordinating operations therein regulated shall be
sufficiently experienced and capable to perform with competence and
understanding in the interests of public safety.	When any person is incompetent
by reason of intoxication, disorderly conduct, obvious inexperience or a
language barrier, the Chief shall summarily require operations to be
discontinued.EQUIPMENT
