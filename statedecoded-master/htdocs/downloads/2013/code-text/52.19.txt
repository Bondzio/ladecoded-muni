                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NEWS MEDIA IDENTIFICATION CARDS – RECORDS. (§ 52.19)

  NEWS MEDIA IDENTIFICATION CARDS – RECORDS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 150,115, Eff. 10/20/77.)	A.	The Board or the Department
(if the Board has delegated authority to the Department) shall at all times
maintain a correct current list of the names, addresses and employers (if any)
of the bearers of all cards which have been issued pursuant to Section 52.16
A.	B.	Each person to whom a card has been issued shall, within five days, notify
the Board in writing as to each and every change of his or her residence address
or employer.
