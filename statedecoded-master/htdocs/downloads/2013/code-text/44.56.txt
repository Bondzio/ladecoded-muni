                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LICENSING OF BINGO SUPPLIERS. (§ 44.56)

  LICENSING OF BINGO SUPPLIERS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 164,083, Eff. 11/6/88.)	(1)	The Police Department shall have
the authority to license businesses which provide bingo supplies or equipment to
organizations licensed to play bingo in the City of Los Angeles and to
promulgate and administer rules and regulations with respect to the licensing of
such organizations.  (First Sentence Amended by Ord. No. 171,262, Eff.
10/4/96.) Such rules shall be submitted to and approved by the City
Council.	(2)	No person shall engage in, conduct, manage or carry on the business
of selling, or sell, bingo supplies or equipment to any organization licensed to
play bingo in the City of Los Angeles without a license for such purpose issued
by the Police Department.  (Amended by Ord. No. 171,262, Eff. 10/4/96.)
