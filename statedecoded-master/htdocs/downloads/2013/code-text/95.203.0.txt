                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

A. (§ 95.203.0)

  
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,327, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	Section 203.0 of
the C.M.C. is adopted by reference, except that the C.M.C. definition of the
following terms is not adopted:	AIR, SUPPLY	The following definition is also
adopted:	AIR, SUPPLY is air being provided to a space or piece of equipment from
the outside or inside a building by means of ducts or openings.
