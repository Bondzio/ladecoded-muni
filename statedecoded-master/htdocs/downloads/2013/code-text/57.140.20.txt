                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RECHARGE OF PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS. (§ 57.140.20)

  RECHARGE OF PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.	A.	The acceptability of recharge
materials to be used for the recharging of foam or other extinguishers which are
dependent upon a chemical reaction for their operation, shall be based upon a
chemical analysis as well as performance of the extinguisher.	B.	If a dry
chemical type extinguisher is recharged with recharge materials other than those
specified by the manufacturer, the permittee performing the recharge shall use
recharge materials that will not alter the rating of the extinguisher nor reduce
the efficiency of its operation.
