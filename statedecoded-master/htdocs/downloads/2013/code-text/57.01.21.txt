                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTHORITY TO ESTABLISH BARRIERS. (ยง 57.01.21)

ย AUTHORITY TO ESTABLISH BARRIERS.	Police Officers and members of the
Department are empowered to erect barriers at points of access to a fire or
other emergency and to prevent any and all persons from passing through any such
barriers, and from entering into any space set apart or enclosed by means
thereof, except persons having a pass as hereinafter specified.
