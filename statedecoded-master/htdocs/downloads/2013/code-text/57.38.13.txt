                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REPAIR. (§ 57.38.13)

  REPAIR.	A.	A tank vehicle shall not be operated unless it is in good
mechanical condition, devoid of accumulations of grease, oil, or other
flammables, and free of leaks.	B.	No cargo tank shall be repaired by any method
employing a flame, arc, or other source of ignition unless the tank is purged of
all flammable or combustible liquids and vapors, or made safe in another manner
approved by the Chief.
