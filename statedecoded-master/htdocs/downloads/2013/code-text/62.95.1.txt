                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HORSEBACK RIDING PROHIBITED ON MEDIANS. (§ 62.95.1)

  HORSEBACK RIDING PROHIBITED ON MEDIANS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 165,293, Eff. 12/14/89.)	Horses are prohibited on the median
of any street in the City of Los Angeles except in those medians where bridle
paths exist.
