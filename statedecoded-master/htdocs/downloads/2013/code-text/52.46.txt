                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SHORT WAVE RADIOS, USE OF. (§ 52.46)

  SHORT WAVE RADIOS, USE OF.	No person who intercepts, overhears or receives
any message or communication transmitted by any radio transmission station
operating upon a wave length or radio frequency assigned by the Federal
Communication Commission for use by any police or law enforcement department
shall, for the financial benefit of himself or another communicate such message
or communication to another or directly or indirectly use the information so
obtained.
