                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FEES FOR MOVED BUILDINGS AND STRUCTURES. (§ 93.0228)

  FEES FOR MOVED BUILDINGS AND STRUCTURES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 171,185, Eff. 7/22/96.)	A fee of $100.00 shall be charged
for the inspection of the existing electrical wiring, if any, in or on a moved
building or structure.  Fees as required elsewhere in this division shall be
paid before any other electrical wiring installed except that necessary to
connect the existing electrical wiring to the grounding electrode.
