                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INDOOR MOISTURE CONTROL. (§ 99.05.505)

99.05.505.1     Buildings shall meet or exceed the provisions of Los Angeles
Building Code Sections 1203 (Ventilation) and Division 14 (Exterior Walls). 
For additional measures not applicable to low-rise residential occupancies, see
Section 5.407.2 of this code.
