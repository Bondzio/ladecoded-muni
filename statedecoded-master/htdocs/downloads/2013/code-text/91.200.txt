                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 91.200)

  GENERAL.
	Chapter 2 of the CBC is adopted by reference with the following exceptions,
modifications and additions:91.201.4.Terms Not Defined.  Where terms, phrases
and words are not defined they shall have the ordinarily accepted meaning as the
context implies.  The definitions in Webster’s Third New International
Dictionary of the English Language, Unabridged shall be considered as providing
ordinarily accepted meanings.
