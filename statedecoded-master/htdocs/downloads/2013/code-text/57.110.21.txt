                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROJECTION OF CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM. (§ 57.110.21)

  PROJECTION OF CELLULOSE NITRATE FILM.	No person shall use any cellulose
nitrate film in any motion picture projection machine in any assembly occupancy
unless the same is located in a projection room designed and constructed in
accordance with requirements of Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Building Code).
