                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DILUTION AND NEUTRALIZATION. (§ 57.72.31)

  DILUTION AND NEUTRALIZATION.	Areas used for the storage or processing of
corrosive materials shall be equipped with at least one of the
following:	A.	Water outlets, with attached hose, for adequate dilution and
flushing of spills to a safe location.	B.	A covered and labeled container of
absorbent-neutralizer mixture suitable for the neutralization and absorption of
corrosive materials spills.  There shall not be less than one such container
holding 50 pounds of an absorbent-neutralizer mixture.
