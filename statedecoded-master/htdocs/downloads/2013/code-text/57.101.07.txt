                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SOURCE OF IGNITION. (ยง 57.101.07)

ย SOURCE OF IGNITION.	A.	No open flame or other source of ignition shall be
permitted inside any aircraft factory or hangar which is used for application of
Class I or II liquids as finishes, nor shall any open flame or flame-producing
devices be permitted in any hangar, except in locations approved by the
Chief.	B.	No ignition source not a part of the operation shall be located,
brought, or permitted within 50 feet of any transfer apparatus at an aircraft
fueling station.
