                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

CHOCK BLOCKS AND PARKING BRAKES. (§ 57.44.18)

  CHOCK BLOCKS AND PARKING BRAKES.	Every cargo tank vehicle shall be equipped
with at least two chock blocks.  The parking brake shall be set and chock
blocks shall be placed in such a manner as to prevent the forward or backward
motion of the vehicle whenever it is parked, left unattended by the driver, or
during loading and unloading operations.
