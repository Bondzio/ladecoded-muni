                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.61.01)

  SCOPE.	The provisions of this division shall regulate industrial ovens and
furnaces which are not an integral part of a building or room.  In addition to
the requirements specified herein, ovens and furnaces shall be constructed,
installed, and maintained in compliance with L.A.F.D. Standard No.
7.	EXCEPTIONS:	1.	Drying ovens which are regulated by Division 84 of this
article.	2.	Warm-air furnaces used for comfort heating.	Industrial ovens and
furnaces which are an integral part of a building or room are regulated by
Chapter 9 of the L.A.M.C. (Mechanical Code).
