                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

B. (§ 95.204.0)

  
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,327, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	Section 204.0 of
the C.M.C. is adopted by reference, except that the C.M.C. definition of the
following term is not adopted:	BUILDING CODE	The following definition is also
adopted:	BOARD is the Board of Building and Safety Commissioners of the City of
Los Angeles.	BUILDING CODE is the City of Los Angeles Building Code, set forth
in Article 1 of Chapter IX of the Los Angeles Municipal Code.
