	(Added by Ord. No. 171,260, Eff. 8/30/96.)	The provisions of this division may
be applied to all buildings designed under building codes in effect prior to
January 13, 1976, or built with building permits issued prior to January 13,
1977, having concrete floors and/or concrete roofs supported by reinforced
concrete walls or concrete frames and columns, and/or concrete frames with
masonry infills.
