                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

POUND, HOURS OPEN. (§ 53.01)

  POUND, HOURS OPEN.	The public pound of this City, including district animal
shelters, shall be kept open to the public for the transaction of business
during such hours and on such days as shall be established by the General
Manager.  (Amended by Ord. No. 152,811, Eff. 10/1/79.)
