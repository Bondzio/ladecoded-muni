                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COLLECTION – DEPOSITING OF. (§ 53.25)

  COLLECTION – DEPOSITING OF.	All moneys received by the Department for
licenses issued under the provisions of this article and all moneys collected
under actions brought as herein provided to recover unpaid license taxes, and
all moneys received by said Department from sales of animals, or as fees from
any source, shall be paid by the Department into the City Treasury and credited
to the departmental receipts of said Department. (Amended  by Ord. No. 133,983,
Eff. 4/10/67.)
