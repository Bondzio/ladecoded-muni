                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 94.2001.0)

  SCOPE.	The standards of this division provide the minimum requirements for
the design and installation of automatic fire protection systems.  The design
of all fire protection systems shall be in conformity with accepted engineering
practices and the provisions of this Code.	Automatic fire sprinkler systems
shall be installed in locations required by the Building Code, and all fire
sprinkler systems shall be in conformance with the approved plans and this Code.
