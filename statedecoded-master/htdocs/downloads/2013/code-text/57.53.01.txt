                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MINIMUM SAFETY. (§ 57.53.01)

  MINIMUM SAFETY.	Reasonable care shall be exercised to prevent personal
injuries or property damage arising from fire or explosion caused by flammable
dusts.  Unless otherwise regulated by this division, compliance with L.A.F.D.
Standard No. 42 is required with respect to flammable or explosive dust.
