                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WARNING FOR SMALL CRAFT. (§ 57.95.54)

  WARNING FOR SMALL CRAFT.	At least two placards reading “DANGER – KEEP
AWAY” in letters not less than six inches in height on a contrasting
background shall be located on offshore side of vessel transferring Grade A, B,
C, or D products or Class 1 and 2 liquefied petroleum gases, in such a position
that placards may be readily seen by approaching craft.  Only one such sign
shall be required for barges.  Such signs shall be furnished by the Terminal at
which the vessel is moored.
