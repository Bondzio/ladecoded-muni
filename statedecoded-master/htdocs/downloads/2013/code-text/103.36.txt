                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION WITHOUT HEARING. (§ 103.36)

  SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION WITHOUT HEARING.	(a)	Conviction.  If any person
holding a permit under this article is convicted in any court of the violation
of any law relative to his permit, the Board may revoke said permit forthwith
without any further action thereon other than giving notice of revocation to the
permittee.	(b)	Failure to Comply with Order in Regard to Stolen Property.  The
Board may, without a hearing, suspend or revoke the permit of a permittee who
fails to comply with an order of the Board made pursuant to Sec.103.42.
