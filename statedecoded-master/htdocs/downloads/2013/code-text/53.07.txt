ย ANIMALS ON UNENCLOSED PREMISES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 150,337, Eff. 1/1/78.)	No person shall stake out, herd or
graze any animal upon any unenclosed lot or land in any manner so that said
animal may be or go beyond the boundary of such lot or land.
