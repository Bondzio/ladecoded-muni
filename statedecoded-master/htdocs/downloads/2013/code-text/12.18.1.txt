                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

“WC” WARNER CENTER SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE. (§ 12.18.1)

  “WC” WARNER CENTER SPECIFIC PLAN ZONE.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,870, Eff. 8/9/93.)	The following regulations shall apply
in the “WC” Warner Center Specific Plan Zone:	A.	Purpose.  The regulations
set forth in this section are intended to reflect the regulations contained in
the Warner Center Specific Plan Ordinance.	B.	Use.  No building, structure or
land shall be used and no building or structure shall be erected, structurally
altered, enlarged or maintained, except as permitted by Sections 7 and 10 of the
Warner Center Specific Plan Ordinance.	C.	Area.  No building or structure shall
be erected or maintained nor shall the enlargement of any building or structure
be erected or maintained unless the yard, lot area, setback and loading spaces
required by Sections 7 and 10 of the Warner Center Specific Plan are provided
and maintained in connection with the building, structure or enlargement.
