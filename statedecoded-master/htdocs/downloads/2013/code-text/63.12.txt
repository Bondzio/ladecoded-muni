                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FILING FEE. (§ 63.12)

  FILING FEE.	All applicants for such permits shall deposit with the Board of
Playground and Recreation Commissioners a filing fee of Ten Dollars ($10.00) at
the time of making such application, which sum shall be retained by the Board in
the event such application is finally granted. In the event the application is
denied, the Board shall return any and all deposits to the applicant.
