                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOSE AND NOZZLE. (ยง 57.100.23)

ย HOSE AND NOZZLE.	No hose used for transferring Class I, II, or IIIA liquids
from any fixed dispensing apparatus shall be more than 14 feet long, including
nozzle.	EXCEPTION:	The Chief may approve longer lengths of hose upon
satisfactory proof of need.
