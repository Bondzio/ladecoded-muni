                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EMPLOYEES – USE OF RUBBISH. (§ 66.29)

  EMPLOYEES – USE OF RUBBISH.	No employee of this City shall remove or
dispose of, for said employee’s individual use or benefit, any of the contents
of any vessel, tank or receptacle used for the collection, removal or disposal
of rubbish.  (Amended by Ord. No. 108,011, Eff. 10/1/56.)
