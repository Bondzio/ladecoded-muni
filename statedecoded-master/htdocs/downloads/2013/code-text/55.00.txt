                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GUNS – PERMITS. (§ 55.00)

  GUNS – PERMITS.

	No person shall fire, shoot or discharge any gun, rifle, pistol or other
firearm, or any air-gun, air-rifle or air-pistol, or any spring-gun,
spring-rifle or spring-pistol, or any carbon dioxide or other gas operated gun,
rifle or pistol without having first obtained a written permit from the Board of
Police Commissioners.
