ย CAPTIVE BALLOONS AND SIMILAR DEVICES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173,944, Eff. 6/18/01.)	It shall be unlawful to permit a
balloon or similar device which floats in the air and is restrained, attached or
held in place by a cord, rope, cable or similar means, to float, ascend, rise or
remain aloft at a vertical height of five feet or more above the surface of the
ground measured to the highest point of the balloon or similar device.
