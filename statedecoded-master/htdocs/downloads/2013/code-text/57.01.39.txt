                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

AUTHORITY TO LIST HAZARDOUS MATERIALS. (§ 57.01.39)

  AUTHORITY TO LIST HAZARDOUS MATERIALS.	The Chief shall have the authority to
establish a list of hazardous materials and/or to adopt, by reference,
nationally recognized documents listing hazardous materials as may be necessary
to carry out the purpose and intent of this article. Hazardous materials shall
be defined as set forth in Division 2 of this article.FIRE CODE REVISION
