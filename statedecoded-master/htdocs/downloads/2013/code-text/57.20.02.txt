                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISCARDING FLAMING OR GLOWING OBJECTS. (§ 57.20.02)

  DISCARDING FLAMING OR GLOWING OBJECTS.	No person shall throw or place, or
cause to be thrown or placed, any lighted match, cigar, cigarette, ashes, or
other flaming or glowing substance or thing in any place where it may directly
or indirectly start a fire.
