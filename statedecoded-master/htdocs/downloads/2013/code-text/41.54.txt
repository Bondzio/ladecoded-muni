ย FISHING WITHIN ONE MILE OF PUBLIC SEWER OUTLET.
	(Added by Ord. No. 127,508, Eff. 6/29/64.)	(a)	It shall be unlawful for any
person to catch or take by means of any line, hook, seine, net or by any other
means or device, any fish in or from any water within one mile from the outlet
or point of discharge of any public sewer.	(b)	It shall be unlawful for any
person to sell, offer, or expose for sale, in the City of Los Angeles any fish
caught in or taken from any water within a distance of one mile from the outlet
or point of discharge of any public sewer.
