                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

LICENSES – WHAT TO STATE. (§ 53.17)

  LICENSES – WHAT TO STATE.	(a)	Each such license shall state:	(1)	The name
and residence of the person to whom such license is issued;	(2)	The amount paid
therefor and the character thereof;	(3)	The date when issued;	(4)	The date on
which such license shall expire;	(5)	A description of the dog for which such
license is issued;	(6)	The number of the metallic tag accompanying the
same;	(b)	(None)
