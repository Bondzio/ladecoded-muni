                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MOTION PICTURE SHOWS. (§ 103.109)

  MOTION PICTURE SHOWS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,676, Eff. 1/11/04.)	(a)	Defined.  As used in this
article, “motion picture shows” means the exhibiting or presenting of motion
pictures to the public in any theater, show house or other place of
entertainment.	(b)	Permit Required.  No person shall engage in the business of
exhibiting or presenting motion picture shows without a written permit from the
Board.
