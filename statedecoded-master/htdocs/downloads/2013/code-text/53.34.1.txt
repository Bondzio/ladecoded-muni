ย MENACING DOGS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,864, Eff. 7/7/93.)	No person, owning or having custody
or control of any dog, whether or not restrained by a substantial chain or
leash, shall permit the dog to unlawfully assault, threaten or menace any human
being or other animal upon any public street, sidewalk, park or other public
property, or in or upon the premises or private property of another.
