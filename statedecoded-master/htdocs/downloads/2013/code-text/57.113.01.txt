                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.113.01)

  SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 169,944, Eff. 8/20/94.)	This division and applicable
provisions of Titles 19 and Part 2, Title 24, C.C.R., shall regulate any
institutional occupancy used or intended to be used for a community care
facility or Group I Division 1, 2, 3, or Group I, Division 1A or 2A occupancy. 
Additional requirements for fire alarm systems, fire extinguishers, exits, and
other appropriate provisions are addressed in other divisions of this article.
