                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEFINITIONS. (§ 99.04.202)

  DEFINITIONS.99.04.202.1.    The following words and terms shall, for the
purposes of this chapter and as used elsewhere in this code, have the meanings
shown herein.	ENERGY STAR.  A joint program of the U.S. Environmental
Protection Agency and the U.S. Department of Energy.  ENERGY STAR is a
voluntary program designed to identify and promote energy-efficient products and
practices.
