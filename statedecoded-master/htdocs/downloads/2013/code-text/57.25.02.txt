                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

POSTING OF SIGNS PROHIBITING SMOKING AND BURNING. (§ 57.25.02)

  POSTING OF SIGNS PROHIBITING SMOKING AND BURNING.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 176,943, Eff. 10/5/05.)	There shall be no open burning or
smoking in the Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone.  The Chief shall cause such
districts to be posted at all times by suitable signs giving notice thereof.
