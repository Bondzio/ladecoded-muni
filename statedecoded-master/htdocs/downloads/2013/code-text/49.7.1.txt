                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

RELATION TO THE CITY CHARTER. (§ 49.7.1)

  RELATION TO THE CITY CHARTER.

	The provisions of this Article relating to contribution limitations are in
addition to and supplement the regulations contained in Charter Sections 470 and
609(e).  The provisions of this Article relating to public financing and
expenditure limitations are adopted pursuant to the authorization contained in
Charter Section 471.
