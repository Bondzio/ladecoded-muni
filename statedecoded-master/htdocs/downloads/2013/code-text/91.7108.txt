APPLICATION OF METHANE SEEPAGE REGULATIONS TO LOCATIONS OR AREAS OUTSIDE THE
METHANE ZONE AND METHANE BUFFER ZONE BOUNDARIES. (ยง 91.7108)

ย APPLICATION OF METHANE SEEPAGE REGULATIONS TO LOCATIONS OR AREAS OUTSIDE THE
METHANE ZONE AND METHANE BUFFER ZONE BOUNDARIES.	Upon a determination by the
Department of Building and Safety that a hazard may exist from methane intrusion
at a geographical location or in an area outside the boundaries established in
Section 91.7103 of this Code, the Department of Building and Safety and the Fire
Department may enforce any or all of the requirements of Division 71 of this
Code as required to preclude potential fire or explosion from methane
concentration.
