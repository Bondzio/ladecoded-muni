                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PARKING OF AUTOMOBILES. (§ 57.115.10)

  PARKING OF AUTOMOBILES.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 169,944, Eff. 8/20/94.)	Automotive equipment that is
necessary to the operation of the establishment shall not be parked within 20
feet of the tent or membrane structure.  No other automotive equipment or
internal combustion engines shall be located within 100 feet of a tent or
membrane structure except vehicles parked on a public street shall park at least
20 feet from any tent or membrane structure.
