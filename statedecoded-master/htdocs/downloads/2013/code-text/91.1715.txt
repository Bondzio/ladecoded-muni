                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MATERIAL AND TEST STANDARDS. (§ 91.1715)

  MATERIAL AND TEST STANDARDS.	Section 1715 of the CBC is adopted by reference,
except Sections 1715.1, 1715.1.1 and 1715.2 of the CBC are not adopted and in
lieu, Sections 91.1715.1, 91.1715.1.1 and 91.1715.2 are added.91.1715.1.  Test
Standards for Joist Hangers and Connectors.91.1715.1.1.  Test Standards for
Joist Hangers.  The vertical load-bearing capacity, torsional moment capacity
and deflection characteristics of joist hangers shall be determined in
accordance with ASTM D 1761 using lumber having a specific gravity of 0.49 or
greater, but not greater than 0.55, as determined in accordance with AF&#038;PA
NDS for the joist and headers.  Required testing shall be conducted or
witnessed by an approved agency.	EXCEPTION:  The joist length shall not be
required to exceed 24 inches (610 mm).91.1715.2.  Concrete and Clay Roof
Tiles.  Required tests for concrete and clay roof tiles shall be conducted or
witnessed by an approved testing agency.
