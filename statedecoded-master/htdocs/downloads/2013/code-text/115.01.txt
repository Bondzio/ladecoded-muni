                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURPOSE. (§ 115.01)

  PURPOSE.

	The Council enacts this legislation for the sole purpose of securing and
promoting the public health, comfort, safety, and welfare of its citizenry. 
While recognizing that certain uses of sound amplifying equipment are protected
by the constitutional rights of freedom of speech and assembly, the Council
nevertheless feels obligated to reasonably regulate the use of sound amplifying
equipment in order to protect the correlative constitutional rights of the
citizens of this community to privacy and freedom from public nuisance of loud
and unnecessary noise.
