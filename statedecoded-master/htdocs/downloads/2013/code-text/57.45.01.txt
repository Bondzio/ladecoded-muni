                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (§ 57.45.01)

  SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	The provisions of this division
and LAFD Standard No. 68 shall regulate the design, installation, maintenance,
and testing of nonflammable medical gas systems when used for medical purposes
in noninstitutional facilities.	If a conflict arises between LAFD Standard No.
68 and the provisions of this division, the more restrictive shall apply.
