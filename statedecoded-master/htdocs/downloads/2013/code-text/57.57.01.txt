                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SCOPE. (ยง 57.57.01)

ย SCOPE.	This division shall regulate the storage or warehousing of combustible
materials inside of buildings in relation to arrangement, location, and size of
areas, height of stored materials, separations, housekeeping, and fire
protection. The specifications set forth in L.A.F.D. Standard No. 61 may be used
in the design and construction of rack storage of combustible materials over 12
feet in height.
