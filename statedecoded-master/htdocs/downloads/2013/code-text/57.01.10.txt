                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

INTERPRETATION OF SECTION NUMBERS. (ยง 57.01.10)

ย INTERPRETATION OF SECTION NUMBERS.	In reading a section number from left to
right, the first two digits indicate that this article is Chapter 5, Article 7,
of the Los Angeles Municipal Code. The digits after the first decimal point and
preceding the second decimal point indicate the divisions within this article.
The digits following the second decimal point designate the section numbers
within each division.
