                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SEALING OF PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS. (§ 57.140.22)

  SEALING OF PORTABLE FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.	A.	Whenever practicable,
extinguishers shall be sealed after being charged or recharged, in such a manner
that the extinguisher cannot be discharged without breaking the seal.	B.	All
seal and wire assemblies used for sealing extinguishers shall use a seal press
that bears the Certificate of Registration number of the person performing the
charge or recharge of the extinguisher. Methods for sealing extinguishers other
than seal and wire methods may be approved by the Chief.
