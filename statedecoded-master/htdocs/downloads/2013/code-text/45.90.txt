                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENFORCEMENT. (§ 45.90)

  ENFORCEMENT.	A.	Civil Action.  Any aggrieved person may enforce the
provisions of this article by means of a Civil Action.	B.	Injunction.	1.	Any
person who commits, or proposes to commit, an act in violation of this article
may be enjoined therefrom by a court of competent jurisdiction.	2.	Action for
Injunction under this subsection may be brought by any aggrieved person, by the
City Attorney, or by any person or entity which will fairly and adequately
represent the interests of the protected class.	C.	Non-Exclusive.  Nothing in
this article shall preclude any aggrieved person from seeking any other remedy
provided by law.	D.	Exception.  Notwithstanding any provision of this code to
the contrary, no criminal penalties shall attach for any violation of the
provision of this article.
