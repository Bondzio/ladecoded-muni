                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SITE WORK. (§ 91.3304)

  SITE WORK.
	(Amended by Ord. 181,758, Eff. 8/8/11.)	Section 3304 of the CBC is adopted by
reference, except Section 3304.1.4 of the CBC is not adopted and in lieu Section
91.3304.1.4 is added.91.3304.1.4.  Fill Supporting Foundations.  Fill to be
used to support the foundations of any building or structure shall comply with
CBC Section 1804.5 and Division 70 of this Code.  Special inspections of
compacted fill shall be in accordance with CBC Section 1704.7.
