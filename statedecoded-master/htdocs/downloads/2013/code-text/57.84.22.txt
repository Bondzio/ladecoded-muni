                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURGING INTERVAL. (§ 57.84.22)

  PURGING INTERVAL.	The ventilating system in any oven in which flammable
vapors or gases may collect from the act of drying materials in the oven or from
unburned fuel in a direct fired oven shall be operated until at least four
complete changes of air have been made before the burner ignition system may be
operated, fuel turned on, or the conveyor operated.
