                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PROTECTION OF LIQUEFIED FLAMMABLE GAS DISPENSING APPARATUS. (§ 57.94.22)

  PROTECTION OF LIQUEFIED FLAMMABLE GAS DISPENSING APPARATUS.	Liquefied
flammable gas dispensing apparatus shall be installed on a concrete foundation,
and shall be adequately protected by crash barriers or guard rails not less than
30 inches in height measured above the adjacent grade.  Crash barriers and
guard rails shall be of noncombustible material, and have a strength equivalent
to that of six inch standard weight steel pipes.  Where vertical posts are
utilized, they shall be imbedded in not less than two feet of concrete below
grade surface.
