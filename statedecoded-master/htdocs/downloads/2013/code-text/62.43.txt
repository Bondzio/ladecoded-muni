                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

MANHOLES – WARNING SIGNS. (§ 62.43)

  MANHOLES – WARNING SIGNS.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 121,900, Eff. 6/4/62.)	Except as otherwise provided in
Section 62.42, no person shall open or remove the cover of any manhole, or allow
any manhole to remain open in or upon any street or sidewalk, without first
placing immediately adjacent to the opening of any manhole, a barrier or manhole
guard at least two red flags, and, from sunset until sunrise of the following
day, at least two warning lights. Such guards or warning devices shall be
maintained at all times while the manhole remains open. Such warning devices
shall be so located that they are clearly visible to approaching traffic.
