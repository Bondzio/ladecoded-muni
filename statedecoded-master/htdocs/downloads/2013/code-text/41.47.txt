                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WATER CLOSETS – USE OF. (§ 41.47)

  WATER CLOSETS – USE OF.
	(Added by Ord. No. 127,508, Eff. 6/29/64.)	No person owning, controlling or
having charge of any bathroom or water closet room shall permit any person to
use such rooms for any purpose other than toilet purposes.
