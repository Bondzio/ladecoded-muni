                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PUBLIC DANCE. (§ 21.85)

  PUBLIC DANCE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 166,204, Eff. 10/11/90, Oper. 1/1/91.)	For every person
conducting or staging any public dance, whether for profit or not, to which the
public at large is admitted or in which the public at large is allowed to
participate, the tax shall be $177.38 per day.
