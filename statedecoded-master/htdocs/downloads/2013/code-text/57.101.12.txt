                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PERMITS REQUIRED. (§ 57.101.12)

  PERMITS REQUIRED.	A valid Permit from the Department is required in
accordance with Division 4 of this article, when a building or premises is used
as an aircraft fueling station, private airport, or heliport.  Further, a valid
Permit and General Approval is required, in accordance with Divisions 4 and 5 of
this article, for an aircraft fuel servicing tank vehicle designed and operated
for the purpose of fueling aircraft on the grounds of an airport or heliport.
