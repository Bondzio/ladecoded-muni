                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DISPENSING NEAR OPEN FLAME. (§ 57.100.24)

  DISPENSING NEAR OPEN FLAME.	No person shall dispense any Class I, II, or IIIA
liquid within 25 feet of any open flame.  No dispensing apparatus shall be
located, maintained, or used in any repair shop portion of any garage or in any
other location where open flames or flame producing devices are used or
installed within 25 feet thereof.
