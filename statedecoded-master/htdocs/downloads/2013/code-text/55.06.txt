USE OF BOWS AND ARROWS PROHIBITED. (§ 55.06)

  USE OF BOWS AND ARROWS PROHIBITED.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 146,676, Eff. 11/25/74.)	The use of bows and arrows in the
City of Los Angeles is prohibited except when shooting at targets on an
established public or private range.	For the purpose of this section, the
following phrases shall be construed as hereinafter set out:	An “Established
target range” is one where adequate precautions have been taken to insure that
arrows do not pass beyond the perimeter of the target range facilities.	The term
“bows and arrows” shall include crossbows and any other device for the
shooting of bolts or arrows capable of piercing flesh.
