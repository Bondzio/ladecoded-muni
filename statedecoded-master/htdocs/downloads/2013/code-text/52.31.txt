                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

POLICE IDENTIFICATION CARDS – IMITATION OF. (§ 52.31)

  POLICE IDENTIFICATION CARDS – IMITATION OF.	No person shall print or issue
or have in his possession any identification card similar to the identification
card mentioned in Section 52.29 of this Code, or any identification card stating
that the person whose name appears thereon is a police officer or sworn officer
of this City.
