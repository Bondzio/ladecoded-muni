                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL. (§ 161.1001.1)

.  GENERAL.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 175,325, Eff. 8/10/03.)	Any person or entity subject to an
order pursuant to Division 7 of this article, or an inspection fee imposed
without hearing pursuant to Division 9 of this article, may appeal the order or
fee to, or may request an extension of the time from, a Senior Inspector.  The
appeal shall be made in writing, upon appropriate forms provided by the
Department, and shall specify the grounds for appeal.  The appeal shall be
filed within ten calendar days of the issuance of the order, or within ten
calendar days of imposition of the fee.  The Senior Inspector shall issue a
decision within ten calendar days of the filing of the appeal.  A copy of the
decision shall be served on the person or entity subject to the order or fee by
first class United States mail, postage prepaid, or in person.
