                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

PURPOSE. (ยง 57.01.02)

ย PURPOSE.	It is the purpose of this article to prescribe laws for the
safeguarding of life and property from fire, explosion, panic, or other
hazardous conditions which may arise in the use or occupancy of buildings,
structures, or premises; and to prescribe such other laws as it may be the duty
of the Fire Department to enforce.
