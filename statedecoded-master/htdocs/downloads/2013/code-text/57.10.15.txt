                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

EMERGENCY PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. (§ 57.10.15)

  EMERGENCY PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.
	(Added by Ord. No. 168,176, Eff. 9/20/92.)	A.	In all containment areas greater
than 1,000 square feet, five “Tyvek” type protective entry suits, rated for
use in an asbestos containment area, shall be provided in the clean room.	B.	All
suits shall be extra large and shall be kept in a red box clearly identified
with white 3” letters stating, “Fire Department Emergency Use Only.”  The
box shall be readily accessible at all times.
