                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ARTIFICIAL HEATING. (§ 57.50.11)

  ARTIFICIAL HEATING.	A.	Artificial heating is prohibited in a film vault.  In
any other room in which cellulose nitrate film is handled, processed, or stored
heating shall be restricted to steam not exceeding 15 pounds pressure, hot
water, approved electric steam radiators, or indirect heating systems.	B.	All
radiators, heating coils, and pipes shall be protected by substantial wire mesh
guards.	C.	The use of portable space heaters is prohibited in any location where
cellulose nitrate film is used, processed, produced, stored, or handled.
