                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

SUPPORTS. (§ 57.84.14)

  SUPPORTS.	Every length of such vent pipe shall be securely and substantially
supported.  Every such vent pipe shall extend at least three feet above the
roof of any building within 15 feet, and if it projects more than four feet
above the roof of the building, or has a joint in such vent pipe above the roof,
it shall be securely guyed or braced.
