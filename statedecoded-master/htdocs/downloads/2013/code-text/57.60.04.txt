                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REMOVAL OF SLUDGE AND EXTRANEOUS MATERIALS. (§ 57.60.04)

  REMOVAL OF SLUDGE AND EXTRANEOUS MATERIALS.	A.	Accumulations of sediment,
products of partial decomposition and articles which have fallen to the bottom
shall be removed as often as necessary to prevent uneven heating of the bath. 
Because of possible thermite action, no article shall be allowed to remain in
the bottom of the pot.	B.	Precautions shall be taken to prevent the entrapment
of water or air in the treated material or in the tools. All combustible
material shall be kept out of the bath.
