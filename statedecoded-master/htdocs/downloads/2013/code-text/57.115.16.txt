                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

HOUSEKEEPING. (§ 57.115.16)

  HOUSEKEEPING.	A.	All weeds and hazardous vegetation shall be removed from the
area occupied by any tent or membrane structure and from premises adjacent to or
within 50 feet of any tent or membrane structure.	B.	Hay, straw, trash, and
other combustible material shall not be stored within 50 feet of any tent or
membrane structure.	C.	The grounds both inside and outside of tents and membrane
structures shall be kept free and clear of hazardous refuse.  Such waste shall
be stored in approved containers and locations in accordance with Division 21 of
this article.
