                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FLAMMABLE AND COMBUSTIBLE LIQUIDS. (§ 57.110.18)

  FLAMMABLE AND COMBUSTIBLE LIQUIDS.	Flammable and combustible liquids shall
not be used or stored within any assembly occupancy.	EXCEPTIONS:	1.	Quantities
not exceeding 10 gallons for maintenance purposes and operation of equipment
when stored in approved containers and approved locations.	2.	Storage in excess
of 10 gallons that is used for maintenance purposes and operation of equipment
when stored in hazardous material cabinets.
