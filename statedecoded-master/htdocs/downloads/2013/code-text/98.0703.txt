                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ENFORCEMENT AUTHORITY. (§ 98.0703)

  ENFORCEMENT AUTHORITY.
	(Added by Ord. No. 172,782, Eff. 9/30/99.)	The Superintendent is authorized to
administer and enforce the provisions of this division.  The Superintendent or
anyone designated by the Superintendent may exercise any enforcement powers as
provided in Chapter IX of this Code.
