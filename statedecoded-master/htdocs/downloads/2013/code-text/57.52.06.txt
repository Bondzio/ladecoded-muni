                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

WAREHOUSES AND STORES. (§ 57.52.06)

  WAREHOUSES AND STORES.	A.	Magnesium storage in warehouses and stores in a
quantity greater than 1,000 cubic feet shall be separated into piles not larger
than 1,000 cubic feet each with aisles between, equal in width to not less than
the height of the piles.	B.	Combustible materials shall be separated from
magnesium storage by aisles equal in width to not less than the height of the
piles of magnesium plus 10 feet.
