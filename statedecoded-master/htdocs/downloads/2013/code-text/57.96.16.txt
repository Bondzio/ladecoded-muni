                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FIRE PROTECTION. (§ 57.96.16)

  FIRE PROTECTION.	A.	At each limited core hole drill site there shall be
provided and maintained at least two portable fire extinguishers, each having a
10-B classification and located within 50 feet of the core hole.	B.	At each
unlimited core hole drill site there shall be provided and maintained at least
two portable fire extinguishers, each having a 20-B classification and located
within 100 feet of the core hole.
