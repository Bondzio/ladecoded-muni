                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMPLIANCE WITH SMOKING SIGNS. (§ 57.23.02)

  COMPLIANCE WITH SMOKING SIGNS.	It shall be unlawful for any person to remove
or destroy any such “NO SMOKING” sign or to light, or ignite or otherwise
set fire to or smoke any cigar, cigarette, pipe, tobacco, or other form of
smouldering substance, nor hold, possess, throw, or deposit any lighted or
smouldering substance in any place where such signs are posted, or in any other
place where smoking would occasion or constitute a fire or life hazard.
