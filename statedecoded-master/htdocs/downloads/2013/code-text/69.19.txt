                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

COMMERCIAL ACTIVITIES PROHIBITED. (ยง 69.19)

ย COMMERCIAL ACTIVITIES PROHIBITED.	No person shall solicit, sell, hawk, or
peddle any goods, wares, or merchandise of any sort whatsoever within the
entrance channel; nor shall any person distribute circulars, handbills or
advertising matter within the entrance channel, nor erect any signs, posters, or
other advertising media upon any breakwater, structure, sign, or marker within
said entrance channel.
