                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

ANNUAL INSPECTION OF COMPLIANCE WITH FAR AVERAGING COVENANT. (§ 19.11)

  ANNUAL INSPECTION OF COMPLIANCE WITH FAR AVERAGING COVENANT.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 173,268, Eff. 7/1/00, Oper. 7/1/00.)	A fee of $300.00
shall be charged and collected by the Department of Building and Safety to cover
the cost of an annual inspection to monitor compliance with the FAR Averaging
Covenant required pursuant to Sections 12.24 B.25. and 12.24 C.58. prior to July
1, 2000 and Section 12.24 W.19. on and after July 1, 2000, and for maintaining
records of those covenants.
