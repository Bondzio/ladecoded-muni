                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FEES FOR OFF HOURS PLAN CHECK AND OTHER SERVICES (§ 98.0422)

  FEES FOR OFF HOURS PLAN CHECK AND OTHER SERVICES
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,668, Eff. 8/28/97.)	At the request of the applicant, the
Department may, at its discretion, provide plan check or other services at other
than normal working hours upon application therefor by an applicant.  A fee, in
addition to fees charged elsewhere in this code, equal to 50 percent of the fees
for plan checking or other services or processing shall be collected at the time
of the request.
