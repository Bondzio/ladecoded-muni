                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

REPAIR WORK IN GARAGES. (§ 57.100.33)

  REPAIR WORK IN GARAGES.	Repair and storage areas of garages shall be
separated. Repair work involving the use of open flame shall be kept at least 25
feet from storage areas.  Paint, carpenter and upholstery shops shall not be
located in the same area with stored vehicles.  Tire vulcanizing equipment,
water heaters, forges, and other fuel burning equipment shall not be installed
in the storage sections of garages.
