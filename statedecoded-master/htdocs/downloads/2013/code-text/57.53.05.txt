                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DUST-TIGHT ENCLOSURES. (§ 57.53.05)

  DUST-TIGHT ENCLOSURES.	All dust producing or dust agitating machinery, such
as grinding mills and separators, and all elevators, elevator legs, spouts,
hoppers, and other conveyors shall be provided with casings or enclosures
maintained as nearly dust-tight as possible.DUST COLLECTION SYSTEM DESIGN
