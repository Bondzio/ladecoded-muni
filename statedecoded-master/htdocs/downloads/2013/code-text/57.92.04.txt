                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

FLAMMABLE AND COMBUSTIBLE LIQUID HANDLING. (§ 57.92.04)

  FLAMMABLE AND COMBUSTIBLE LIQUID HANDLING.	Containers, tanks, tank vehicles,
tank cars, storage, handling and transferring of flammable and combustible
liquids shall conform to the requirements of applicable sections of Divisions
30, 31, and 38 of this article.
