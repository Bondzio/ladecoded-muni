ย SCOPE.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 167,326, Eff. 11/16/91.)	The provisions of this division
and LAFD Standard No. 68 shall regulate the storage, processing, use, and
transportation of explosive materials.	If a conflict arises between LAFD
Standard No. 68 and the provisions of this division, the more restrictive shall
apply.
