                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

H. (§ 95.210.0)

  	Section 210.0 of the C.M.C. is adopted by reference with the following
additions:	HAZARDOUS PROCESS PIPING (HPP) is a process material piping or tubing
conveying a liquid or gas that has a degree-of hazard rating in health,
flammability or reactivity of Class 3 or 4 as ranked by the Fire
Code.	HORSEPOWER is the horsepower of the power unit driving the refrigerant
compressor. If the compressor is electrically driven, the horsepower rating
shall be determined as set forth in Article 430 of the National Electrical Code
incorporated by reference in the Electrical Code. (1 HP = 1 Ton = 12,000 Btu/h)
