                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

APPLICATION – CONTENTS OF. (§ 62.108)

  APPLICATION – CONTENTS OF.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 142,123, Eff. 7/31/71.)	Any person desiring a permit to
perform any work mentioned in Section 62.105 or Section 65.04 shall file a
written application therefor with the Board, which shall specify:	(a)	The
location, nature and amount of work to be performed;	(b)	The material to be
used;	(c)	Such other information as the Board may require.
