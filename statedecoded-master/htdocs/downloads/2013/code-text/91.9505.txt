                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS. (§ 91.9505)

  GENERAL REQUIREMENTS.
	(Added by Ord. No. 171,260, Eff. 8/30/96.)	When the owner of each building
within the scope of this division causes an investigation of the existing
construction, a structural analysis shall be made of the building by a
structural engineer licensed by the State of California.	EXCEPTION:  Regular
concrete shear wall buildings, of four stories in height and under, may be shown
to be in conformance with this division by filing a report signed by a licensed
architect, civil or structural engineer containing the information specified in
Section 91.9512.
