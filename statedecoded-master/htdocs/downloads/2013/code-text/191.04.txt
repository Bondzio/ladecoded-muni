                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

NON-HAZARDOUS CONTAMINATED SOIL TRANSFER / PROCESSING OPERATION PERMIT. (§
191.04)

  NON-HAZARDOUS CONTAMINATED SOIL TRANSFER / PROCESSING OPERATION PERMIT.	No
contaminated soil transfer/processing operation shall be operated without first
obtaining a permit from the Department.  An application shall be filed with the
Department giving information as required by the Department and, if approved by
the Department, an annual permit may be issued upon payment of an annual
inspection fee as found in the Solid Waste and Recycled Materials Facility Fee
table found in Sec. 193.02.4.
