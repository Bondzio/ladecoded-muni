                            LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE

DEPUTY INSPECTOR. (§ 97.0318)

  DEPUTY INSPECTOR.
	(Amended by Ord. No. 179,329, Eff. 12/10/07, Oper. 1/1/08.)	Every shall, within
21 days after inspecting any equipment or apparatus listed in this article,
forward to the Department reports and copies of operating permits on forms
approved by the Department.  These reports shall be in addition to those
furnished to the Division of Industrial Safety of the State of California.
